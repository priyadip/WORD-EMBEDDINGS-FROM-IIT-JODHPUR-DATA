abstract toric varieties play an important role as bridge between algebraic geometry and combinatorics
like toric varieties toric vector bundles are rich class of varieties which admit combinatorial description
following the classification due to klyachko toric vector bundle is captured by subspace arrangement decorated by toric data
this makes toric vector bundles an accessible for concepts from algebraic geometry
in this talk review some of these results and describe how combinatorial descriptions enable us to study stability properties of toric vector bundles
