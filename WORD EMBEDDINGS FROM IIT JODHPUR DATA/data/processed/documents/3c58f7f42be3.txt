hospital and medical support network in addition to healthcare iit jodhpur has established formal agreements with several leading hospitals to ensure priority treatment for its students faculty staff and their registered dependents
these include goyal hospital and research centre medipulse hospital asg eye hospital vasundhara hospital and aiims jodhpur
the institute health center hc plays central role in coordinating and supervising the treatment of patients admitted to these empanelled hospitals
this ensures continuity of care and assistance during hospitalization

extension of medical services visitors to the institute may access basic healthcare services through the health center during their stay on campus upon request
in emergency situations medical services are extended to jodhpur community members residing in the campus area to provide timely first aid and support
arrangements are routinely made for specialist doctor visits on the campus psychiatrist gynaecologist paediatrician ent specialists endocrinologist orthopedic specialist on weekly basis
the health center also organizes various health awareness medical screening vaccination camps and blood donation camps in coordination with hospitals and other agencies
the health center is the first point of contact for any medical needs and it aims to cater provide primary health care to the students staff faculties and their dependent in the campus
