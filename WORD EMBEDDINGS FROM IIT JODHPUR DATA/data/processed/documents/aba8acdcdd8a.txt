congratulations to dr sandip murarka for receiving the crsi bronze medal sandip murarka has been conferred the prestigious crsi bronze medal by the chemical research society of india crsi at the recently concluded crsi national symposium in chemistry held at dibrugarh university
this distinguished recognition acknowledges his significant contributions to the fields of organic synthesis and catalysis
iit jodhpur congratulates murarka on this honor and wishes him continued success in his future endeavors
