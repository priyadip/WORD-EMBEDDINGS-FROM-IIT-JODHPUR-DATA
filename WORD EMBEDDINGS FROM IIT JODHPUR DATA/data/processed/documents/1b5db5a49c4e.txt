of mathematics abstract in this talk we discuss piecewise and global polynomial based projection methods to solve classical integral and equations
this study covers both linear and nonlinear equations with smooth weakly singular and hypersingular kernels
we further extend our analysis to solve fractional order equations with smooth and weakly singular kernels
we have rigorously studied the convergence analysis of the proposed methods
further we obtain superconvergence for the weakly singular integral equations by improving the regularity of the exact solution using smoothing transformation
several numerical examples have been implemented to demonstrate the theoretical results
