multimodal interaction laboratory mil multimodal interaction is natural way for humans to perceive and act in coordinated manner to perform given task
this involves the senses namely touch haptics vision audio smell and taste
thus multimodal interaction lab mil in the department of electrical engineering at indian institute of technology jodhpur seeks to perform fundamental and applied research that helps to understand how humans interact and respond to stimuli develop computational models that can mimic important functionality in human perception design interactive systems for diverse applications like immersive multimedia and medical domain design efficient algorithm for modeling and representation of objects and scenes design efficient algorithms for reconstruction object detection and recognition and vision applications design novel computation based imaging system to perceive the desired information from the scene and develop processing methods for applications
