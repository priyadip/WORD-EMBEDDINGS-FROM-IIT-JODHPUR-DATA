welcome to the department of physics the subject of physics is inherently natural science subject based on strong theoretical foundations and experimental findings
the subject closely intersects with almost all the branches of engineering and the current research activities of physics across the world made huge impact on new advanced and emerging technologies
keeping these facts into consideration our department is committed to imparting education based on strong academic curriculum comprising elements of theoretical concepts laboratory experiments and most importantly experiments
the graduates of the department are expected to have deep insight into the domain of physics together with strong bent of mind to pursue research and translate it into technology
we offer broad range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs
