welcome to the office of resources and alumni dora the office of alumni relations at iit jodhpur serves as the central platform for nurturing meaningful and enduring connections between the institute and its alumni community worldwide
with vision to build strong engaged and supportive alumni network the office plays vital role in advancing the institute mission and vision by fostering networking collaboration and fundraising initiatives
key roles and objectives engagement networking strengthening lifelong relationships by organizing alumni meets chapter launches reunions and professional networking opportunities
outreach communication maintaining continuous dialogue with alumni through digital platforms newsletters and social media channels
fundraising promoting culture of giving back by designing and managing fundraising campaigns endowment drives and targeted initiatives to support students research and infrastructure
connect facilitating mentorship internships and career guidance opportunities enabling students to benefit from alumni expertise and professional networks
primarily these activities are coordinated through the society of alumni affairs saa alumni chapters
institutional advancement aligning alumni contributions with the strategic development goals of the institute thereby enhancing iit jodhpur global presence and reputation
