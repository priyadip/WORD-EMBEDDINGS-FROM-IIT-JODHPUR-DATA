he then moved to germany where he spent nearly eight years pursuing advanced studies
in he completed his master of science in life science informatics from the university of bonn germany
following this he obtained his doctoral degree in statistical genomics from the university of kiel germany in with funding from the german research foundation dfg
professional career since december yadav has been working as an associate professor in the bioscience bioengineering department at the indian institute of technology iit jodhpur
prior to this from june to december he served as an assistant professor at iit jodhpur
he is also an affiliated faculty member at the school of artificial intelligence data science at iit jodhpur
before joining iit jodhpur yadav worked as senior data scientist at reliance jio hyderabad india and as research scientist at dupont hyderabad india
from november to july he worked as postdoctoral scientist at the renowned german cancer research center dkfz in heidelberg germany
additionally in he served as research assistant at the helmholtz research center munich germany
research work yadav primary research interests include statistical genetics personalized healthcare and the application of deep learning in data analysis
with over years of experience in interaction studies method development and integrated data analysis he has made significant contributions to these fields
he is currently serving as the principal investigator pi for the genome india project funded by the department of biotechnology dbt government of india where he has been actively involved in various aspects of the analysis of indian genomes with particular focus on interactions
additionally he is leading an icmr intermediate project aimed at identifying and polygenic risk interactions in the indian population to improve risk stratification for prediabetes and type diabetes mellitus
yadav has extensive experience in developing statistical methods for interaction studies
during his time at dkfz he worked on drawing statistical inferences from multiple myeloma cancer datasets collected from hospitals in germany and the uk
at the helmholtz center munich he contributed to the development of machine learning algorithms for quantitative relationship qsar analysis
his research has been published in several international journals and he has contributed to numerous international conferences seminars and workshops
yadav also serves as an invited review editor for the international journal of diabetes in developing countries and frontiers in genetics
awards honours yadav has received several prestigious awards and fellowships throughout his career
he was awarded an undergraduate scholarship from panjab university to pursue his bachelor of engineering degree
he was also granted the german research foundation dfg fellowship to complete his doctoral studies and later received the deutsche krebshilfe postdoctoral fellowship
in yadav was honored with the inspire faculty award by the indian national science academy insa
the following year in he received the returning expert award from the german development cooperation giz
in he was awarded travel award by the human cell atlas hca to present his research at the hca asia meeting
that same year his project was selected for the scientific visiting fellowship sshn by the french institute in india embassy of france
outside of his academic achievements yadav is also passionate about sports
in he earned gold medal in volleyball as the team captain at the staff sports meet held at iit gandhinagar
in yadav was selected as senior individual member of the indian national academy of engineering inae
