title of the talk rational equivalence on algebraic groups date and time am on dec abstract rational equivalence on an algebraic variety is like path connectedness for usual topological spaces
we get very useful information on the structure of algebraic groups if we study this equivalence
the talk will explain this notion and present some recent results in the subject without getting into technicalities
this should be accessible to graduate students as well as phd students
