his research focuses on the development of turbulence and transition models using advanced machine learning approaches including evolutionary algorithms and deep learning to enhance the accuracy of computational fluid dynamics cfd simulations for gas turbine applications
he is also actively involved in developing coursework at the intersection of machine learning and fluid mechanics
prior to joining iit jodhpur he was postdoctoral researcher at the university of melbourne australia
before this he worked on computational fluid dynamics problems related to underwater vehicles

and in computational fluid dynamics from the university of melbourne in collaboration with ge aerospace
