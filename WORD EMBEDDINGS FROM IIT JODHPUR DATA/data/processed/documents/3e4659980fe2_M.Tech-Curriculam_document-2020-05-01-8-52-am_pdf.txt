metallurgical and materials engineering introduction metallurgical and materials engineering is an interdisciplinary branch that deals with converting ore into product by focusing on design extraction processing and characterization of materials for aerospace automotive energy electronics and healthcare applications
the following are the focus areas of the department process metallurgy structural materials functional materials and computational materials engineering
while the process metallurgy deals with mineral beneficiation and metal extraction the structural materials deals with understanding the correlation to expand the application of given material
in the functional materials research materials for specific functions such as energy harvesting healthcare and information processing are studied
the computational materials engineering provides understanding of materials from atomistic to macro length scales and help in materials selection alloy design and improvement of metallurgical processes
to train the students for the roles of future academicians scientists innovators and entrepreneurs
