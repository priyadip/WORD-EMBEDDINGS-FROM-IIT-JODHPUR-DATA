research seminar by lalit vaishya on march at pm speaker lalit vaishya title of the talk behavior of hecke eigenvalues of automorphic form over certain sequences date time and venue march pm pm at seminar hall dept of mathematics
abstract we present some of our work which deals with some problems in number theory
in the first part of the talk we present an analogue of the prime number theorem associated to binary quadratic forms with certain assumptions and its application
in the second part of the talk we consider question concerning higher moments and behavior of signs of the fourier coefficients of hecke eigenforms indexed by sparse set of integers
moreover we determine the size in terms of conductor of associated for the first sign change of hecke eigenvalues indexed by the integers which are represented by primitive integral binary quadratic form of fixed discriminant with the class number
in the third part of the talk we present result on the of the diophantine equation and the construction of family of elliptic curves with certain arithmetic properties
at last we aim to discuss our research proposal associated to siegel modular forms and asymptotic distribution on zeros of jacobi form
about the speaker lalit vaishya is currently working as faculty fellow at unit indian statistical institute new delhi india
prior to this he has done postdoctoral fellowships at the institute of mathematical sciences niser bhubaneswar and kerala school of mathematics
he has done from harish chandra research institute prayagraj
his main research interests are theory of automorphic forms and analytic number theory
