ug program mechanical engineering the key objectives of this program are
to enable students to have fundamental understanding of the core concepts of mechanical engineering
to integrate analytical and computational ability with experimental skills to create individuals competent in professional engineering practices to stimulate critical thinking towards application of mechanical engineering concepts in interdisciplinary problem solving to expose students to advanced concepts like ai ml industry and iot for product and system design to inculcate an attitude towards commitment to engineering ethics leadership qualities and professional development

strong understanding of fundamentals of thermofluids mechanical design and manufacturing processes and systems
