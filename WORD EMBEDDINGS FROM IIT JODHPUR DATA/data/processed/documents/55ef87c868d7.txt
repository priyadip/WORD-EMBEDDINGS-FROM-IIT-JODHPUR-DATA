at iit jodhpur mukerji leads two major transdisciplinary initiatives thar designs which explores human adaptation and ecosystem phenomics in the thar desert using technologies including population genomics citizen science and ecology and yurtech centre of excellence centre of excellence in integrative precision health at aide school that builds ai enabled frameworks for early actionable health interventions
