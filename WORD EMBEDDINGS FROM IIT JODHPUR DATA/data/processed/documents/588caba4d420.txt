research profile our research group at iit jodhpur focuses on integrating data analytics into process systems engineering aiming to advance the field through innovative research and practical applications
specifically our group focuses on developing robust adaptive and explainable predictive models for soft sensing process monitoring and control design as well as optimization schemes for chemical processes
the research aims to enhance productivity safety and efficiency in industrial plants by addressing challenges such as missing data delays outliers and mismatches
