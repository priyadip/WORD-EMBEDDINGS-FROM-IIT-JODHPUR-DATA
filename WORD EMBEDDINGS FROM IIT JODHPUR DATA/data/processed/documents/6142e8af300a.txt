pedagogy to establish systems for dynamic development implementation and evaluation of futuristic pedagogy including teaching and experiential learning
research have globally engaged research ecosystem with facilities in place for attaining leadership in research on academic social national and industrial fronts while capitalizing on emerging and opportunities
outreach to be the institute of choice for lifelong learning journey of working professionals alumni and the community
institutional collaboration have an efficient platform in place for forging impactful partnerships with academia research institutes business organizations civil society governments and other agencies across the world for contributing to larger goals for humanity
industry connect ensure ease of collaboration with industry for joint projects ipr development technology transfer and encouraging startups along with efficient supporting infrastructure and systems
financial plan set up innovative resource mobilization mechanisms and expenditure management systems embedded in the internal budgetary processes to have ample funds for actualizing the institute vision and goals
infrastructure the institute will have secure evolving futuristic digital and physical infrastructure and ancillary services to meet all its needs along with knowledge infrastructure for supporting learning and enabling skill development
student life cycle students will find at iit jodhpur vibrant learning environment with opportunities for excelling in curricular and extracurricular activities ii an effective career development process for their successful initiation to the professions of their choice and iii strong linkage with alumni through regular interactions support for their lifelong learning and professional development and enabling their active participation as stakeholder in the affairs of the institute
