of mathematics abstract quantum topology refers to interactions between problems in the topology of low dimensional manifolds and ideas and methods from quantum physics
knot theory has been particularly fruitful avenue for such interactions and for further interactions with computational techniques
beginning with gentle introduction to knots will present results from numerical and machine learning ml experiments on large dataset of knot invariants that we generated
our results include highly accurate predictions of geometric properties from simple quantum polynomials
will also demonstrate utility of our ml techniques in number theory
will end with future directions in this and other areas of my research
about the speaker pratik roy is freestanding postdoctoral fellow at the university of the witwatersrand in johannesburg south africa as principal investigator on the grant new inequalities for quantum entropy
his primary interests are machine learning applications to quantum topology and mathematical physics and quantum information theory
he also works on problems in the theory of special functions especially generalizations of hypergeometric series
before going to johannesburg he was an institute postdoctoral fellow at iit madras where he worked on quantum thermodynamics
he obtained his phd in theoretical physics from chennai mathematical institute
