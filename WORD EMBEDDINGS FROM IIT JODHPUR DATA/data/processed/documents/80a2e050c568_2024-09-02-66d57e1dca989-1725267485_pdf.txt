the internal committee and its composition the director of the institute shall have the powers to constitute the internal committee in conformity with the provisions of section of the act in the following presiding officer be senior most woman board of governors of the institute one person from each department of the institute from amongst the academic staff of the department concerned one person from amongst the staff of the institute two persons from amongst staff of the institute committed to the cause of women or who has had experience in social work or have legal knowledge one external member from amongst legal fraternity governmental organizations or associations committed to the cause of women or person familiar with issues relating sexual harassment and one secretarial staff who will be convenor of the internal committee but will not have any powers as member of the internal committee provided that least total members excluding secretarial staff be so nominated shall be women
the presiding officer and every member of the committee shall hold office for such period for three years from the date of their nomination or as may be specified by the board of governors
post completion the tenure may be extended for another term of three years
the internal committee shall have quorum comprising of minimum of five members including the chairperson and the external member
the inquiry into any complaint shall be made by the internal committee in accordance with the principles of natural justice
complaint of sexual harassment an aggrieved person shall submit written complaint to the internal committee within three months from the date ofthe incident and in case of series of incidents within period of three months from the date of the last incident
the complaint should contain the name and contact details of the aggrieved person details of the nature of harassment date and time of the incident and the name of the person against whom the complaint is being lodged
it should contain wherever possible list of witnesses and other evidence in support of the complaint
in exceptional circumstances and for reasons to be recorded in writing the ic may take cognizance of grievances relating to sexual harassment and initiate appropriate inquiry
the process for making complaint and conducting inquirv the internal committee shall comply with the procedure prescribed in these rules and the act for making complaint and inquiring into the complaint in time bound manner
the institute shall provide all necessary facilities to the internal committee to conduct the inquiry expeditiously and with required
conciliation the aggrieved person may seek conciliation in order to settle the matter
no monetary settlement should be made as basis of conciliation
the internal committee shall also ensure that the conciliation is not being sought by the complainant under duress
where settlement is arrived at copies of the settlement shall be provided to the aggrieved person and the respondent and no further inquiry shall be conducted by the internal committee
confidentiality the internal committee and all those in the chain of information flow of the complaint including but not limited to the member of the institute staff witnesses are duty bound to keep all information confidential and breach of any confidentiality shall punishable accordance with the institute policy on misconduct
applicability of ccs rules in the case the respondent is governed by ccs rules the ic shall make fact finding inquiry and refer the same to the disciplinary authority under ccs rules for further action
process of conducting an inquiry the internal committee shall upon receipt of the complaint shall meet aggrieved take statement complainant
such statement should be recorded and signed by them with the date and time of recording
simultaneously the internal committee shall send one copy of the complaint to the respondent within period of seven days of such receipt
upon receipt of the copy of the complaint the respondent shall file his or her reply to the complaint along with the list of documents and names and addresses of witnesses if any within period of when the respondent appears before the internal committee and accepts all the allegations made against the shall be required to prepare its report and submit the same to the director of the institute within ten days
if the respondent does not accept the allegations the internal committee shall interview all such witnesses and call to it all such evidence as maybe needed to investigate the complaint
while recording the statement of the respondent or witnesses the internal committee shall ensure compliance of the rules of natural justice
provided that in order to ensure safety security privacy witnesses concerned will not allow complainant or witnesses in person
however under such circumstances cross examination may permitted mode in writing
the identities victim or the respondent offender shall not be made public
during the pendency of the inquiry on written request made by person the internal committee make such interim recommendations to the director as consistent with the practice and conventions of the institute
if arrives conclusion allegations against the respondent are false and malicious it will recommend appropriate punitive measures to be taken against the complainant
mere inability to substantiate complaint or provide adequate proof need not attract such punitive action aga inst the inquiry has to be completed within period of ninety days from the receipt of the complaint
inquiry report the internal committee shall consider all facts and statements placed before it and conclude its findings
the finding so concluded is to be given to both the parties providing them maximum of days ime to submit their representation if any
after considering the representation submitted if any by the parties the internal prepare report its findings recommendations
original copy of the report together with statements of witnesses and other shall be submitted by the internal committee to the director of the institute within ten days of completion of the inquiry
the director of the institute shall cause the implementation of the recommendations of the committee within period of thirty days fromthe receipt of the inquiry report
