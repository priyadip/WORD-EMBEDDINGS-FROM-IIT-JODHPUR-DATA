where is iit jodhpur
iit jodhpur is located on the outskirts of the city of jodhpur and is well connected to other parts of country by road rail and air
the institute is located on national highway at karwad village about km from the railway station and km from the airport it takes approximately mins and mins to drive from airport and railway station to the institute respectively
