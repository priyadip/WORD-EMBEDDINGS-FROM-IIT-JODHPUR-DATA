the board of governors bog in its meeting held on june has approved the policy for instituting the research excellence award of iit jodhpur to encourage the faculty members for excellence in research and publications
the guidelines are given below
this award shall be decided by selection committee constituted by the director
the award recognizes and honors faculty members who through their work at ut jodhpur have achieved excellence in research as demonstrated by publication showing technology development published in the calendar year under consideration
shall be invited for this award from the current faculty members of iit jodhpur
the nomination should include brief description of the work its impact and justification along with copy of the publication for which the nomination is made
the publication must appear in the journal in the calendar year under consideration for the award
an individual is welcome to nominate one or more colleagues for this award or nominate herself
the period under consideration will be the calendar year
the last date of nomination would be march of next year
the award will be given on the institute foundation day
the award shall consist of silver plaque and citation
the expenditure for this award shall be made from the endowment
in the absence of endowment the overhead fund may be utilized
the number of such wards in year can be decided by the institute as per requirement registrar secretary board governors
