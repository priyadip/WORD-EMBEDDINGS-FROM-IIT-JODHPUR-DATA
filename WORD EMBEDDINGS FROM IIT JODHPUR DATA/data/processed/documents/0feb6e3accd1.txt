date time venue august at pm on seminar hall dept
of mathematics abstract in this work we propose novel method for approximating solutions to fractional differential equations in both and twovariable cases using legendre wavelets
our approach combines the legendregauss quadrature formula with the tau method to construct an operational matrix specifically for legendre wavelets
this matrix enables the efficient conversion of the original fractional differential equations into systems of linear algebraic equations
to demonstrate the accuracy and effectiveness of the proposed method we present several illustrative test problems
