research seminar by kiran meena on sep at speaker kiran meena title of the talk geometry using riemannian maps date time and venue sep at meeting room dept of mathematics
about the speaker kiran meena is currently working as assistant professor at lady shri ram college for women university of delhi
she has obtained from banaras hindu university and has postdoctoral experience from research institute
her research area is differential geometry
abstract in recent years the theory of riemannian maps has been analysed extensively
in this talk will discuss the geometry of natural submanifolds arising out of riemannian maps
towards this will establish my jointly published results when the source manifold admits ricci soliton structure and also will describe the concept of riemannian warped product maps introduced in one of my jointly published papers
finally will discuss the clairaut conditions for riemannian maps and their important geometric implications
