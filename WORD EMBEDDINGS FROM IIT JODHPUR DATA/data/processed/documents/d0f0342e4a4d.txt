about dean of administration shree prakash tiwari dean of administration shree prakash tiwari assumed the office as the first dean of administration doad of iit jodhpur on june
doad position was created in as part of administrative reforms for efficient functioning
tiwari is currently professor in department of electrical engineering
prior to joining iit jodhpur as faculty member in tiwari had worked as postdoctoral fellow in school of ece at georgia institute of technology atlanta usa for years
he had done his from ee department iit bombay from to during which he had also worked at ntu singapore for about months
tiwari is senior member of ieee and serves as selected member of technical committees on flexible electronics and displays and electronic materials of ieee electron devices society
tiwari was visvesvaraya young faculty research fellow of ministry of electronics and information technology government of india for
he was part of indian delegate to attend frontiers of engineering symposium in bremen germany organized by alexander von humboldt foundation and department of science and technology followed by connect programme award by humboldt foundation and plus award to visit and work in germany in
at iit jodhpur he leads the flexible large area microelectronics flame research group with focus towards biodegradable electronics
he was recently conferred with institute award for research senior researcher award engineering from iit jodhpur
he has over research articles including more than in journals of high repute with many in ieee acs and wiley journals
after joining doad position tiwari has been part of various key initiatives including signing of mous with various nits for direct phd program for their graduates in
as dean of administration tiwari is also project investigator of second phase of jodhpur city knowledge and innovation cluster jckic project which is an initiative of the office of the principal scientific adviser psa to the government of india
during initial phase of his term he assisted in setting up the policies of iit jodhpur research park
he also helped in finalising the commercial establishment licencing rules of iit jodhpur as chairperson
in addition he has been part of various committees of the institute as member or chairperson
he oversees the activities of various sections under office of doad directly or through pic with aim of ensuring highest standards of administrative ecosystem
