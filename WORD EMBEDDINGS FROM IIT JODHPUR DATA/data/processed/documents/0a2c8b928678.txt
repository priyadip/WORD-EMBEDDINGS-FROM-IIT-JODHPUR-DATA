research pandey current research program focuses on expanding the landscape of rare earth elements chemistry which are actually not rare but critical for the development of sustainable modern technologies
the research area mainly focuses on fundamental understanding of the structure bonding electronic and magnetic properties of organometallic and coordination compounds of rare earth metals supported by advanced ligand platforms like cyclic alkyl amino carbene caac and mesoionic carbene mic
beyond fundamental chemistry the isolated rare earth metal complexes will be explored for applications in functional molecular materials with emergent quantum properties challenging photo catalytic transformations and activation of small molecules
eventually in the long run the electronic magnetic and luminescent properties of molecular compounds of rare earth metals are harnessed for the critical and innovative technological advancements via highly collaborative interdisciplinary research in areas of spintronics photonics display technology and critical mineral separation technology
