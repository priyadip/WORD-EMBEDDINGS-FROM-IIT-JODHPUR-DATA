research our group is interested in studying organometallic catalysis which shape and guide the chemical processes of the industry
our research work focuses on designing organometallic complexes bearing pincer and nitrogen phosphine and carbene based ligands
we explore the designed novel systems in homogeneous catalytic reactions which play pivotal role in the synthesis of industrially important molecules for fine chemicals pharmaceuticals agrochemical and so on
