student life iit jodhpur gymkhana iit jodhpur assures holistic development of students
the institute provides an excellent academic environment that allows nurturing multidimensional talents of student
through students gymkhana the institute creates ample opportunity for student to hone their skills in various extra and activities
the students gymkhana is student body comprised of seven student societies namely students
cultural and literary society design arts sports games society campus life society student elected represented society academic career society and science technology society the societies are headed by student secretaries and the gymkhana is led by student general secretary
through various clubs under each societies both and activities are organized throughout the academic year
students of iit jodhpur participate in various national and international level events and bring accolades
flagship events of different societies keep the campus atmosphere colourful and vibrant
