editorial policy editorial policy for techscape the science technology and education journal of iit jodhpur the following is the policy for techscape the science technology and education journal of iit jodhpur
purpose focus and scope techscape the science technology and education journal of iit jodhpur will feature latest developments in research and pedagogy at iit jodhpur views and commentaries on science and technology in the world at large and having connection with the institute features and research snippets outreach and campus highlights
it will have articles for serious readers understandable without specialized knowledge
the content will be curated to be of general interest in particular to the iit jodhpur community and to all stakeholders at large
language the content of the journal will be in english
major sections sources of information the major sections and sequence in which the contents of techscape will be organized is as below
editorial section written by the publication committee that introduces the issue and all the articles in particular issue and gives the readers sense of what to expect
director column section where the director expresses his thoughts related to the contemporary local and global developments for the issue being published
news views latest stories from iitj in terms of initiatives new programs achievements and recognition in terms of awards research iv
opinions and commentaries on latest developments in the fields of science technology and education will also be part of this section
in focus detailed articles relevant to what latest at iitj and in the world
research snippets short features describing research initiatives at iit jodhpur and their expected outcomes
scientific social connect stories of from iit jodhpur that have directly impacted the masses and are transforming society viii
innovation gallery recent research developments that result in technology transfer
iit jodhpur bioscope photos and videos of science and technology activities from the campus along with people and
span extent and frequency the journal will be released thrice in an academic year with one issue to commemorate institute foundation day and one issue to commemorate the annual convocation whenever these events may be held
format and design the format and design adopted for the foundation day issue of techscape in august will be followed for further issues
hosting and distribution the journal will be hosted electronically on the institute website
an electronic copy will also be sent to list of external organizations provided by the institute public relations officer
hard copies of the journal will be sent to specific organizations list of which will be prepared by the committee and approved by the director
process of review initial copies of the articles would be sent to members of the institute publication committee for copyediting and review
after review the articles will be compiled into final copy and sent to the director for inputs and approval
the design and layout of the journal will be implemented after the director approves the final copy
selection of articles the selection of articles for publication in techscape the science technology and education journal of iit jodhpur shall be the prerogative of the director iit jodhpur and the institute publication committee
revision in policy as per requirements arising from time to time the institute publication committee shall discuss and propose revisions to the existing policy
notes for contributors
contributors to techscape may be from iit jodhpur or from other reputed educational research and industrial organizations in india and across the world
contributors outside iit jodhpur may submit entries via invitation only ii
techscape publishes original contributions that have not been published or being considered for publications elsewhere iii
the director iit jodhpur and the institute publication committee ipc as their editorial discretion reserves the right to accept or reject articles submitted for publication in techscape
the institute publication committee ipc under its review process may suggest revisions related to the title subheadings and content etc
the director and the committee also reserve the right to slot an article in specific section or issue of the journal as deemed appropriate copyright will be retained by the author and the institution that the author is affiliated to
the institute publication committee has no objection to authors republishing their articles in other publications
however the republished article must mention prominently that version of the article was first published in techscape the science technology and education journal of iit jodhpur
instructions for use of any photographs schematics or infographics
the authors need to ensure that they have the ownership or necessary permissions for the used
the graphics must have minimum resolution of dpi
