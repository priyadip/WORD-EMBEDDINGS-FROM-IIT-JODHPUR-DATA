introduction chemistry involves the study of substances their properties structures reactions and transformations
chemistry is interlinked with various scientific disciplines and play an important role in our daily life
it broadly covers various areas including medicinal chemistry analytical chemistry organometallic chemistry polymer chemistry quantum chemistry catalysis and many aspects of materials science
in the modern society knowledge of chemistry is essential for addressing most of the challenges related to water energy food agriculture security and healthcare
objective of the programme the major objective of the program is to bring out professionals having fundamental knowledge and specific skill sets of various aspects of chemistry such as organic reactions and their mechanism and transition metals organometallics and catalysis physical and spectroscopic techniques inorganic and solid state material synthesis computational and theoretical chemistry
the department focuses on preparing future students to take up challenges at various levels of academics ds and industries
expected graduate attributes capability to appreciate synthetic methods for development of important chemical products such as pharmaceutical drugs paints petrochemicals food additives plastics etc
ability to use characterization tools for analysing reactions and monitoring chemical processes for product development ability to handle analytical spectroscopic microscopic and other instrumental methods for interdisciplinary research areas
use of computational tools to understand and analyse chemical and biophysical processes molecular structures and advanced materials
ability to comprehend write and present scientific articles and technical reports
develop oral and written communication skills
appreciation and adherence to norms of professional ethics and sense of intellectual integrity
ability to plan and manage technical research projects and work in team
learning outcome strong fundamentals in theoretical and practical aspects of organic inorganic and physical chemistry
develop appreciation of wet chemistry analytical instrumentation and experimental data handling skills to solve scientific problems to address technological challenges in various domains
knowledge of computational and theoretical chemistry
skills for material synthesis and processes for device integration
develop the ability to exercise independent learning thinking and problem solving skills
develop the ability to interact in environment and acquire time and project management skills for carrying out guided research work in any of the research area related to science and technology
develop skills to communicate scientific learning and present ideas
