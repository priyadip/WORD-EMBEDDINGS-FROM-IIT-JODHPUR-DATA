the office of security transports at the indian institute of technology jodhpur is dedicated to ensuring the safety and security of the campus community while providing efficient and reliable transportation services
our mission is to create secure and environment that supports the academic and operational needs of the institute
we oversee campus security implementing measures to protect students faculty staff and visitors
this includes security patrols surveillance systems and emergency response protocols
our team is committed to maintaining safe campus atmosphere preventing incidents and addressing security concerns promptly
the office of security transports also manages transportation services ensuring the smooth operation of campus shuttles and other transport facilities
we coordinate transportation logistics for academic events field trips and daily commutes striving to provide timely and convenient services for all members of the iit jodhpur community
we work closely with local authorities and emergency services to enhance campus security and manage crises effectively
our office conducts regular safety drills and awareness programs to educate the campus community about safety practices and protocols
by fostering secure and transport system the office of security transports plays vital role in supporting the institute mission ensuring that students and staff can focus on their academic and professional pursuits without concern for their safety and mobility
