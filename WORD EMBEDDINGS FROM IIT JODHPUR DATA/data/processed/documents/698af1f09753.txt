abstract graph homomorphism is powerful tool for understanding the structure of graphs
in this talk we will focus primarily on problems related to graph homomorphisms from structural perspective with some discussion of computational aspects
we will explore how homomorphisms reveal key structural properties of graphs
further we will discuss the application of these structural insights including an application in the cops and robber game
we will conclude the talk by outlining some further research directions
about the speaker suchismita mishra is currently doing postdoctoral position at the institute of mathematical sciences chennai
prior to this she has done several postdoc positions at the universidad bello santiago chile and iit gandhinagar
she obtained from iit madras
her research interests include graph theory algebraic combinatorics and parameterized algorithms
