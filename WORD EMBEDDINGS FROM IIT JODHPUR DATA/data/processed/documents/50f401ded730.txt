what is the duration of fellowship
the duration of the fellowship is initially for period of years extendable for further period of one more year based on the progress of the fellow and the recommendation of the mentor faculty member and approval by duly constituted committee for the purpose
do receive fellowship grant
fellow shall be paid fellowship amount of rs
if available suitable accommodation may be provided as per iit jodhpur norms
alternatively each fellow shall be paid house rent allowance of rs
what other benefits do receive
pdf will be entitled to receive the following benefits contingency grant contingency grant of per annum will be provided to the fellow towards research equipment consumables and contingency
the fellow may use only upto of the contingency grant for attending national or international
medical outpatient medical benefits and limited insurance will be provided for coverage
what happens after submitting application
after receipt of soft and hard copies of your application the selection process begins
the process adopted for recruitment of faculty members at iit jodhpur involves five levels namely level screening based on the need in certain areas within the listed disciplines level scrutiny based on academic background and research preparation presented in the application level seminar in person or via mutual presence software on internet level interview in person or via mutual presence software on internet
how much time does the selection process take
applications will be screened for interview once every three months
each cycle of selection should be completed in about three months
