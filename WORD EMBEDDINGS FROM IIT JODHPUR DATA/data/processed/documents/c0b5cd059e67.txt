arora leads the structured active materials lab where his group integrates theory computation and experiments to investigate how structure geometry and activity can be harnessed to design metamaterials with programmable and adaptive mechanical responses
his research has been recognized with several honors including the thomas hughes fellowship the aps distinguished student award and the nsf presenter fellowship
beyond research he is actively involved in science outreach and has served as an outreach fellow for the national committee for theoretical and applied mechanics
