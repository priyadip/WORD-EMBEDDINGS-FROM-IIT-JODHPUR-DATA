entrepreneurs everywhere concept of entrepreneurship includes everyone working in any institution creating new products processes services under extreme conditions of uncertainty
entrepreneurship is management realisation of any concept in physical world under conditions of uncertainty requires special management skills which can be useful in any enterprise in any sector or industry
validated learning entrepreneurs learn how to build sustainable technology and business based upon their skill set acquired through years of effort
their learning gets validated scientifically by running experiments that allow entrepreneurs to test each element of their concept
learn fundamental activity of is to turn ideas into tangible entities measures how customers respond and then learn to persevere or rethink
professional going through this process learns how to work in an iterative cycle for realisation of the conceptual plan
