of mathematics abstract in this talk we present some tools to study singularities of ring in the prime characteristic case
these include the study of closure operations on ideals and associated hilbert functions
singularities defined in terms of the behavior of the frobenius endomorphism on the ring are called
we study tight closure closure operation on ideals introduced by hochster and huneke in and the function of ideals and their blowup algebras
functions were introduced by kunz in in his work characterizing regular local rings in the prime characteristic setting
in the process we introduce the concept of tight hilbert polynomials whose coefficients help characterize singularities and explore the behavior of functions in various classes of rings including face rings of simplicial complexes
finally we consider finite group acting on polynomial ring over field
we study the connection between the algebraic properties of the invariant ring and the properties of the group action in the prime characteristic setting the focus being the local cohomology modules of the invariant ring
