jitendra yadav has received the galileo galilei poster award at the international symposium on beyond batteries held in padova italy sept for his poster presentation titled graphitic carbon nitride as the efficient cathode material for the rechargeable battery under an ambient environment
the award was presented by nobel laureate in chemistry stanley whittingham which further adds significance to this recognition
