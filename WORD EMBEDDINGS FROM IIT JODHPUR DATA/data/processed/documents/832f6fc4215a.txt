his specialization lies in environmental engineering with specific focus on air pollution
his research involves weather forecasting climate modeling and air pollution studies using complex model simulations on supercomputers
this work is supplemented by comprehensive data analysis from satellites radiosondes and various instruments
he has been serving as an assistant professor at iit jodhpur since july
prior to this he worked as postdoctoral research associate at washington state university where he focused on wildfire modeling
he has also held visiting scholar positions at the university of manchester specializing in aerosol chemical speciation and at the max planck institute for chemistry where he contributed to weather research and forecasting
sharma expertise and research continue to contribute to advancements in environmental engineering and air pollution studies
