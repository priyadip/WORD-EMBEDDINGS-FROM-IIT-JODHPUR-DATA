all research highlights structure and exfoliation mechanism of boron nanosheets tittle of the paper structure and exfoliation mechanism of boron nanosheets nature communications pranay ranjan department of metallurgical and materials engineering iit jodhpur description exfoliation of nanosheets from der waals crystals represents an emerging strategy for materials engineering that could significantly increase the library of materials
yet the exfoliation mechanism in which nanosheets are derived from crystals that are not intrinsically layered remains unclear
here we show that planar defects in the starting boron material promote the exfoliation of boron combining exfoliation scanning transmission electron microscopy raman spectroscopy and density functional theory calculations
we demonstrate that boron nanosheets consist of planar arrangement of icosahedral cleaved along the planes of boron
correspondingly intrinsic stacking faults in boron form parallel layers of faulted planes in the same orientation as the exfoliated nanosheets reducing the cleavage energy
planar defects represent potential engineerable pathway for exfoliating sheets from boron and more broadly the other covalently bonded materials
