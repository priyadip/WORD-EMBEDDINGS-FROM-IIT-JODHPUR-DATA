navigating phd and beyond iit jodhpur empowering the next generation of researchers
on january iit jodhpur in collaboration with the american chemical society acs hosted navigating phd and dynamic event designed to equip young researchers with the essential skills perspectives and strategies to excel in academia and industry
the event featured insightful sessions by distinguished experts including avinash kumar agarwal director iit jodhpur ashutosh sharma scholar in residence and president insa deeksha gupta acs anand madhukar teri sas and ajay jha acs
key takeaways importance of sustainability in research mastering the art of scientific communication navigating career pathways making the most of phd day filled with networking and inspiration this event reaffirmed the commitment to fostering robust research ecosystem
