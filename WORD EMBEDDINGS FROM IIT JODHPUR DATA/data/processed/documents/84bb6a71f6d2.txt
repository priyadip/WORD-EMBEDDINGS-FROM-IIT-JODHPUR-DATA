sandip murarka associate professor email call language web profile biography murarka pursued his phd from wwu under the supervision of armido studer
after completing his he worked as postdoctoral research fellow in the laboratory of herbert waldmann at max planck institute of molecular physiology dortmund
following stay as team leader in reputed pharmaceutical company syngene international limited he decided to return to academia
in may he joined the indian institute of technology jodhpur india as an assistant professor and was promoted to the post of associate professor in june
he is recipient of the prestigious institute senior researcher award science category by iit jodhpur the early career research award by serb government of india the thieme chemistry journal award by thieme and the merck young scientist award by merck
he is an early career advisory board member ecab of several journals such as chemistryselect chemistry an asian journal and organic chemistry frontiers rsc
he is also fellow of the indian chemical society fics and member of the royal society of chemistry mrsc
research murarka current research activities include the study of novel activation modes and the development of chemoselective and sustainable synthetic transformations
