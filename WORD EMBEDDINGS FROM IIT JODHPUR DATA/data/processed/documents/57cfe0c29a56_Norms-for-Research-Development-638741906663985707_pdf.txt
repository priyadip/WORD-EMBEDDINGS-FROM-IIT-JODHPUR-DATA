types of projects the institute encourages faculty members to seek external funds from government and government agencies to pursue activities in areas of their competence
the office of administers these projects in keeping with the guidelines agreed upon with the funding agencies
currently the office administers four types of projects namely sponsored research projects consultancy projects outreach projects and routine testing projects and fellowship projects
in addition it administers three others projects based on funding from grant in aid from mhrd namely seed grant professional development account pda and institute projects
sponsored research projects individual or group of faculty members of the institute can write research project proposals to seek competitive grants from research funding agencies in india or abroad
faculty members may approach various national dst dbt csir brns ardb drdo and isro or international bilateral government agencies such as and russian agencies funding agencies and industries for sponsored research project
the pi will adhere to the funding agency norms alongside complying with the institute term and conditions
also some funding agencies may provide grants for writing books preparing films and creating resources
such projects shall be treated also as sponsored research projects
in specific instances the office of will administer the collaborative projects which are projects seeking fund from funding agency simultaneously for more than one institute including iit jodhpur
the project proposal should bifurcate clearly the money requested from the funding agency for institute and for the other institute
the pi should obtain separate approval of the director before seeking such internal financial support from external agencies
sponsored research project proposals may be submitted as per the format of the respective funding agencies
