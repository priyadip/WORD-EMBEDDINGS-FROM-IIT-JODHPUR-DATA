we are delighted to share that shahzad ahmed phd research scholar from the department of materials engineering indian institute of technology jodhpur has been honored with the best oral presentation award at the international conference on materials science and engineering held on october at the millennium hotel paris charles de gaulle paris france organized by prezentis
shahzad was invited to present his talk titled fabricated zno ito for spiciness detection in chili his work presents an innovative platform for flavor and monitoring promising step toward smart sensing technologies
iit jodhpur applauds his outstanding achievement and extends best wishes for his continued contributions to research and innovation
