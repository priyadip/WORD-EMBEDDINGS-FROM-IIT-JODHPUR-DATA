catalan numbers enumerate numerous combinatorial objects including dyck paths and plane trees
in haglund and haiman introduced of catalan numbers as weighted generating function for dyck paths
these polynomials also arise as the hilbert series of certain in the diagonal coinvariant ring whose dimension matches the th catalan number
major open problem in this area is finding combinatorial proof of the symmetry of these polynomials in and
in this talk we begin with an overview of these concepts before presenting recent joint work with anne schilling and joseph pappe
we introduce two new polynomials defined using novel statistics on dyck paths and prove their symmetry by developing new duality on plane trees
we conclude with further applications and open problems in combinatorics
