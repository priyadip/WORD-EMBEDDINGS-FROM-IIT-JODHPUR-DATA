congratulations to moumita mandal for the acceptance of her research paper in advances in applied mathematics and mechanics title jacobi spectral galerkin methods for class of nonlinear weakly singular volterra integral equations authors kapil kant moumita mandal and gnaneshwar nelakanti journal advances in applied mathematics and mechanics year publisher global science press abstract we propose the jacobi spectral galerkin and jacobi spectral multi galerkin methods with their iterated versions for obtaining the superconvergence results of general class of nonlinear volterra integral equations with kernel where which have an abel type and an endpoint singularity
the exact solutions for these types of integral equations are singular at the initial point of integration
first we apply transformation of independent variables to find new integral equation with sufficiently smooth solution
then we discuss the superconvergence rates for the transformed equation in both uniform and weighted norms
we obtain the order of convergence in jacobi spectral galerkin method and in uniform and weighted norms respectively
whereas iterated jacobi spectral galerkin method converges with the order of convergence in both uniform and weighted l2 norms
we also show that iterated jacobi spectral multi galerkin method converges with the orders log and in uniform and weighted norms respectively
theoretical results are verified by numerical illustrations
