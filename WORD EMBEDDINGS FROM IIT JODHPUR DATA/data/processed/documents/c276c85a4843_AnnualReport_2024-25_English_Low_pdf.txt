chandramouli the central theme of the dynamical theory is the geometric rigidity of the attractors
the main technique is renormalization
renormalization is method to study asymptotic small scale geometry of the attractor of systems which are at the transition from simple to chaotic dynamics
the existence of unique renormalization fixed point which is also hyperbolic among generic smooth enough maps plays crucial role in the renormalization theory
it gives the universality phenomenon and rigidity of the attractor
my contributions show the breakdown of uniqueness and rigidity when one approaches from below the minimal smoothness thresholds
and finally we show that the renormalization operator acting on low smoothness space of maps has infinite topological entropy
which describes the of renormalization in contrast to the rigidity phenomenon in dynamics
