call about arun kumar is an associate professor in the department of mechanical engineering at the indian institute of technology iit jodhpur
his area of expertise lies in flows and shock waves
he has established the shock waves and flow laboratory swahs lab at iit jodhpur which focuses on experimental and computational research related to supersonic and hypersonic flow phenomena including fuel injection mixing enhancement and blast wave interactions
the shock waves and flow laboratory swahs lab is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of research and technology development in the aerospace and defense sectors
in addition to traditional aerodynamic applications the lab actively explores multidisciplinary extensions of shock wave and flow research into fields such as refrigeration systems biomedical engineering industrial processing and water purification
ongoing research includes projects on supersonic intakes supersonic mixing in scramjet combustors flow control and actuators gas turbine cooling electric propulsion systems shock and blast wave attenuation as well as novel applications like solar ejector refrigeration steam water purification and shock biomedical techniques
