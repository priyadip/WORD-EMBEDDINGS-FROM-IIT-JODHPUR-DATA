in mathematics introduction the classical mathematics provides foundations for analysis of key principles behind modern and emergent technologies modern mathematics coupled with computational capabilities is opening new avenues for further explorations
mathematics program at iit jodhpur aims at tapping these opportunities by developing workforce specialized in mathematics needed for developing key technologies
the proposed four semester program strives to provide balance between analysis and application of mathematics
the compulsory courses provide the basic understanding and foundation of mathematics enhancing analytic capabilities of the students
students can further explore their area of interest with program and open elective courses
the project component spread over last two semesters provides research exposure to the students and provides an opportunity to apply their mathematical skills to solve problems
students are encouraged to undertake research summer internships in institute of repute industry and organizations
objective of the programme
program in mathematics at iit jodhpur is offered with the objective of tapping opportunities for nurturing motivated students in the frontline areas and well trained workforce for academia and industry
the program also strives to provide vibrant environment to support independent thinking and developing the spirit of team work
expected graduate attributes
skill set to analyze abstract mathematical structures
understanding about the recent thrust areas in pure and applied mathematics such as algebra analysis statistics and computation
ability to formulate mathematics from computational aspect
knowledge and capability to develop and apply mathematical models for real life problems
skill set to understand the abstraction of mathematical structures
analytical and scientific skill set to pursue higher studies and research in diverse areas of mathematics

understanding of fundamental aspects of real complex and functional analysis
basic understanding of linear and abstract algebra
knowledge about basics and use of probability theory statistics and differential equations
skill set to cater computational aspects using programming techniques
basic understanding of symbolic solution tools such as matlab or mathematica
