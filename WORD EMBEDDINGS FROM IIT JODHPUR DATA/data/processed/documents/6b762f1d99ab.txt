he was an associate professor and assistant professor in the same department from september onward
prior to joining iit jodhpur bhatnagar spent four years as research associate in the department of electrical and computer engineering at university of windsor canada from september to august
bhatnagar received his phd in interdisciplinary area of applied mathematics and computer science and an msc in applied mathematics from the indian institute of technology roorkee india in and respectively
he is also the project director of tih ihub drishti section organisation promoted by and at iit jodhpur established under national mission on interdisciplinary cyber physical systems of the government of india
his research interests lie in the broad area of wavelet and fractional transform theory with applications in multimedia security digital watermarking and encryption techniques hashing protocols biometrics image analysis segmentation and completion
bhatnagar is the author of over scholarly publications in peer reviewed technical journals and conference proceedings
he was recently listed under researchers in artificial intelligence image processing in the newly released stanford university listing of the top of researchers globally
he is member of several professional bodies including ieee acm siam and ams
he is associated with several journals as the technical committee member and has been regularly associated with international in various roles such as program chair area chair session chair and tpc member
