his research interests lie broadly in extremal graph theory and combinatorics

in extremal combinatorics he has worked on fractional variant of intersecting families of set systems using probabilistic and linear algebraic tools
his current research interests include the study of intersecting families of graphs via methods and the study of path representations of graphs
