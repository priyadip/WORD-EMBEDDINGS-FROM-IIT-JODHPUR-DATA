exercise of the powers conferred by section read with section of the institutes of technology act of the council with the prior approval of the visitor hereby frames the following first statutes of indian institute of technology jodhpur namely short title and commencement these statutes may be called the first statutes of indian institute of technology jodhpur
they shall come into force on the date of their publication in the official gazette
definitions in these first statutes unless the context otherwise requires act means the institutes of technology act as amended from time to time authorities means the authorities of the institute as specified in section of the act building and works committee means the building and works committee of the institute central government means the government of india dean means the dean of the institute department school centre and division means the department school centre division and unit respectively established by the institute from time to time finance committee means the finance committee of the institute head means the head of the department school centre division or such other unit as the case may be institute means the indian institute of technology jodhpur officers means the officers of the institute ordinances means the ordinances of the institute as may be framed from time to time under section of the act faculty means professor associate professor and assistant professor regular and contract rules means the rules framed by the board student means student admitted to prescribed programme of the institute through the laid down procedure and whose name is borne on the rolls of the institute means the warden of the halls of residence and hostels of the institute and schedule means schedule annexed to these statutes

meeting of the board the board shall meet as often as may be necessary but not less than twice during calendar year
meetings of the board shall ordinarily be convened by the chairman either on his motion or at the request of the director or on requisition signed by not less than three members of the board
five members including one member from outside the institute either through personal presence or video conference or teleconference shall form quorum for meeting
hkkjr dk all the matters considered at the meetings of the board shall be decided by majority of the votes of the members present including chairman
if the votes are equally divided the chairman shall have second or casting vote
written notice of the meeting shall be sent by the registrar to every member at least two weeks before the date of the meeting
the notice shall state the place the date and the time of the meeting
the notice may be delivered by post electronic mail or fax at the address of each member as recorded in the institute and if so sent shall be deemed to have been delivered
notwithstanding the provisions of the immediately preceding provision the chairman may call meeting of the board at short notice to consider urgent matters
the agenda of the meeting shall be circulated by the registrar to the members at least ten days before the meeting
notices for inclusion of any item on the agenda must reach the registrar at least ten days before the meeting
the chairman may permit inclusion of any item for which due notice has not been received
the ruling of the chairman in regard to all matters relating to procedure shall be final
the minutes of the proceedings of meeting of the board shall be prepared by the registrar and circulated to all the members of the board
the minutes along with any amendment suggested shall be placed for confirmation at the next meeting of the board
after the minutes are confirmed the minutes shall be signed by the chairman
authentication of the orders and decisions of the board all orders and decisions of the board shall be authenticated by the signature of the registrar
