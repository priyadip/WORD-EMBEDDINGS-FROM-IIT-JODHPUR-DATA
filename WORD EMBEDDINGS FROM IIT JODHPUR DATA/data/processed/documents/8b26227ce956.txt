the department of mathematics is organizing an introductory workshop on the lean proof assistant from to february
the workshop will introduce the participants to theorem proving in lean using the mathlib library and building definitions in lean
no prior experience with lean is needed all faculty phd scholars and masters students in the department are welcome
the workshop will be led by prathamesh assistant professor of computer science krea university
to get feel for theorem proving in lean we will organize from to february where we will play the natural number game and get started with installing lean
