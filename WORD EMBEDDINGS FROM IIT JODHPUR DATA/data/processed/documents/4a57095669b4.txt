she is the author of the mla first book monograph utopia and the village in south asian literatures palgrave macmillan uk and the editor of literature and transmodernity of worlds in contact brill july jan and maritime transmodernities literatures of the indian ocean postcolonial text
her critical essays have featured in journals such as south asian history and culture asian review of world history university of toronto quarterly indian theatre journal and economic and political weekly among others
she was the at the samyukta research foundation thiruvananthapuram and the landhaus fellow at the rachel carson center for environment and society university of munich germany
anupama is the author of the novel where mayflies live forever picador the poetry collection twenty odd love poems the writer workshop and several short stories featured in print and online venues
