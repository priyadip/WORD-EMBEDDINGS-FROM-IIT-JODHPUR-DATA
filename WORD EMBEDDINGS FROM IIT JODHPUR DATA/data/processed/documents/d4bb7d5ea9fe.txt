professor avinash kumar agarwal professor avinash kumar agarwal took over the charge of director iit jodhpur on may
he obtained his undergraduate degree in mechanical engineering from malviya regional engineering college jaipur and his mtech energy and phd energy from the indian institute of technology iit delhi
after his fellowship at the erc uw madison usa he returned to india in and joined iit kanpur
he was visiting professor at the university of loughborough uk photonics institute university of vienna austria hanyang university and kaist south korea
agarwal is interested in research in ic engines combustion conventional and alternative fuels hcng fuelled engine development lca and tco analyses fuel sprays lubricating oil tribology optical diagnostics laser ignition hcci particulates and emission control and large bore engines
he has developed hydrogen and cng engines in automotive sizes and developed the first electronic fuel injection system equipped with locomotive engine for indian railways
currently agarwal is developing methanol and vehicles for the agricultural sectors
agarwal has published over international journal and conference papers edited books and book chapters attracting scopus and google scholar citations
agarwal is the editor of fuel of the journal of energy and environmental sustainability associate editor of the asme journal of energy resources technology asme open journal of engineering international journal of vehicle systems modelling and testing and the journal of the institute of engineers series and an editorial board member of imeche international journal of engine research
he edited wiley vch germany handbook of combustion volumes pages the most updated combustion compilation globally
for his outstanding contributions agarwal is conferred upon sir bose national fellowship by serb sae india foundation guru award wsset innovation clarivate analytics india citation in engineering and technology prestigious shanti swarup bhatnagar prize in engineering sciences rajib goyal prize in physical sciences industries platinum jubilee award inae silver jubilee young engineer award dr raman young teachers award sae ralph teetor educational award insa young scientist award uict young scientist award inae young engineer award devendra shukla research fellowship poonam and prabhu goyal endowed chair professorship sbi endowed chair professorship at iit kanpur aicte career award for young teachers dst young scientist award and dst boyscast fellowship in addition to inaugural version of distinguished alumni by mnit jaipur and distinguished alumni by iit delhi
agarwal is highly cited in and is among the top ten hcrs from india among hcr researchers globally in fields of enquiry
he is india number one energy researcher in the recently declared stanford university listing of the top of researchers globally
he is an elected fellow of the society of automotive engineers international usa sae american society of mechanical engineers asme indian national academy of engineering inae international society for energy environment and sustainability isees royal society of chemistry rsc national academy of science allahabad nasi world society of sustainable energy technologies american association for advancement in science aaas and combustion institute usa ci
he is featured in the dst golden jubilee coffee table book under scientists shaping today india released by vigyan prasar government of india on national science day february
at iit kanpur agarwal has established engine research laboratory and he was also the of iit kanpur science and technology research park technopark iitk
he also founded the international society for energy environment and sustainability in and is the editor of the book series entitled energy environment and sustainability published by springer singapore onwards
