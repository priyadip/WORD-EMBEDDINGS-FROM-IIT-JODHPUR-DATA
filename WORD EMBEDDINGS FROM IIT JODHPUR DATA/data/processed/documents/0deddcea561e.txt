congratulations to moumita mandal for the acceptance of her research article in the journal international journal of nonlinear analysis and applications title projection and methods for second kind integral equation authors mandal kant and nelakanti journal international journal of nonlinear analysis and applications volume in press year publisher semnan university abstract in this article we discuss the piecewise polynomial based galerkin method to approximate the solutions of second kind integral equations
we discuss the convergence of the approximate solutions to the exact solutions and obtain the orders of convergence and respectively for galerkin and its iterated galerkin methods in uniform norm where denote the norm of the partition and smoothness of the kernel respectively
we also obtain the superconvergence results for and iterated methods
we show that iterated method has the order of convergence in the uniform norm
numerical results are provided to demonstrate the theoretical results
