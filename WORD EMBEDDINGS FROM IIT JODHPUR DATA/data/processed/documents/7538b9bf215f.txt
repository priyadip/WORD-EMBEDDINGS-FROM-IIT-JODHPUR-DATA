research seminar by tushar bag on march at pm speaker tushar bag title of the talk from bits to qubits journey into quantum error correction with algebraic codes date time and venue march pm pm at seminar hall dept of mathematics
abstract reliable error correction is crucial for the development of quantum computing
this talk will provide an overview of both classical and quantum coding theory laying the foundation for quantum error correction
we will explore how techniques from classical linear be adapted to protect fragile quantum states
additionally will discuss the construction of quantum codes using various families of linear codes including cyclic and group ring codes
special emphasis will be placed on the advantages of codes over traditional cyclic codes along with some of my newly constructed binary and quantum codes
about the speaker tushar bag is currently working as postdoctoral research at normale de lyon france prior to this he has done postdoctoral research at united arab emirates university uae sep apr and carleton university canada apr apr
his main research area is algebraic coding theory
he has done from iit patna and from iit kanpur
