the student wellbeing committee swc at iit jodhpur has been key component of the institute to ensure the mental and emotional wellbeing of students by promoting their personal and academic development
the committee operates along three primary aspects academic and personal
academic support provided by swc includes providing information about academic programs facilitating efficient time management and developing effective study skills
the aspect of swc promotes the development of talents in students and encourages them to explore their hobbies and interests
the personal aspect of swc provides cushion against homesickness and supports students in adjusting to the new environment including addressing concerns and difficulties arising during their stay at the institute
the committee offers personalized guidance support and confidential referral services to students
it coordinates with yourdost an online counseling and emotional wellness platform and organizes workshops on key issues faced by students
finally the committee organizes events to encourage interaction among students of different years as well as staff and faculty members
through its various initiatives the swc strives to make iit jodhpur home away from home for all students

embracing mistakes errors and failure your path to success akash vishwakarma in the journey of life mistakes errors and failures are the uninvited but necessary guests
they are not to be feared or shunned for they hold the potential for growth resilience and personal development within them
as students staff entrepreneurs or leaders it vital to comprehend that these experiences are not roadblocks but rather stepping stones on your path to success
mistakes are not indicators of weakness but rather symbols of your courage to step beyond your comfort zone
they serve as invaluable learning opportunities that can lead to personal and academic growth
when you make mistake don merely dwell on it instead reflect on what went wrong and why
this process allows you to make adjustments and avoid repeating the same mistake in the future
every mistake is stepping stone on the path to mastery
errors often accompany the pursuit of excellence
they are natural part of any learning process and should not be seen as signs of inadequacy
thomas edison the renowned light bulb inventor famously said have not failed
ve just found ways that won work
this shift in perspective from failure to learning enabled him to succeed ultimately
rowling the author of the harry potter series faced rejection from multiple publishers before finding success
her story serves as testament to the fact that failure is not it stepping stone toward realizing your potential
growth mindset is the belief that your abilities and intelligence can be developed through dedication and hard work
when you approach challenges with growth mindset you see them as opportunities for rather than as judgments of your abilities
be hopeful resilient and keep moving forward knowing that mistakes and failures guide you towards brighter future
