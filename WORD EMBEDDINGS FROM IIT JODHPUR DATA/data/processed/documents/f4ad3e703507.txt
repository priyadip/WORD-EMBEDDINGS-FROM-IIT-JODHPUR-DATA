welcome to office of alumni affairs the office of alumni affairs at the indian institute of technology jodhpur is dedicated to fostering strong lifelong connections between the institute and its alumni
our mission is to engage support and celebrate our alumni community while promoting the interests and welfare of iit jodhpur
we provide wide range of services including organizing reunions networking events and professional development opportunities
our team works to create vibrant and supportive alumni network that contributes to the growth and success of both the institute and its graduates
the office of alumni affairs plays crucial role in maintaining alumni records facilitating communication between alumni and the institute and recognizing the achievements and contributions of our alumni through various awards and honors
we are committed to enhancing the alumni experience by offering exclusive benefits volunteer opportunities and platforms for alumni to give back to iit jodhpur through mentorship guest lectures and philanthropic support
our office also collaborates with other departments to promote alumni involvement in institute activities research initiatives and student mentorship programs ensuring that the alumni community remains an integral part of iit jodhpur ongoing development
