big news iit jodhpur moves into its permanent campus the much awaited movement of iit jodhpur into its sprawling permanent campus located on nh nagaur road karwar village jodhpur city took place during may june
by shifting its entire academic activities to its permanent campus the institute crossed major milestone
this new campus has been planned meticulously and envisioned to stand as symbol of academics simple but deep
more importantly it will be an international exemplar of sustainability with strategies for ensuring net zero energy water and waste
the other salient features of the permanent campus are walking campus which is pedestrian oriented and bicycle dominant learning facilitated anywhere anytime with wireless ict backbone including enabled learning spaces with flexible shared public spaces thermally comfortable smart buildings with griha star compliant buildings and griha ld benchmark campus including dense desert settlement morphology low height buildings up to maximum of storeys built with low embodied energy materials and improved local and traditional methods plantation with native species soil stabilization protection from dusty wind to arrest erosion desertification and soil moisture over time rain water harvesting and water reduction and sewage recycling together greening the site over time and segregated wastes and customized recycling
