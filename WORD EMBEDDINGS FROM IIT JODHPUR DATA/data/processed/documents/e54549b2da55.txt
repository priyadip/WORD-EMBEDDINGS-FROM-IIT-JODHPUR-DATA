welcome to quantum information and computation what we do the quantum information and computation qic group at iit jodhpur is working towards analyzing classical and quantum correlations from the perspective of practical interface between quantum optics and quantum information processing
such correlations occupy central position in the quest for understanding and harvesting the power of quantum mechanics and fundamentals of quantum information processing
as group we are addressing some of the key issues in characterizing multiqubit entanglement
from the applications perspective the spectrum includes but is not limited to quantum key distribution quantum dense coding quantum teleportation quantum cryptography quantum game theory and quantum secure communication
for practical implementation of any quantum information task it is important to consider the role of noise on the chosen task
the group is interested in the systematic study of quantum information and computation in realistic scenarios including the effect of ambient noise using ideas and techniques of open quantum systems
