he earned his phd from the international institute of information technology hyderabad and has over years of interdisciplinary experience at the intersection of art design and research
his work focuses on indigenous knowledge systems and developing sustainable solutions for artisan communities across india
lohar has been associated with several research projects and initiatives documenting preserving and empowering traditional art practices
he has received numerous prestigious awards and fellowships including the junior fellowship from the centre for cultural resources and training ccrt and has participated in international artist residencies
his scholarly interests include indian folk aesthetics visual grammar and the media empowerment of traditional artists
through his work lohar contributes meaningfully to both academic scholarship and applications that benefit marginalized creative communities
