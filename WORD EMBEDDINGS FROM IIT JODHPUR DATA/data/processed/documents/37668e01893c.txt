the program of the department is uniquely designed to train students with required abilities viz
deep technical knowledge technical communication skills both oral and written understanding of intellectual property issues and general writing skills so as to enable them to effectively manage their professional requirement at respective work places
department has unique combination of young and experienced faculty having high research profile in the respective domains
department provides opportunities to the student to pursue advanced research in wide range of new and emerging research domains including the following advanced materials and devices astrophysics computational condensed matter physics high energy particle physics materials for energy harvesting and storage materials technologies for strategic applications molecular devices nanoscience nanotechnology optics photonics plasma physics and cold plasma technology quantum computing and information quantum optics soft matter physics the high level of research is enabled by motivated group of expert faculty members supported by large pool of advanced research equipments and computational facilities
some of the salient features of the program are as under opportunities to pursue multidisciplinary as well as transdisciplinary research provision to have more than one supervisor from same or different subject streams opportunities for participation in international collaborative projects financial support to participate in international and national conferences opportunity to use any laboratory facility of parent department as well as of any other department of the institute access to equipment at centre for advanced scientific equipment case access to central supercomputer for advanced computational work access to large number of software resources including matlab mathematica ansys hfss etc
