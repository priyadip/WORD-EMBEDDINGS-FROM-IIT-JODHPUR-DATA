research seminar by gyanendra kumar verma on nov at pm title of the talk codes for locally bounded functions date time nov at noon online abstract in this talk we first discuss several bounds on code size for read channels
subsequently we discuss general framework for recently proposed coding framework namely codes lenz et which are designed to protect the function evaluation of message against errors
we further investigate the concept of locally bounded functions for read channels and establish bounds on the optimal redundancy of codes
