about kedar dicholkar is an assistant professor at the school of design iit jodhpur
he holds an integrated master of in design from iit kanpur where his research focused on interventions in indian classical music through visualization systems thinking and exploration
dicholkar academic and research interests span ux research interaction design embodied cognition design methodology and the use of emerging media to support and cultural practices
prior to joining iit jodhpur he was associated with the symbiosis institute of design where he contributed to teaching curriculum development and mentoring for bdes and mdes students
alongside his ongoing research and creative practice of art and indian classical music kedar has been awarded scholarship from the centre for cultural resources and training ccrt
