call about dr srijit biswas is an assistant professor in the department of mechanical engineering and associate centre head of the manekshaw centre for national security studies and research at iit jodhpur
he holds phd in mechanical engineering from nit agartala
his expertise spans ic engines alternative fuels combustion performance emissions experimental and simulation uav propulsion and sustainability assessment through life cycle assessment lca assessment tea and assessment sea
prior to joining iit jodhpur he served as scientist at iit kanpur where he worked extensively on lca and tco analysis of indian transportation powertrains as well as the design development of uav engines
dr biswas has authored over scie journal papers nine conference papers and one edited book with springer
he is recipient of the bose young scientist award by isees and frequently serves as an invited speaker in various oem forums
