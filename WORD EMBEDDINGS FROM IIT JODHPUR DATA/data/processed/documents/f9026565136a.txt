css the masters in computational social science msc
css which commenced in july is the flagship program of the school of liberal arts sola indian institute of technology iit jodhpur
the program seeks to develop graduates equipped with theoretical technical and knowledge that will enable them to construct improve analyze and apply models to understand processes and phenomena
bridging disciplines and technical expertise this program is also in line with the national education policy and also with the government of india vision which seeks future skill sets for future society
program description in the first year students are exposed to introductory papers in the intersection of social science and computational methods including statistics programming in machine learning data visualization etc
these are aimed at preparing students to undertake tasks in data analytics and to derive insights
