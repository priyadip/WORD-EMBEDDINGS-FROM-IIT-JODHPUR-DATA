he joined the institute in february
prior to this he was postdoctoral fellow at carnegie mellon university cmu pittsburgh usa from and he continued at cmu as special faculty researcher until early
at cmu he worked on the belle ii experiment at superkekb japan where he contributed to the development and analysis of the central drift chamber and conducted studies in flavor physics
belle ii is one of the world leading experiments in precision flavor physics aiming to test the standard model and search for signs of new physics through rare decays and cp violation measurements
jitendra is part of the belle ii collaboration from iit jodhpur
his doctoral research conducted in collaboration with the alice large ion collider experiment experiment at the large hadron collider lhc cern switzerland focused on azimuthal correlations in collisions
alice is major experiment at the lhc dedicated to studying the plasma and the properties of strongly interacting matter under extreme conditions
following his degree jitendra served as research associate and project scientist at iit bombay
research key areas flavour physics hadron spectroscopy and qcd in physics detector studies jitendra research group at iit jodhpur is working on experimental particle physics with primary focus on flavor physics lfuv cp violation rare decays at the belle ii experiment
he also works on hadron spectroscopy pentaquarks to investigate the dynamics of quantum chromodynamics qcd in the regime
in addition the group investigates the application of techniques to enhance data analysis and search for rare processes or bsm physics
his group is also exploring quantum measurement concepts for precision measurements and foundational aspects of particle physics
