quantum information computation qic in the past thirty years significant theoretical progress has been made in quantum information science focusing on the role of entanglement and nonlocal correlations in computing paving the way for new computing paradigms with potential benefits in speed efficiency and security
handling and processing quantum information involves controlling and protecting nonlocal correlations over quantum channels
due to the delicate nature of these correlations and their susceptibility to environmental interactions scaling up quantum systems remains highly challenging
the complexity inherent in quantum theory drives both academic and industrial investment in solving these theoretical and technological problems
major tech companies including ibm google microsoft intel and others are working on issues such as control gate fidelity and scalability including quantum error correction
additionally the government of india has launched research program on quantum enabled science technology quest and the ministry of electronics and information technology meity has initiated various related projects
research groups the quantum information and computation qic group at iit jodhpur is working towards analyzing classical and quantum correlations from the perspective of practical interface between quantum optics and quantum information processing
from the application perspective the spectrum includes but is not limited to quantum key distribution quantum dense coding quantum teleportation quantum cryptography quantum game theory and quantum secure communication quantum sensing and materials research for quantum technologies
for practical implementation of any quantum information task it is important to consider the role of noise in the chosen task
