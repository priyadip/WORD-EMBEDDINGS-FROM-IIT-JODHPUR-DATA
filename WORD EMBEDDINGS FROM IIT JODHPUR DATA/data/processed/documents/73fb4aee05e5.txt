from november to april he was postdoctoral researcher at the center for energy systems research tennessee tech university usa
his research focuses on the operational planning of active electric distribution networks including renewable energy integration management control and topology optimization
his emerging interests include energy storage systems and quantum applications in power systems
vijayan has received several notable recognitions including the shankar dayal sharma medal for best student of iit kanpur the trilok chandra goel memorial gold medal for research contributing to sustainable energy and the iit kanpur excellence award for leadership in student affairs all in
he was also awarded the iit kanpur gymkhana blues the dst awsar award and the ieee india council outstanding volunteer award
he has published research in reputed venues including ieee transactions and leading international conferences
an active ieee volunteer he has served as secretary of the ieee power energy society region region and as chair of the educational and technical activities subcommittee of the ieee pes global student chapters committee
