introduction the department of humanities and social sciences hss plays unique role in an institute where the ethos of science and technology is predominant
within the coordinates of the iit system the department offers unique scope to study and comprehend the critical role of technology within context
with faculty members who specialize in diverse disciplines and students from spectrum of backgrounds the department provides an enriching platform where technical education can be complemented with human and social understanding
furthermore the department with its faculty from wide spectrum of diverse disciplines paves the way for learning and discussions on multiple issues ranging from the rigor of analysis to engagement with multiple cultures individual struggles political trends dialogue and reasoning
thus the department of hss facilitates and expansive point of view amongst the student community
these young minds that come in contact with varied knowledge systems are likely to evolve into reflective and thinking individuals with refined sensibilities
such process is expected to enable them to appreciate life more creatively effectively and holistically and contribute in positive way to the nation at large
objectives the iit system and fraternity was set up in order to create the sensibility that was necessary for newly independent developing nation
it has never been about mere production of technocrats
the humanities and social science disciplines offer iit undergraduates holistic and education making them suitable for changing global education and work ecosystem where disciplinary boundaries perish and interdisciplinarity emerges as the new norm
to achieve that the hss education in the iits introduces students to processes of knowledge production in the discipline and makes them reflect on the interconnectedness that exists between different disciplines
what we seek to create is an exciting body of new knowledge that will allow us to operate from unbiased free thinking spaces
expected graduate attributes student who has completed hss electives in the curriculum is expected to have the ability to
develop finer sensibilities experience the nuances of creativity and the power of imagination
appreciate and analyses modes of engagement with diverse aesthetic and cultural forms understand diversity of values thought and accept differences
empathize with diverse aspects of human endeavor and to be sensitive to societal needs
learning outcomes student after having hss education in the iit system is supposed to
identify and understand social issues and devise solutions built on social awareness and informed imagination
become pragmatic individual who can contribute in creating sustainable futures
gain awareness of rights as citizen and location within the wider social context reflect critically on own disciplinary biases develop sense of interdisciplinarity and acquaint herself with the various processes of knowledge production
skillsets targeted creative thinking empathy reflective learning engaging with society critical thinking logical reasoning argumentation metacognitive ability imagination ethical sensibility scientific thinking
