prior to joining iit jodhpur she spent four years january december as postdoctoral researcher at the laboratory of atmospheric chemistry paul scherrer institut switzerland
during this period she investigated the impact of various combustion technologies operating conditions and atmospheric oxidation regimes on gas and particulate emissions from residential biomass combustion
additionally her research focused on the quantification characterization and source apportionment of carbonaceous aerosols in new delhi highly polluted indian megacity
during her at iit kanpur she worked on understanding the relationship between the properties of ambient aerosols and their cloud condensation nuclei ccn activity
her doctoral thesis was titled effects of aerosol size chemical composition mixing state and volatility on cloud condensation nuclei activity ccn in the ambient atmosphere
