introduction reckoned as one of the most versatile engineering disciplines mechanical engineering virtually touches every aspect of modern life from mobile phones and biomedical devices to aircrafts and power plants
while design and manufacturing involves design of mechanisms and the analysis of materials systems include methods of energy conversions heat transfer and fluid flow
our faculty are involved in wide range of projects in the areas of energy conversion and power systems heat transfer and fluid mechanics mechanics of solid mechanical vibrations robotics autonomous unmanned vehicles design optimization acoustics and noise control control systems rotor dynamics materials and applications biomechanics bio inspired thermofluids fluid structure interaction etc
additionally efforts are being made to introduce advance concepts like smart manufacturing industry computational techniques computing applications of artificial intelligence machine learning algorithms sensors and iot as part of solving mechanical engineering problems
keeping balance between theory and the department intends to provide its students solid foundation in core as well as emerging areas of mechanical engineering by inspiring critical thinking and nurturing problem solving skills

to integrate analytical and computational ability with experimental skills to create individuals competent in professional engineering practices to stimulate critical thinking towards application of mechanical engineering concepts in interdisciplinary problem solving to expose students to advanced concepts like ai ml industry and iot for product and system design to inculcate an attitude towards commitment to engineering ethics leadership qualities and professional
