departmental seminar by rakesh arora on october at speaker rakesh arora title of the talk large class of nonlocal elliptic equations with singular nonlinearities date and time october about the speaker assistant professor department of mathematics abstract in this talk we address the questions of existence uniqueness and boundary behavior of the positive solution of posed in smooth bounded domain rn with homogeneous dirichlet or outer boundary conditions
the operator belongs to general class of nonlocal operators including typical fractional laplacians restricted fractional laplacian rfl censored fractional laplacian cfl and spectral fractional laplacian sfl
the nonlinear term covers three different amalgamations of singular nonlinearities with singular exponent in particular purely singular nonlinearity singular nonlinearity with source term and singular nonlinearity with an absorption term
based on precise analysis of the green kernel we developed new unifying approach that empowered us to construct theory for nonlocal elliptic equations involving singular nonlinearities
in particular we show the existence of critical singular exponents and which provides fairly complete classification of nonlocal elliptic equations with singular nonlinearities via subtle boundary behavior of the solution
various types of nonlocal operators are discussed to exemplify the wide applicability of our theory
