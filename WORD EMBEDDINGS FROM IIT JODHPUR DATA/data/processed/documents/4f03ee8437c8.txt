he earned his in optics and engineering physics from lund university sweden in under the supervision of edouard berrocal where he developed advanced laser diagnostics for probing optically dense media
he subsequently held postdoctoral and research scientist positions at fau erlangen in germany and jointly at the university of gothenburg in sweden and nasa jet propulsion laboratory at the california institute of technology caltech in the usa
he has been mentored by several globally renowned scientists and pioneers in their fields including lihong wang caltech murthy gudipati nasa and wolfgang heidrich kaust
mishra has received numerous accolades including the indian academy of sciences summer research fellowship visiting student fellowship from raman research institute spie student author travel award petra awards for young researchers ilass europe foundation scholarship invited speaker gordon research conference swedish research council international postdoctoral fellowship adlerbert foundation sustainable research fellowship gothenburg radhakrishnan young scientist award from kanpur and the young researcher award from erlangen graduate school in advanced optical technologies fau erlangen germany

more recently he reported recognized as the world fastest molecular mapping technique
his research has been featured in physics world eurekaalert

mishra has served as an adjunct faculty at iit indore where he founded fourier energy solutions pvt
