faculty member receives excellence in doctoral research award from iit roorkee we are delighted to share that akshay gupta assistant professor in the department of civil and infrastructure engineering has been honored with the excellence in doctoral research award by the indian institute of technology iit roorkee
the award was presented by jitendra singh hon ble minister of state independent charge for science and technology in recognition of gupta outstanding research contributions
this prestigious recognition not only highlights the quality and impact of his doctoral work but also reflects the growing research excellence and academic strength of our institution
