completed my and in mechanical engineering from iit kharagpur

for my thesis evaluated numerical schemes for shock modeling in an adaptive mesh framework
my thesis involved developing an code for simulating mixed convection film condensation using coordinate transformation and mesh to address matrix stiffness solved via perturbation methods
pursued my at the university of massachusetts dartmouth under mehdi raessi focusing on flows interacting with moving rigid bodies
due to significant interface deformation used the method on fixed grid avoiding remeshing and enabling large topological changes
this solver was applied to ocean wave energy converters yielding key insights into dynamic similarity
as postdoctoral fellow at umass dartmouth studied droplet dynamics from interactions and developed solver for evaporating deforming interfaces
