school of liberal arts indian institute of technology jodhpur report on sola exhibition at the quindecennial celebrations of iit jodhpur rachel philip assistant professor of sociology sreedevi assistant professor of english farhat naz head and associate professor of sociology the school of liberal arts showcased different aspects of its inception history and current activities at the exhibition organized on the occasion of the launch of quindecennial celebrations of iit jodhpur on march
this event also marked the inauguration of intellia the board of department societies festival
demonstrating the diversity vibrancy and novelty of its research academic output and student activities the school of liberal arts made its presence felt at the quindecennial celebrations of iit jodhpur
the sola stall hosted many visitors and saw great footfall
the chief guest of the event chandrasekhar secretary department of science and technology government of india visited the exhibition on march and interacted with the faculty and students commenting in particular the importance of the work done in the areas of sustainability psychology and environmental humanities
phd students from the school curated and presented different aspects of the exhibition over and of march as well for intellia the board of departmental societies festival for the alumni of the iit jodhpur as well as for the visiting delegates from the university of buffalo new york usa
the vision of the school of liberal arts was represented in tangible form through the various showcases of the exhibition
the exhibition was curated in manner designed to reflect the school novel open futuristic transdisciplinary and inclusive liberal humanist approach
here are some interesting highlights from the exhibition

visitors could also learn about the center of excellence on art and digital immersion adi under sola
