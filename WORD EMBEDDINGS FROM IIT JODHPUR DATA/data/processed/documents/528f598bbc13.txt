of mathematics abstract the poisson process is fundamental process widely used to model count data
in recent years subordinated poisson as the poisson process the poisson process and the fractional poisson attracted significant attention from researchers
in this talk we present several variants of generalized counting processes focusing on versions of the fractional poisson process and other generalized counting processes where the time changes are introduced via an independent subordinator and its inverse
we also highlight our current work on the tempered version of the hawkes process and its generalized form
about the speaker neha gupta currently affiliated with bombay and iim indore as research scientist on the bharatgen project
prior to this she was postdoctoral researcher at iit kanpur and before that she was research associate at isi delhi
neha gupta earned her from iit ropar
her research interests lie in fractional stochastic processes and subordinated versions of certain counting processes
