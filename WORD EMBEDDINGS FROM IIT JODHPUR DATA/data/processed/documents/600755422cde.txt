welcome to the visitors hostel the visitors hostel at the indian institute of technology jodhpur is dedicated to providing comfortable and convenient lodging facilities for visiting faculty researchers guests and dignitaries
our mission is to offer welcoming and hospitable environment that enhances the experience of visitors to the campus
we manage range of rooms and suites equipped with modern amenities to ensure pleasant stay
our facilities include dining services internet access and recreational areas designed to cater to the diverse needs of our guests
the visitors hostel is strategically located to provide easy access to the institute academic and research facilities
we aim to offer serene and conducive atmosphere allowing guests to relax and focus on their work or engagements at iit jodhpur
our team is committed to delivering exceptional service ensuring that all guests have memorable and comfortable stay
we take pride in our attention to detail and personalized approach addressing the specific requirements and preferences of each visitor
