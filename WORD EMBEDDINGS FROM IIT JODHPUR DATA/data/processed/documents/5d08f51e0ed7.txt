her academic journey includes the completion of phd from the esteemed indian institute of technology hyderabad during which she also had the opportunity to contribute as partner phd scholar with swinburne university of technology australia
sumana educational background comprises bachelor in visual arts degree bva from the college of art design university of burdwan
subsequently she completed master degree mfa from sarojini naidu school of arts and communication university of hyderabad
her creative inspiration draws from diverse array of sources ranging from short stories and novels to bengali literature cinema and hindi cinema that vividly depict different behavioural perceptions
her creative design practics manifests in both narrative explorations and representations of instantaneous thoughts inviting viewers to engage with the profound layers of meaning within each piece
her practiced acknowledged with prestigious awards such as the inlaks fine arts award in and the national academy award from lalit kala academy new delhi in elizabeth greenshields grant from canada usa
currently she is assistant professor in the school of design iit jodhpur and actively engaged with the contemporary art gallery dhi artspace located in hyderabad
