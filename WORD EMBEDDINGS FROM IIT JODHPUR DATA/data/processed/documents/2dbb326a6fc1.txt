convocation of iit jodhpur june the indian institute of technology jodhpur is proud to announce its convocation ceremony which will be held on june at the iit jodhpur campus
this significant event will celebrate the achievements of over students who will be awarded degrees across wide range of disciplines
the session will be presided over by shri kiran kumar chairman board of governors iit jodhpur
the convocation will also feature an address by avinash kumar agarwal director iit jodhpur
the undergraduate session will be honored by the presence of esteemed dignitaries with shri bhajanlal sharma hon ble chief minister of rajasthan serving as the chief guest
rudra pratap hon ble vice chancellor of plaksha university will grace the occasion as guest of honor
the postgraduate session will feature shri senapathy kris gopalakrishnan chairman of axilor ventures and managing trustee of pratiksha trust pratithi investments bengaluru as the chief guest
jagadesh kumar former chairman of the university grants commission ugc professor retd
of electrical engineering at iit delhi and former vice chancellor of jawaharlal nehru university will join as guest of honor
upholding its commitment to academic excellence and research iit jodhpur continues to shape the future of innovation and education
the convocation marks yet another milestone in the institute journey of growth and achievement
