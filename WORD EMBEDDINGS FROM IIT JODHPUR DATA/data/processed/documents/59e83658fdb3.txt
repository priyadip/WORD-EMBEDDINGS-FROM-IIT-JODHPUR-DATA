research seminar by mithun das on at pm title of the talk the pair correlation statistic in number theory and fourier optimization date time at pm abstract in this seminar we will discuss the pair correlation statistic for sequences
we briefly describe the uniform distribution and possonian pair correlation
then we discuss our current work related to gue pair correlation statistic consider analogs of montgomery form factor for zeros of the riemann zeta function in the case of arbitrary sequences satisfying some basic assumptions and connect their estimation to two extremal problems in fourier analysis which are promptly studied
as applications we provide average bounds of form factors related to some sequences of number theoretic interest such as the zeros of primitive elements of the selberg class dedekind zeta functions and the real and imaginary parts of the riemann zeta function
in the last case our results bear an implication to conjecture of gonek and ki showing it can not hold in some situations
this is joint work with ismoilov and ramos from sissa trieste
