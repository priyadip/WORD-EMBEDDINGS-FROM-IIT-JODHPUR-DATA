grievance redressal committee institute grievance redressal committee iit jodhpur constituted the institute grievance redressal committee to address the grievances of the employees of the institute in an effective manner
the grievances received by the institute are forwarded to the committee to redress the issues based on seriousness
the objectives of the institute grievance redressal committee are to address grievance received from the employees of the institute if any arising in discharging their duties submit report to the director within two weeks of receiving the grievance after due consultation with the employee concerned
the employees may submit their grievances at email constitution of the institute grievance redressal committee
