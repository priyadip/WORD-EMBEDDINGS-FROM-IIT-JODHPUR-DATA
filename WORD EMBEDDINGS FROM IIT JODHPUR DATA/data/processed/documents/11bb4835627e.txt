he is also affiliated with the rishabh centre for research and innovation in clean energy
his research interests are blend of theory and practice focusing on the design and development of clean energy technologies using experimental and numerical techniques
his investigations span wide range of areas including micro combustors scramjets microgas turbines spray diagnostics gasturbines and fundamental droplet studies
he has been awarded multiple research grants including those from the royal academy of engineering uk the ministry of power mop india the ministry of education moe india centre for high technology cht india cesc rajasthan renewable energy corporation and ge vernova
he was also awarded the leaders in innovation fellowship rae uk and leeds institute of fluid dynamics visiting fellow uk
before joining iitj he was postdoctoral researcher at eindhoven university of technology and delft university of technology in the netherlands
he earned his phd in turbulent combustion modelling at eindhoven university of technology
he also holds an mtech from iit madras and btech from kalyani government engineering college
before his doctoral studies sudipto gained valuable experience working for over seven years in the engineering and industrial services division of tata consultancy services leading indian multinational corporation
