khel khel mein vigyan multilingual stem comics for children khel khel mein vigyan is unique series of comic books for children that brings science technology engineering and mathematics stem concepts to life through fun relatable and exciting narratives
each story is thoughtfully designed to ignite curiosity promote critical thinking and help children see how stem is woven into their everyday experiences
the first two storybooks of this series were launched on june in the august presence of speaker of lok sabha shri om birla
joint initiative of the center for education technology for education cete and school of design iit jodhpur the khel khel mein vigyan storybooks are now launched in indian languages english hindi assamese bengali odia punjabi gujarati marathi kannada telugu malayalam tamil supporting children in learning science through their matribhasha
this initiative aligns with the national education policy nep which emphasizes multilingual education and the importance of conceptual learning in one native language in formative years
by combining storytelling with stem the series nurtures foundational thinking promotes inclusivity and fosters love for science from an early learning both accessible and joyful for children across diverse linguistic backgrounds
