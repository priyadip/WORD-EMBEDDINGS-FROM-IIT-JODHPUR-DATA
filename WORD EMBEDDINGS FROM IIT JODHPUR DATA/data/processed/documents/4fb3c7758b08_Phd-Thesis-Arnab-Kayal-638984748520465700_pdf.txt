spectral projection methods for integral and equations arnab kayal p20ma001 supervisor moumita mandal department of mathematics iit jodhpur abstract integral and equations are widely used in various scientific and engineering fields
finding highly accurate approximate solutions often poses significant challenges especially when dealing with singularities in the kernel and tions
this thesis explores the development of spectral projection methods to find imate solutions for various types of linear and nonlinear integral and equations as well as eigenvalue problems of the integral operators
we develop jacobi spectral galerkin approach for finding approximate solutions of the linear fredholm integral equations with an algebraic weakly singular kernel
the exact solution to this type of equation is generally
our approach incorporates the singular part of the kernel into single jacobi weight function
this enables us to achieve superconvergence rates in both supremum and weighted l2 norms for solutions using the iterated jacobi spectral galerkin method
we also propose jacobi spectral method and its iterated version based on the galerkin approach and obtain superconvergence results
additionally we improve the convergence rate of all the proposed methods by improving the regularity of exact solution using smoothing transformation
further we apply our proposed jacobi spectral galerkin approach to address the eigenvalue problem of fredholm integral operators with an algebraic weakly singular kernel on banach space
using this approach we achieve superconvergence rates for the approximate eigenvalues obtained by the jacobi spectral method
superconvergence for the approximate eigenfunctions is obtained by the iterated jacobi spectral method
next we investigate class of nonlinear fredholm equations with smooth kernel
we find that the legendre spectral and iterated version of galerkin and methods yield convergence results despite the presence of unbounded differential operator
further we explore the nonlinear weakly singular fredholm integral equations of hammerstein type
our proposed iterated jacobi spectral galerkin method achieves similar superconvergence results for solutions as observed in the linear case
additionally we strate the applicability of the jacobi spectral galerkin approach for class of nonlinear weakly singular equations achieving superconvergence
finally we consider algebraic weakly singular volterra integral equations and propose chebyshev spectral galerkin collocation and methods to obtain approximate solutions
we achieve superconvergence for the chebyshev spectral and methods after the smoothing tion and obtain sharp error bounds in the supremum norm by leveraging the behaviour of the exact solution
numerical implementations of all the proposed methods are carried out to verify the theoretical results
