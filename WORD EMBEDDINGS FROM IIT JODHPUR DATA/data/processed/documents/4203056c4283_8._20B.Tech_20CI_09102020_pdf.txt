introduction the rapidly urbanizing society and increasing quality of life demand reliable and intelligent infrastructure systems which are capable of catering to the societal needs at different scales from an individual to the community level
through this undergraduate program in civil infrastructure engineering iit jodhpur is making stride to imagine the course structure that incorporates and integrates the elements of conventional civil engineering with advanced transformative technologies such as artificial intelligence ai cps digital twins dt and automated management and information systems
additionally major thrust is also planned on the design implementation and maintenance of scale integrated infrastructure systems across different domains
objective of the program to provide an expanded but holistic understanding of different civil and infrastructure systems and an understanding of the differences similarities and relations between different scales and components of it
to produce graduates with an ability to integrate conventional methods with emerging technologies to design intelligent civil and infrastructure systems from multidisciplinary perspective
to provide exposure to techniques such as iot machine learning big data physical systems and digital twin dt in the design and maintenance of different civil and infrastructure systems
expected graduate attribute
strong foundation in the analytical experimental and computational methods to study various aspects of civil and infrastructure systems
thorough understanding of green and sustainable materials practices and principles to design resilient infrastructure systems
an ability to understand problems related to air and water pollution solid waste management ecological and environmental degradation and their monitoring and remediation
ability to adopt safe methodologies coupled with advanced technologies in the design and construction of civil and infrastructure systems
ability to monitor and evaluate health of old structures and renovating to bring sustainability
an understanding of the use of digital twins dt cps iot artificial intelligence ai in designing monitoring surveillance and security of infrastructures
an innate ability to take multidisciplinary approach to problem solving and willingness to go beyond conventional paths
strong understanding of structural engineering and its application in reinforced concrete prestressed concrete and steel structure design
knowledge of geotechnical engineering for ground improvement and foundation
knowledge of pavement design traffic planning and highway design for urban transport
knowledge of critical experiments and advanced mathematical models for environmental engineering
knowledge of gis and remote sensing for application in agriculture environment exploitation and constructing digital twin of built environment
knowledge of smart and sustainable material and best practices for construction and infrastructure
strong foundation in computational methods and artificial intelligence ai for application in various civil and infrastructure problems
knowledge of advanced visualization techniques and digital twins for application in smart infrastructure
knowledge of town planning logistics and layout engineering
