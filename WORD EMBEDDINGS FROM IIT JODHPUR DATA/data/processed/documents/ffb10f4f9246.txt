kumar joined the department of electrical engineering at the indian institute of technology iit jodhpur as an assistant professor in
at iit jodhpur kumar directs the wearable robotics and control werocon laboratory
his research lies at the intersection of robotics control and biomechanics with focus on wearable robots including powered prosthetic legs and exoskeletons
he develops nonlinear adaptive and control methods that personalize assistance to each user while ensuring robust performance across everyday terrains and tasks
