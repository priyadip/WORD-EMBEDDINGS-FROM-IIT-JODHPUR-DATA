her academic journey includes phd in mathematics from iiser mohali followed by postdoctoral fellowships at iiser pune under the nbhm and serb national post doctoral fellowships
she was awarded gold medal for securing the first position in her msc mathematics program at panjab university in
she has been serving as faculty member at iit jodhpur since
she has taught wide range of mathematics courses including real analysis number theory abstract algebra linear algebra optimization and specialized subjects like group theory for machine learning and representations of finite groups
she is also teaching regularly in national level schools sponsored by national center of mathematics ncm and indian women and mathematics iwm
she has also organized workshops and academic programs such as the annual foundation women in maths progress and challenges and the online sagemath workshop
in research kaur has made significant contributions to algebra particularly in the areas of finite groups conjugacy classes and representations of finite groups quadratic forms quandles and quandle rings
she is the principal investigator pi of project titled computations in wreath product funded by the science and engineering research board serb under the matrics scheme
