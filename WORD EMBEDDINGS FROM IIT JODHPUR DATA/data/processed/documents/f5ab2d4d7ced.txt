source spaces are real complex real sasakian kenmotsu cosymplectic and almost space forms
to support these general forms in the particular cases when the target or source spaces are real complex sasakian and kenmotsu space forms we verify known casorati inequalities for riemannian maps and riemannian submersions
further we give casorati inequalities for invariant and riemannian maps resp
toward information on geometric characteristics we discuss the equality cases
we also exemplify the general forms
