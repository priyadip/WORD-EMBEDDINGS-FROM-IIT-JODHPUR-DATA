of mathematics abstract recently higgs bundles have received lot of attention
higgs bundle over an algebraic curve is vector bundle with twisted endomorphism which roughly speaking can be thought of as matrix of on the curve
an important question is to calculate the volume of the groupoid of higgs bundles over finite fields
in schiffmann succeeded in finding the corresponding generating function and together with mozgovoy reduced the task to counting pairs of vector bundles and nilpotent endomorphisms
it was recently generalized by mellit to the case of vector bundles with nilpotent endomorphisms preserving flags at marked points
an important part of mellit calculation is counting in the case of and marked points that allows him to relate the corresponding generating function with the macdonald polynomials
it is natural and important question to generalize mellit calculations to arbitrary reductive groups
in this talk will start by discussing the generalization to the counting of principal with nilpotent sections in the case of and two points for an arbitrary split reductive group and show that we get an explicit formula in this case
after that will give summary of my other completed projects explicit formulas of mixed hodge polynomials of character varieties of nilpotent groups joint work with li and and tropical geometry joint work with zeitlin
will then mention the projects that am currently working on motivic classes of moduli spaces of quiver representations joint work with li mozgovoy and reineke and on formal punctured disc joint work with kashyap and sage
finally will mention projects that am planning to work on in the coming future
