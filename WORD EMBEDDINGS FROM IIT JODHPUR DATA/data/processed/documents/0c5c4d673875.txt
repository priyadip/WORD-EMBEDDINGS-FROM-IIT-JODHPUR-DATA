welcome to the office of estate the office of estate at the indian institute of technology jodhpur is dedicated to managing and maintaining the institute physical infrastructure and assets
our mission is to ensure safe and sustainable campus environment that supports academic excellence and fosters vibrant community
we oversee the planning development and maintenance of campus facilities including buildings roads utilities and landscaping
our team is committed to implementing best practices in estate management ensuring that all infrastructure projects meet the highest standards of quality and safety
the office of estate is responsible for space allocation ensuring that all departments and units have the facilities they need to operate effectively
we also manage campus services such as housekeeping security and transportation ensuring comfortable and secure environment for students faculty and staff
sustainability is key focus of our office and we are committed to implementing green practices in campus operations
this includes building designs waste management programs and water conservation initiatives
through proactive maintenance and strategic planning we aim to enhance the functionality and aesthetics of the campus supporting the institute mission of excellence in education and research
