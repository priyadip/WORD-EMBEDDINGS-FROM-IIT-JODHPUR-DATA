due to lower loss siw is becoming popular to design components especially for narrow band applications
the loss of such structures can further be reduced by using empty siw esiw technology
attractive properties like compact size lower implementation cost and higher power handling make siw and esiw suitable to design passive microwave components like filters diplexers multiplexers power dividing and combining networks antennas
on the other hand its ease of integration with other planar circuits like microstrip cpw makes it suitable to implement active components like amplifiers mixers oscillators
