congratulations to moumita mandal for the acceptance of her research paper in the journal of analysis title novel image inpainting framework using regression authors maiti kumar jain and bhatnagar journal acm transactions on internet technology year publisher acm abstract in this paper regression based image inpainting framework is proposed
the core idea is to fill the unknown region in two stages extrapolate the edges to the unknown region and then fill the unknown pixels values in each demarcated by the extended edges
canny edge detection and linear edge extension are used to respectively identify and extend edges to the unknown region followed by regression within each to predict the unknown pixel values
two different regression models based on neighbours and support vectors machine svm are used to predict the unknown pixel values
the proposed framework has the advantage of inpainting without requiring prior training on any image dataset
the extensive experiments on different images with contrasting distortions demonstrate the robustness of the proposed framework and detailed comparative analysis shows that the proposed technique outperforms existing image inpainting methods
finally the proposed techniques are applied to mri images suffering from susceptibility artifacts to illustrate the practical usage of the proposed work
