shrutidhara sarma receives prestigious women in sensors wiley award at fleps shrutidhara sarma assistant professor department of mechanical engineering indian institute of technology jodhpur and pi of fern lab has been honoured with the women in sensors wiley best paper award at the ieee fleps conference held in national university of singapore nus singapore
the award recognizes her outstanding contribution to the field of flexible sensors through the paper titled assistive living and wireless emergency alert system for users
this innovative work presents wearable wireless sensing platform designed to enhance safety and autonomy for individuals with limited mobility
the ieee fleps international conference on flexible and printable sensors and systems is premier global forum that brings together researchers engineers and scientists to share the latest developments in flexible and printable sensors and systems and their applications across healthcare wearables iot and smart systems
