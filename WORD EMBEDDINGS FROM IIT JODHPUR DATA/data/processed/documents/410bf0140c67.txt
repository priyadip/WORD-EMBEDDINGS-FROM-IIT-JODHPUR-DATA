program in bridge engineering the department of civil and infrastructure engineering at iit jodhpur proudly announces the launch of an
program in bridge engineering initiative in india
this specialized program is designed to equip students with the expertise needed to tackle the growing challenges in bridge design construction and maintenance
with india infrastructure expanding rapidly the demand for highly skilled bridge engineers has never been greater
this program offers core courses in structural analysis bridge design and advanced mechanics alongside electives in prestressed concrete bridges and structural health monitoring
this initiative aligns with india viksit bharat vision ensuring that the next generation of engineers is prepared to drive innovation in infrastructure development
