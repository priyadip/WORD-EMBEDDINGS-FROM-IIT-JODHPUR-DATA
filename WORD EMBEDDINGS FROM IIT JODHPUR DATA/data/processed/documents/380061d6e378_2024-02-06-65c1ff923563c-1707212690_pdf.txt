postgraduate diploma in next generation omics technologies and applications program structure certificate and diploma course in next generation omics technologies and applications
introduction the certificate and diploma course in next generation omics technologies and applications is holistic training program designed to meet the requirements of people from different backgrounds such as healthcare professional students people from industry or academia as well as service provider
the program would cater to the needs of upskilling training and orientation of different based techniques and the analysis of data and their applications towards healthcare pathogens clinical nutrition and agriculture
recent advancements and wide use of technology are producing an explosion of biological datasets that serve as source for computational and statistical modeling of diseases and biological processes
the analysis of various omics datasets can provide better understanding of the underlying disease mechanism prevention and prediction
moreover sequencing data analysis helps medical professionals in clinical applications as well as enable advanced understanding of basic biological problems
this program provides an overview of the technologies for massively parallel sequencing technologies for addressing different biological questions
moreover this program will focus on concepts for developing and implementing computational methods for learning from omics data managing those data and using the data for biological research to improve human health
objective of the programme the objective of this programme is to cultivate an understanding of studies technologies and computational methods for analysis of datasets
the course aims to provide the cutting edge skill sets to efficiently plan for experiments generate the different layers of biological data analyze and interpret the outcomes
expected attributes ability to understand different technologies
capability to design the experiment for conducting omics studies
gain an aptitude to apply the knowledge for different omics applications
ability to apply machine learning statistics and computational methods for analysis of the different omics data
learning outcome knowledge of available technologies including their comparison in terms of advantages and disadvantages for specific applications
design study efficiently including effective budget planning
knowledge of different ngs and microarray experiments and their possible applications
knowledge of computational and statistical concepts for analysis of omics datasets
develop skills to interpret the analysis results for biological inference
efficiently plan and manage technical projects in different omics domains
