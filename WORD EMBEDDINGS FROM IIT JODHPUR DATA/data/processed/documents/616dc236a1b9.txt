of mathematics abstract linear codes form large family of codes
due to their algebraic properties they are the most studied codes from the mathematical point of view
in this talk we will be focusing on two families of linear codes and our work on determining their parameters
after reviewing the basics of linear codes we will introduce family of linear codes called toric codes over hypersimplices
these codes were defined by jaramillo pinto and villarreal in
they determined the dimension and minimum distance of these codes
in this talk we will discuss our work on computing the weight of these codes using tools from bases theory
following this we will discuss our work on the number of minimal weight codewords and weight codewords of these toric codes
these are joint works with carvalho ufu brazil
then we will discuss another parameter of linear codes called generalized hamming weights
we will demonstrate how tools from bases theory can also be used to determine the generalized hamming weights of these codes
this is joint work with my supervisor sanjay kumar singh iiser bhopal
we will conclude this talk by discussing my other works in brief and potential future directions
