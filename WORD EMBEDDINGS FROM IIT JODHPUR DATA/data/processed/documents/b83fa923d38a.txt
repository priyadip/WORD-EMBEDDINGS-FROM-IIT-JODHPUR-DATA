message from the head of the department welcome to the department of mathematics
the department of mathematics is dedicated to advancing the fields of pure mathematics applied mathematics and statistics
our vision is to emphasize both the fundamental principles of core mathematics and the foundational concepts essential for analyzing key principles applied in technologies
since its establishment the department has been actively engaged in research across various critical areas of mathematics and computing
the department also aimed to explore modern aspects of mathematics coupled with computational capabilities to develop workforce specialized in mathematical aspects of key technologies
with this inclination the department initiated academic programs to address the mathematical needs of both industry and academia
currently we offer various programmes namely
program in mathematics program in and computational sciences two year program in data and computational sciences an integrated teacher in education program itep with specialization in mathematics and
program with specialization in different areas of mathematics computing and data and computational sciences
the department also offers minor program in data science for the ug students
the department faculty are active researchers in different areas namely algebra complex analysis topological dynamics lowdimensional chaos renormalization in dynamics dynamics in neuroscience mathematical physics scientific computation numerical analysis optimization techniques partial differential equations wavelet analysis fractional transform theory image processing financial risk analysis categorical data analysis reliability theory financial mathematics and applied probability
