research seminar by asha kumari meena on july at am title of the talk domain invariant numerical schemes for hyperbolic balance laws date time and venue july am pm seminar hall dept
abstract the systems of hyperbolic balance laws arise in several areas especially in computational fluid dynamics cfd and plasma flows
due to lack of theory for the continuous problems designing of stable numerical algorithms for the systems of hyperbolic balance laws is highly
we are interested in high order numerical schemes for fluid flow and plasma flow models which are robust
robustness is achieved by designing positivity preserving numerical schemes
positivity of the scheme is indeed desirable
otherwise we might lose the hyperbolicity of the system and the solution produced by the numerical scheme will have no physical meaning
in particular we consider the discontinuous galerkin method finite difference method and finite volume method
the weno reconstructions are also used to get high order accuracy in finite difference and finite volume methods
the schemes are used to tackle the structure of the models
here we will discuss the construction of these numerical schemes for fluid flow and plasma flow models and their performance by numerical experiments
about the speaker asha kumari meena is currently working as an assistant professor in the department of mathematics at the central university of rajasthan
she earned her in mathematics from
delhi in followed by research fellowship at the tifr centre for applicable mathematics in bangalore
her research interests lie in the areas of scientific computing for fluid and plasma flow models traffic flow modeling and numerical schemes such as finite volume weno and discontinuous galerkin methods
