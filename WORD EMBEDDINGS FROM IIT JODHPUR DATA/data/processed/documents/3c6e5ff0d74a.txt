furthermore the data these sensors collect is essential for enhancing the intelligence of processes and systems
iot encompasses broad range of disciplines from material science to data analytics all vital for developing iot systems
to address these complex needs an research platform idrp has been established for the center for aiot and applications
this platform aims to integrate technologies comprehensively fostering research and development across sectors like industry health and infrastructure which includes safety and environmental monitoring
the collaborative ecosystem in jodhpur which brings together institutions like iitj aiims nlu and police university is poised to drive innovation in aiot in areas such as healthcare environment and cybersecurity
