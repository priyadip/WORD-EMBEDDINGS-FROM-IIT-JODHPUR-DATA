seminars and meetings workshop on sustainability of cement and concrete in the indian context cement and concrete are vital to india infrastructure growth enabling urbanization transportation housing and industrial development
however the sector is and major source of carbon emissions
in line with india climate commitments environmentally responsible practices must be adopted across the entire material production and mix design to construction durability and management
to address these challenges the department of civil and infrastructure engineering at indian institute of technology jodhpur in collaboration with the indian concrete institute rajasthan chapter is organizing workshop on sustainable cement and concrete technologies
the workshop will feature expert talks on materials alternative binders supplementary cementitious materials standards design and enabling policies fostering collaboration among academia industry and policymakers
