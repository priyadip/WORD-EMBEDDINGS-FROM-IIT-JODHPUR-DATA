welcome to the office of inclusivity cell the objectives of the inclusivity cell are given below works towards the institute commitment to maintaining work environment wherein faculty staff and students from different communities can work in coherent environment without discrimination on the grounds of caste creed age disability gender reassignment partnership race religion belief sex or sexual orientation implementation of government of india reservation policies welfare measures for scheduled castes sc scheduled tribes st other backward class obc persons with disability pwd economically weaker section ews lgbtq other minority communities about the admission of students the recruitment of faculty and positions and other benefits as applicable responsible for addressing academic and issues and problems faced by the students belonging to the scheduled castes sc scheduled tribes st other backward class obc persons with disability pwd economically weaker section ews lgbtq other minority communities also responsible for addressing the received from faculty and staff members belonging to these communities of the institute the cell maintains dedicated web page on the institute website to enable the person to lodge complaints if any without discrimination on the grounds of caste creed age disability gender reassignment partnership race religion belief sex or sexual orientation the cell is also entrusted with organizing events towards the sensitization among the students faculty and staff to desist from any act of discrimination against caste creed age disability gender reassignment partnership race religion belief sex or sexual orientation
