natfoe symposium iit jodhpur the national frontiers of engineering symposium was launched by the indian national academy of engineering inae in as its annual flagship event
past symposia were organized at iit delhi february iit delhi april iit madras october igcar kalpakkam september siksha anusandhan university bhubaneswar august iit hyderabad september iit guwahati october and iit gandhinagar september
this year inae hosted its ninth edition the national frontiers of engineering symposium in jodhpur during june
this symposium was organized by arun kumar singh assistant professor department of electrical engineering indian institute of technology jodhpur
the symposium brought together about active engineers ages years from companies universities and government laboratories to discuss research and technical work across range of engineering fields
convening engineers from disparate fields and challenging them to think about developments and problems at the frontiers of areas different from their own lead to variety of desirable results
these include collaborative work transfer of new techniques and approaches across fields and establishment of contacts among the next generation of leaders in engineering
the format of symposium encouraged informal collective as well as discussions among participants
speakers focused their talks on current research in their disciplines to colleagues outside their field and to address questions such as what are the major research problems and distinctive tools of your field
what are the current limitations in advancing your field
how might insight derived from other fields contribute to overcoming these limitations
this year the young engineer participants and inae fellows from iits iisc drdo laboratories and industries participated in the event
the participating agencies were indian institute of bombay delhi gandhinagar jodhpur kanpur roorkee ropar iisc bangalore bisr jaipur drdo laboratories defence laboratory defence electronics application laboratory vehicle research and development establishment combat vehicle research and development establishment and industries like tata motors reliance power bidgley india pvt
