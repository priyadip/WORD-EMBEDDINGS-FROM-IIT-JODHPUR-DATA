alkylation of heterocycles via palladium catalysis
senapati hota kloene empel murarka and koenigs angew
methods enabling direct alkylation of heterocycles are of fundamental importance in the modification of natural products bioactive molecules and medicinally relevant compounds
however there is scarcity of general strategy for the direct alkylation of variety of heterocycles using commercially available alkyl surrogates
we report an operationally simple direct alkylation of heterocycles using alkyl halides under the visible light irradiation with good scalability and functional group tolerance
our studies suggest that the photoinduced alkylation proceeds through cascade of events comprising alkyl radical addition deprotonation and oxidation
combination of experiments and computations was employed for the generalization of this strategy which was successfully translated towards the modification of natural products and pharmaceuticals
