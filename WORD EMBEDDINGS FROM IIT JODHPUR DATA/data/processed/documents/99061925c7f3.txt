research seminar by vidya sagar on nov at pm title of the talk construction of certain binary linear codes and their applications date time nov at pm abstract in recent years the study of linear codes over finite rings has attracted significant attention due to their rich algebraic structure and their potential to yield efficient classes of binary codes
in this seminar we begin by exploring construction of linear codes over finite ring and discuss the underlying algebraic framework that makes these constructions particularly interesting
we obtain many families of binary linear codes with desirable properties
several structural features and parameters of these codes will be analyzed
finally we highlight some interesting applications of these binary codes
