Scholarships and Placements
(1) Scholarships and financial assistanceships The awarded scholarships range from National and State SC/ ST schemes like NSP, Top Class etc; MCM (Merit-Cum-Means) to state scholarships Fee Remission to SC/ST students, CSIR/UGC/MHRD grants and other scholarships (2) Placements Sets up internships, trainings and job interviews for the students Industries range from Core Engineering Industries, IT & IT enabled services, Manufacturing and Consultancy Firms to  Investment & Finance Companies, Management Organisations and R&D laboratories Recent trends:- Computer Science and Engineering- 95% placed with an average package of Rs 15-17 Lakhs per annum, Mechanical Engineering- 85% placed with an average package of Rs 9-10 Lakhs per annum
