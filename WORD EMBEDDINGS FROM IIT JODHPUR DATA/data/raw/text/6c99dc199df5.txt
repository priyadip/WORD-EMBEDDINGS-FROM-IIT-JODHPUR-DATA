Message From The Head Of The Department
Welcome to the Department of Mathematics!! The  Department  of  Mathematics  is  dedicated  to  advancing  the  fields  of  pure  mathematics, 
applied  mathematics  and  statistics.  Our  vision  is  to  emphasize  both  the  fundamental  principles  of 
core  mathematics  and  the  foundational  concepts  essential  for  analyzing  key  principles  applied  in 
cutting-edge  technologies.  Since  its  establishment,  the  department  has  been  actively  engaged  in 
research across various critical areas of mathematics and computing. The  department  also  aimed  to  explore  modern  aspects  of  mathematics  coupled  with 
computational  capabilities  to  develop  a  workforce  specialized  in  mathematical  aspects  of  key 
technologies.  With  this  inclination,  the  department  initiated  academic  programs  to  address  the 
mathematical  needs  of  both  industry  and  academia.  Currently,  we  offer  various  programmes, 
namely,  a  two-year  M.Sc.  program  in  Mathematics,  a  four-year  M.Sc-M.Tech  program  in 
Mathematics-Data  and  Computational  Sciences,  a  two  year  M.Tech  program  in  Data  and 
Computational  Sciences,  an  Integrated  Teacher  in  Education  Program  (ITEP)  with  specialization 
in  Mathematics,  and  a  Ph.D.  Program  with  specialization  in  different  areas  of  Mathematics, 
computing  and  Data  and  Computational  Sciences.  The  department  also  offers  a  Minor  program 
in Data Science for the UG students. The  department  faculty  are  active  researchers  in  different  areas,  namely,  Algebra,  Complex 
Analysis,  Topological  Dynamics,  LowDimensional  Chaos,  Renormalization  in  Low-dimensional 
dynamics,  Dynamics  in  Neuroscience,  Mathematical  Physics,  Scientific  Computation,  Numerical 
Analysis,  Optimization  Techniques,  Partial  Differential  Equations,  Wavelet  Analysis,  Fractional 
Transform  Theory,  Image  Processing,  Financial  Risk  Analysis,  Categorical  Data  Analysis, 
Reliability  Theory,  Financial  Mathematics  and  Applied  Probability.  The  department  has  all 
necessary research infrastructures including High Performance Computing.
