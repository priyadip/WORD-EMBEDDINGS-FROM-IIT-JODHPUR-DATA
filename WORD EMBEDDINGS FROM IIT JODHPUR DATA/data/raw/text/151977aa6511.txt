Prof. Kaushal Desai selected as an Associated Editor for the ASME- Journal of Computing and Information Science in Engineering (ASME-JCISE)
Prof. Kaushal Desai selected as an Associated Editor for the ASME- Journal of Computing and Information Science in Engineering (ASME-JCISE)
Prof. Kaushal Desai, Professor, Mechanical Engineering Department, have been nominated as an Associated Editor for the ASME- Journal of Computing and Information Science in Engineering (ASME-JCISE)- (https://asmedigitalcollection.asme.org/computingengineering). ASME-JCISE is a leading journal publishing articles on Algorithms, Computational Methods, Computing Infrastructure, Computer-Interpretable Representations, Human-Computer Interfaces, Information Science, and/or System Architectures that aim to improve product and system lifecycle (e.g., design, manufacturing, operation, maintenance, disposal, recycling, etc.).
