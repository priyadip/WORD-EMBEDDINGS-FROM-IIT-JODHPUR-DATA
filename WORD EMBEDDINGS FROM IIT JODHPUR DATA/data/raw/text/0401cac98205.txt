Vigilance Policy (Anti- Bribery & Anti-Corruption Policy)
Vigilance Policy (Anti- Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy) of Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur plays a key role in ensuring that adequate procedures are in place in order to prevent any activity relating to Bribery, Corruption, illegal gratification and improper benefits. The Policy is aligned with the Government’s drive against anti-bribery & corruption and promotes transparency in all possible ways. Part-time Chief Vigilance Officer, IIT Jodhpur deals with the vigilance matters as per the guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC), Government of India
