Congratulations to Dr Sandip Murarka on his appointment as an Associate Editor of RSC Advances, a prestigious journal published by the Royal Society of Chemistry (RSC).
This recognition reflects his significant contributions to organic synthesis and catalysis and brings distinction to both the Department of Chemistry and the Institute. We wish him every success in this important editorial role.
