Updates Maker Bhavan MoU + IIT Jodhpur has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Anand Rathi Financial Services Limited and Maker Bhavan Foundation on 17th July 2025, to establish 'Anand Rathi Tinkerers' Lab. The MoU was signed by Shri Krishnav Gupta, Associate Director, Anand Rathi Financial Services Limited, and Prof. Ankita Sharma, Dean of Student Affairs, IIT Jodhpur & Ms. Damayanti Bhattacharya, CEO, Maker Bhavan Foundation, in the august presence of Prof. Avinash Kumar Agarwal, Director, IIT Jodhpur. The project aims to set up a state-of-the-art Tinkering Lab at IIT Jodhpur to foster an innovation culture and problem-solving among students.

Awards & Recognition CII Industry-Academia Partnership Awards 2025 + IIT Jodhpur Wins CII Industry–Academia Partnership Award 2025 ( Diamond Category ) Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur has been selected under the Diamond Category for the CII Industry–Academia Partnership Award 2025, recognising the Institute’s sustained commitment to industry engagement, collaborative research, and innovation-driven excellence. The award was conferred during the CII Annual Summit held at the India Habitat Centre, New Delhi, on December 5, 2025, in the august presence of the Hon’ble Minister and senior government dignitaries. The award was received on behalf of IIT Jodhpur by Prof. Kaushal A Desai, Dean (Resources & Alumni). CII Industry-Academia Partnership Awards 2024 + IIT Jodhpur was proudly recognized with the "Grand Jury" Award in the Government Institute category at the 1st edition of the CII Industry-Academia Partnership Awards 2024. This prestigious accolade acknowledges the Institute's consistent efforts in building long-term, high-impact collaborations with industry. The award underscores IIT Jodhpur's leadership in driving innovation, translational research, and knowledge exchange. The Office of Corporate Relations also set up a dynamic exhibition stall during the CII Annual Summit on Technology, Intellectual Property and Industry Academia Partnership 2024 on 12 December 2024. IIT Jodhpur is Academic Partner at the "The India CSR & Sustainability Conclave" + IIT Jodhpur has been on boarded as the Academic Partner for the “The India CSR & Sustainability Conclave” organised by Brands Global Media, scheduled on 2nd August 2025 at the India International Centre, New Delhi. Dr. Kirti Sankhala, Assistant Professor, CETSD, will be a Panel Speaker on the topic “Vision 2047: Building a Net- Zero, Inclusive, and Resilient India” and Dr. Chandana N, Assistant Professor, CETSD, shall participate as an Expert in the sustainability project/case study segment in the conclave. The India CSR & Sustainability Conclave is a flagship national platform that convenes over 150+ stakeholders from across the CSR, ESG, sustainability, social enterprise, and innovation ecosystem. The conclave features Expert panel discussions, Knowledge-sharing keynotes, Exhibitions, Networking opportunities and ICSF Awards 2025 ceremony.
