Mr.Shubham Chamola has received the prestigious Commonwealth Fellowship to work at the University of Cambridge, UK for a duration of one year (Nov 2024-Oct 2025)
Mr.Shubham Chamola , PhD student of Dr. Shahab Ahmad, has received the prestigious Commonwealth Fellowship to work at the University of Cambridge, UK for a duration of one year (Nov 2024-Oct 2025). Mr. Shubham will be working jointly with Prof. Michael De Volder and Dr. Shahab Ahmad towards the development of Photorechargeable batteries.
