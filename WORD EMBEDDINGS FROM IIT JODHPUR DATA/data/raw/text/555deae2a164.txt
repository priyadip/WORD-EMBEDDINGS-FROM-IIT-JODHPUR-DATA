11th National Conference on Technology and Innovation in Mathematics Education (TIME)
The TIME conferences are organized in every two years which
serve a dual role: as a forum in which mathematics educators and teachers will
come together to discuss and to probe major issues associated with the
integration of technology in mathematics teaching and learning. It is a place
where they can share their perspectives, personal experiences, and innovative
teaching practices. TIME 2023 will be the 11th edition in this series. The 10th
TIME conference was held online.   For
more details, see https://www.facebook.com/TIME4math/ How to participate? You can attend the conference as a participant. Please
submit the registration form at the earliest. (Deadline for early bird
registration:  15 November, 2023) https://forms.gle/KoPKvPYCCGVjye6y8 If you are interested in presenting your work at the
conference, first you need to register for the conference, and then submit an
abstract of your proposed presentation. ( Deadline for abstract
submission:  15 November, 2023) https://forms.gle/ikYcTyMWRLapEsReA Participation
