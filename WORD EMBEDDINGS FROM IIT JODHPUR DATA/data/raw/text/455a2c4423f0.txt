Aashish Mathur Associate Professor school Ph.D.: Indian Institute of Technology Delhi email aashishmathur[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Optical Wireless Communications, Physical Layer Security, 5G and Beyond Communications call 0291 280 1362 language Web Profile About Aashish
Mathur (Senior Member, IEEE) received the B.E. degree (Hons.) in electronics
and instrumentation engineering from the Birla Institute of Technology and
Science (BITS) Pilani, Pilani, India, in 2011, the M.Tech. degree in
telecommunication technology and management from the Indian Institute of
Technology (IIT) Delhi, New Delhi, India, in 2013, and the Ph.D. degree in power line communications from the Department of Electrical
Engineering, IIT Delhi, in 2016. He was a Software Engineer with Intel Technology India Pvt. Ltd., Bengaluru,
India, briefly before joining IIT Delhi for his Ph.D. in 2013. He is currently
an Associate Professor with the Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT
Jodhpur, Jodhpur, India. He has also worked as an Assistant Professor with the
Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, BITS Pilani and the Department of Electronics Engineering, IIT (BHU) Varanasi,
Varanasi, India. He was engaged as a Visiting Faculty with the Indian Institute
of Information Technology Kota, Kota, India, for the second semester, from 2018
to 2019. He served as an Adjunct Faculty (part-time) with IIT Delhi from 2019
to 2022 and 2022-2025. His research interests include visible light
communications, free-space optical communications, power line communications,
and physical layer security. Dr. Mathur received the Best Student Paper Award for his co-authored paper in
2017 Conference on Decision and Game Theory for Security (GameSec 2017),
Vienna, Austria. He was awarded the Early Career Research Award by the Science
and Engineering Research Board, DST, Government of India, in 2019. He was
awarded the Teaching Excellence Award at IIT Jodhpur in 2019. He was recognized
as an Exemplary Reviewer 2021 for IEEE Transactions on Communications and IEEE
Open Journal of the Communications Society for 2024. He is serving as an
Associate Editor of IEEE Open Journal of the Communications Society.
