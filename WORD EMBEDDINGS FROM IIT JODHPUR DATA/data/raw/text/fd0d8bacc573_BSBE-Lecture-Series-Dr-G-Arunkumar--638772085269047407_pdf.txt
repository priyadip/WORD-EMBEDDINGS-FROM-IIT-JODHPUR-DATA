Title of the talk: Genomics in Healthcare Services
About the speaker: Dr. Arunkumar completed his Ph.D. in human population genetics at 
Madurai Kamaraj University, Tamilnadu. He served as a graduate research fellow and then 
as a co-investigator in the Genographic project funded by National Geographic. After 
various academic appointments at Chettinad Academy of Research & Education, Chennai, 
and SASTRA University, Tanjore, he currently serves as a geneticist and scientific affairs 
manager at Medgenome. Dr. Arunkumar has over 14 years of experience in human genetics 
and genomics.
