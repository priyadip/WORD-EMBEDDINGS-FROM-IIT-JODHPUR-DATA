Rajit Ranjan Assistant Professor school PhD. TU Delft, Netherlands email rajitranjan[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Topology optimization, Additive Manufacturing, Computational modelling call 0291 280 1532 language Web Profile About Dr. Rajit Ranjan is currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT Jodhpur, India (since March 2025). He holds a Ph.D. from Delft University of Technology, Netherlands, where he was a Marie-Curie Fellow under the European Union’s project titled 'Precision Metal Additive Manufacturing'. He holds an M.S. from the University of Cincinnati, USA, and a B.Tech. from NIT Allahabad, India. Prior to joining IIT Jodhpur, he briefly held a faculty position at BITS Pilani, where he taught Additive Manufacturing. He also worked as a Computational Engineer in Simulation & Design at Alloyed Ltd., Oxford, UK, contributing to cutting-edge industrial R&D in metal additive manufacturing. Dr. Ranjan is a self-motivated multidisciplinary researcher with over 7 years of research experience in multi-physics modeling and topology optimization. His research interests lie in part-scale simulation of additive manufacturing processes and integrating physical models with optimization frameworks. He has total 5 years of industry experience and has worked across five countries, collaborating effectively with international teams.
