Sanhita Das Assistant Professor school PhD. Indian Institute of Science Bangalore email sanhitadas[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Computational Mechanics; Constitutive Modelling; Smart Materials; Amorphous Materials call 0291 280 1666 language Web Profile About: Dr. Sanhita Das is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur . Her research focuses on computational mechanics for understanding the constitutive behavior of amorphous solids, damage and self-healing in polymers, concrete, and geomaterials, as well as the development of smart materials with stimulus sensitivity . She is also interested in smart deployable structures for energy harvesting and post-disaster management , with a future research focus on Physics-Informed Neural Networks . Dr. Das holds a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering from the Indian Institute of Science (2022), an M.Tech. in Structural Engineering from IIT Kharagpur (2013), and a B.Tech. in Civil Engineering from Jadavpur University (2010) . Before joining IIT Jodhpur, she gained diverse industry and research experience. She worked as a Civil Engineer at M.N. Dastur & Company (2010-2011) and as an Engineer in the Structure and Thermal Analysis Department at Airbus Group India Pvt. Ltd. (2013-2016) . She also held academic research positions as a Research Associate at IISc Bangalore (2021-2022) and a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Weizmann Institute of Science, Israel (2022-2023) . At IIT Jodhpur, Dr. Das is involved in teaching Engineering Mechanics, Cyber-Physical Systems in Civil Engineering, Mechanics of Solids, Smart Infrastructure Engineering, and the Finite Element Method . Passionate about material behavior and computational mechanics, she invites students and researchers to explore the fascinating world of materials and structural modeling through her research.
