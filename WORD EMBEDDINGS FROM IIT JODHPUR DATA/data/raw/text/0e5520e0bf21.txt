Department of Civil & Infrastructure Engineering, IIT Jodhpur Proudly announces the Launch of M.Tech. program in Bridge Engineering
The Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering at IIT Jodhpur proudly announces the launch of an M.Tech. program in Bridge Engineering , a first-of-its-kind initiative in India. This specialized program is designed to equip students with the expertise needed to tackle the growing challenges in bridge design, construction, and maintenance. With India's infrastructure expanding rapidly, the demand for highly skilled bridge engineers has never been greater. This program offers core courses in structural analysis, bridge design, and advanced mechanics , alongside industry-relevant electives in prestressed concrete, cable-supported bridges, and structural health monitoring . This initiative aligns with India’s Viksit Bharat 2047 vision , ensuring that the next generation of engineers is prepared to drive innovation in infrastructure development.
