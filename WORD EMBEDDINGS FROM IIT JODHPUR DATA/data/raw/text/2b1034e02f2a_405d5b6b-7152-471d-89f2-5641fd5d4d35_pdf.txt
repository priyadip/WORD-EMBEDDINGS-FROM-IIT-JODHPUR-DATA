The Chairman and the Members of the BoG highly appreciated Dr. Hari Om Yadav, Registrar 
and his colleagues Shri Neeraj Kumar, Junior Superintendent and Shri Lalit Mohan, Junior 
Assistant for their untiring efforts, sincerity, dedication, and efficiency for timely preparation of 
all the documents related to Board of Governors meeting and smooth conduct of the 
proceedings of BoG in a highly professional manner. 
 
The Chairman and the Members of the BoG also expressed their deep sense of appreciation for 
Shri Darsh Kumar Khatwani, Superintendent and Shri Shyam Sunder Singh, Junior Assistant of 
the Office of Faculty Recruitment for their extremely hard work, sincerity, dedication and deep 
sense of responsibility to help in conducting mission mode recruitment of faculty members. 
The team has gone beyond their comfort zone to help institute in the recruitment of Faculty 
Members for all the major Departments of the Institute in a timely manner.

The Chairman and the Members of the BoG conveyed their special appreciation for the team 
consisting of Dr. Hari Om Yadav, Registrar, Shri P.G. Basak (Adviser Administration), 
Shri Prashant Bhardwaj, Assistant Registrar, Shri Laxman Singh, Assistant Registrar and 
Shri Robin Kaintura, Junior Assistant. The team has been working relentlessly with zeal and 
enthusiasm for the past several months to carry out a challenging task of recruitment of 
technical and administrative staff members in a mission mode. The conduct of these 
recruitment involved huge tasks of conducting both written examinations and skill tests, most 
of which have been conducted on weekends.
Dr. Hari Om Yadav, Registrar & Secretary, BoG profusely thanked Hon’ble Chairman, BoG and 
respected Members for their wonderful gesture in appreciating his colleagues of different 
offices for their dedicated efforts and dogged determination in completing many of the official 
assignments well within the time with full sincerity. He expressed his gratitude and added that 
this was all possible under the dynamic and able leadership of the Respected Prof. Santanu 
Chaudhury, Director, IIT Jodhpur. He also thanked Prof. S.R. Vadera, Deputy Director for his 
continuous support.
The Meeting concluded with the thanks to the Chair and the Members of the BoG.
