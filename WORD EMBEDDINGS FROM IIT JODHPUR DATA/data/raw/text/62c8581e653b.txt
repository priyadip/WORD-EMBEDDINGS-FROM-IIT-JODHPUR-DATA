He joined the institute in July 2025. Educational Background: Dr. Gupta completed his Ph.D. at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, specializing in Enhancing High-Speed Road Safety: Insights from Advanced LiDAR-Based Driver Behavior Analysis. Research Interest Dr. Gupta’s research interests lie in the field of transportation engineering, with a particular focus on road safety, driving behavior analysis, crash-risk prediction, and the application of emerging technologies such as LiDAR and camera-based sensing systems. His work contributes significantly to data-driven approaches in support of Vision Zero initiatives, aimed at eliminating traffic fatalities and ensuring safer, more sustainable mobility systems. Dr. Gupta is actively engaged in interdisciplinary research and continues to collaborate with academic and industry partners to develop innovative solutions for urban transportation challenges. Academic Contribution Over the course of his academic career, Dr. Gupta has authored over 22 peer-reviewed publications and conference papers, with his research being cited more than 1000 times. He currently holds an h-index of 13, reflecting the impact and quality of his scholarly contributions.
