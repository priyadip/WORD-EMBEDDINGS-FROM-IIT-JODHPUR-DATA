Departmental Seminar by Mr. Somanka Maiti on August 16, 2021 ,at 04:00 PM
Departmental Seminar by Mr. Somanka Maiti on August 16, 2021 ,at 04:00 PM
Speaker : Mr.  Somanka Maiti Title of Talk:  Fundamentals of Digital Image Processing Abstract: In this presentation, I will be discussing some fundamentals of Digital Image Processing (DIP) and its applications. Primarily, I will be focusing on the Image restoration process. I will talk about some noise models and their noise removal techniques. In today’s scenario, Image processing is one of the fast-growing fields. The basic idea is to convert a raw image into digital form and perform some operations on it to obtain any helpful information from it or get an enhanced image. Date and Time: 4:00 PM on Aug 16 (Monday), 2021
