Online SageMath Workshop
This is to inform you that the Department of Mathematics, IIT Jodhpur is organizing a four-day Online SageMath Workshop during February 15-18, 2021. If you are interested in participating in this workshop, please fill the following form for registration: https://forms.gle/zafwgWN8JjJmy92E9 For more information, see attached poster or visit: https://sites.google.com/view/dilpreetkaur/others/sagemath This workshop is open to the whole Mathematical community. Please feel free to forward this email to your contacts who may be interested in this workshop. If you have any queries regarding the workshop, please write an email to " dilpreetkaur@iitj.ac.in "
