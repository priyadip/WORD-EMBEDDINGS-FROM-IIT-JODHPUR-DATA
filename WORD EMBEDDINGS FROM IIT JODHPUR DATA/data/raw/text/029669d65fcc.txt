Parag Arvind Deshpande Professor email head_che[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in call 0291 280 1701 language Web Profile About : Parag A. Deshpande
is a professor of chemical engineering at the Indian Institute of
Technology Jodhpur. He received his doctoral degree from the Indian
Institute of Science Bangalore in 2011, graduating as the recipient
of the best thesis of the year award in the department of chemical
engineering, for his thesis on development of novel catalytic
materials for clean energy and environmental applications. Following
his doctoral research, he joined Northwestern University for his
postdoctoral research where he applied DFT methods for understanding
the mechanisms of enzymatic reactions with an aim of developing
biomimetic catalysts. He joined IIT Kharagpur in 2012 as a faculty
member, and was associated with it till 2023. He is a recipient of
university gold medal for undergraduate studies, Bristol-Myers Squibb
fellowship for doctoral studies, 2015 and 2019 DAAD fellowship, and
Fulbright-Nehru professional and academic excellence fellowship
2020-21. He advises a research group at IIT Jodhpur which specialises
in computational analysis of chemical and biochemical systems,
employing quantum chemical calculations to understand the underlying
materials science, catalysis and biophysical-biochemical behaviour
with specific interest in carbon capture and NOx reduction catalysis
