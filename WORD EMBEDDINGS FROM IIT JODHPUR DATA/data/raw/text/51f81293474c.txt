Gagan Kewalramani Assistant Professor school Ph.D.: Universite de Lorraine email gagank[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Turbulent flows, Fluid Mechanics, Heat transfer. call 0291 280 1535 About Dr. Gagan Kewalramani joined the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur in April 2025. He completed his Ph.D. in March 2023 from the Laboratoire d'Énergétique et de Mécanique Théorique et Appliquée, affiliated with the University of Lorraine, France. Prior to his appointment at IIT Jodhpur, he worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratoire de Mécanique des Fluides de Lille, under the Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique, France. At IIT Jodhpur, Dr. Kewalramani's work focuses on experimental and theoretical investigations of fluid flows. His areas of research include non-stationary effects in turbulent flow modelling, two-phase turbulence and flows in turbomachinery.
