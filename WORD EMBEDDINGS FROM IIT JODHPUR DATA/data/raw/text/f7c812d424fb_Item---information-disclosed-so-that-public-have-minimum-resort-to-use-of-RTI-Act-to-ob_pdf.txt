Query
1 Compliance status of reservation under the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (RPwD), Act 2016 in
Masters and Ph.D. admissions
Reply PwD candidates will have 5% seats reserved as per Government of India norms for admission in M.Tech.
programs. These seats are to be reserved within the respective category seats (UR, EWS, OBC, SC and ST).
There is no fixed sanction strength for admission of Ph.D. Students.
Query
2 What initiatives taken for opportunities and participation of PwD students in admissions like fee rebate
and relaxation, scholarship, relaxation in academic qualification marks, relaxation in cut-off marks and
reserving higher number of seats?
Reply i) 50% exemption is provided to applicants of SC/ST/PWD applicants in application fee at the of
applying
ii) SC/ST/PWD students are exempted from paying the tuition fee.
iii) All regular Ph.D. students admitted are eligible for assistantship of Rs. 31000/- + HRA (if hostel
accommodation is not available) during the first 2 years and Rs. 35000/- + HRA (if hostel accommodation
is not available) during the next 3 years. All regular M.Tech. (eligible for fellowship) students are provided
assistantship of Rs. 12400/- HRA (if hostel accommodation is not available).
iv) For eligibility criteria of Ph.D. please refer the link
