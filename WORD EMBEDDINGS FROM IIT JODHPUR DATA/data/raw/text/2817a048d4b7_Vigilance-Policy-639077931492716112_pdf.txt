Vigilance Policy
Vigilance Policy (Anti- Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy) of Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur 
plays a key role in ensuring that adequate procedures are in place in order to prevent in any activity 
relating to Bribery, Corruption, Illegal gratification and improper benefits. The Policy is aligned with 
the Government’s drive against anti-bribery & corruption and promotes transparency in all possible 
way. Part-time Chief Vigilance Officer, IIT Jodhpur deals with the vigilance matters as per the 
guidelines of the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC), Government of India.
Objective of Vigilance Policy (Anti- Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy)
The basic objective of the Vigilance Policy (Anti- Bribery and Anti-Corruption Policy) at IIT Jodhpur is to 
promote Integrity and Transparency in the entire functioning of the Institute in all levels. It helps in 
eradicating of bribery and corruption issues. It also empowers the compliance towards the directions 
and requirements of Central Vigilance Commission (CVC), Government of India and Ministry of 
Education (MoE), Government of India.

Part-time Chief Vigilance Officer (CVO)
The Part-time Chief Vigilance Officer (CVO) of the Institute is appointed as per the guidelines of the 
Chief Vigilance Commission (CVC), Government of India and the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India. 
The Part-time CVO is entrusted to eradicate the existence of bribery and corruption in Institute. The 
CVO is required to function as per the guidelines provided by the CVC on the matters pertaining to 
vigilance.
