Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur
Syllabus for the post of JUNIOR ASSISTANT
(A) WRITTEN TEST 
 
General English
1. Comprehension Passage (Short Passage) 
2. Grammar – Tenses, Active & Passive Voice, Direct-Indirect speech, Punctuation, Correction of
Sentences etc.
3. Precis writing. 
4. Noting/Drafting 
5. Letter/E-mail Communication on a given context
General Knowledge
1. Current Affairs 
2. Indian History, Indian Polity, Indian Constitution, General Economics & Geography
Simple Arithmetic (10th Standard)
1. Simplification of Fractions 
2. Simple/Compound Interest 
3. Profit/Loss & Percentages 
4. Other Basic Arithmetic Related Matters
(B) COMPUTER SKILL TEST
1. Elementary MS Word 
2. Elementary MS Excel 
3. PowerPoint Presentation 
4. E-Mail 
5. Typing Test English/Hindi
A. MS Word 
-Word document writing (to test the formatting/editing of various components of MS word 
to be applied in paragraph to be given like Header, Footer, Bold, Italics, Line spacing etc.) 
 
B. MS Excel 
-Formulae, Functions, Arithmetic elementary on Excel.
