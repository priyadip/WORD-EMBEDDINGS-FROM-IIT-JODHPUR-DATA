Facilities 
 
Present Campus 
 
At present IIT Rajasthan is functioning at the Campus of IIT Kanpur. All major Lecture halls, Labs, Computer 
Centers and Central Library and other facilities established by IIT Kanpur for their students were made 
available for students of IIT Rajasthan. 
 
Offices 
 
For the purpose of establishing temporary offices, IIT Kanpur has earmarked certain facilities like big office 
space for the use of IIT Rajasthan on the top floor of the newly constructed Helicopter research building, for 
setting up the Director’s office, Administrative, Academic, Students and Faculty offices along with Study 
Space, Computer Centre, and Library etc.
Lecturer Halls 
 
The state of art Lecture Halls of IIT Kanpur will be shared on alternate shift basis. The classes for the IIT 
Rajasthan will be held in the afternoon session during which the availability of IITK class-rooms is ensured.

Laboratories 
 
The highly advanced and technically equipped state of art laboratories of IIT Kanpur will be shared on shift 
basis. The Labs experimentation for the IIT Rajasthan will be held in the morning session during which the 
availability is ensured.
Student Hostel 
 
For the stay of IIT Rajasthan Students on the Campus of IIT Kanpur existing 7 two storeyed Type V 
apartments are being furnished in the dormitory model for accommodating about fifteen students in each 
apartment in the middle of the faculty houses, providing ample ambience for comfortable stay of IIT 
Rajasthan Students on the green campus of IIT Kanpur. Dining facility is also provided in one of the student 
housing apartments.

Library 
 
Top floor of Helicopter research building of IIT Kanpur allocated for IIT Rajasthan, a space has been 
earmarked for Library purpose. For the purpose of borrowing reference books and referring the journals, a 
highly equipped multi-storeyed P K Kelkar Library with digital archiving and on line reference was also made 
available on specific terms of usage for IIT Rajasthan students.
Computer Centre 
 
Initially the computer Centre of IIT Kanpur has been shared on available slots. Top floor of Helicopter 
research building of IIT Kanpur allocated for IIT Rajasthan, a space has been earmarked for development of

Computer Center Lab. All the hostels are equipped with Internet facility.
Health Services 
The students of IIT Rajasthan were allowed to avail the medical OPD services at the Primary Health Centre of 
IIT Kanpur under Institutional tie up.
Critical and emergency health care was arranged to be availed from identified super and multi specialty 
hospitals including tie-up with Regency Hospital in the city of Kanpur on cashless hospitalization tie up 
taking individual medical insurance from public sector medical insurance provider. For this purpose medical 
insurance was made compulsory for all the students of IIT Rajasthan.
Besides above security and housekeeping facilities were also provided to IIT Rajasthan students by the mentor 
Institute.

Sports 
 
The students of IIT Rajasthan were participated in different intra Institute sports events for girls and boys 
separately like Badminton, Basketball, Athletic, Tug of War & Twenty Nine for Girls and Volleyball, Weight 
Lifting, Athletic, Badminton, Football, Table Tennis, Chess, Bridge and Skating for boys. The girls won 
basketball and 4 out of 6 badminton matches against the Y8 batch of IITK and won the boys volleyball team 
over the Y8. In athlete the students came 3rd in the cross country held during the Kali Puja tournament. 
Among all fresher students won a bronze medal in Weightlifting-Udghosh. 
 
Total 10 students, 8 boys and two girls of IIT Rajasthan participated in inter IIT sports meet held at IIT 
Madras. 
 
Website and Temporary Logo for IIT Rajasthan 
 
Mentoring Institute organized the logo competition for IIT Rajasthan. The temporary logo for IIT Rajasthan 
with the theme of Jantar Mantar ‘the astronomical observatory of Jaipur to commemorate the scientific 
knowledge of medieval India has been adopted for the use of the institute till the final adoption of the Logo. 
 
The website of IIT Rajasthan with its logo was hosted in the IIT Kanpur’s website http://iitk.ac.in/iitj which 
gives information relating to academic, students affairs etc.

Independent Day Celebration 
 
The first Independent Day celebration was held on August 15, 2008 along with IIT Kanpur students at 8.00 
AM. The Director, faculty, staff and students were present. The function started with hoisting of National Flag 
by the Director with the parade of Security Wing. The Director emphasized the importance of the day and 
duties of the citizens especially of students. The students performed the cultural programme. Sweets and 
refreshments were distributed to all who are present. 
 
Republic Day Celebration 
 
The first Republic Day was celebrated on January 26, 2009 with the parade by the Security Wing followed by 
hoisting of National Flag by the Director. The Director in his address highlighted the significance of National 
integrity and commitment to the whole society. 
 
Gandhi Jayanti Celebration 
 
The first Gandhi Jayanti Day was celebrated on October 2, 2008 in order to commemorate the high moral 
values and great deeds of the father of the nation Mahatma Gandhi. The Director, in his message, pointed out 
the significance of his values in the present context. 
 
Cultural Activities : 
 
Drama for Freshers night was organized on 13-15th August, 2008. Mehakdeep Singh and Vipul Sharma 
rocked the freshers night with their bhangra the first official performance of IIT Rajasthan. They got 
accolades for their dance and were selected for the IIT Kanpur Team in Folk Dance for Antaragni’08. 
 
On 15th September a Dance club was started to train budding dancers, to select good dancers for Antaragni 
2008 and to increase students inclination towards dance. 
 
On 19th September 2008 a Literature club of the name “Literati Glitterati Club” was started with the Aims 
(i) to focus on the glamour aspect of literature i.e. write good and recite better (ii) to arrange group discussions 
on socially relevant issues (iii)to arrange debates and talks (in the long run) to imbibe social awareness in 
students. (iv) A part was also dedicated to help students who were not used to English and had problems in 
that field. A Fine Arts Club was started on 19th September, 2008 with the sole objective of this Club was to 
let people roam free with their imagination and help those talents to come out of their shells and to incline all 
students towards arts of any kind. 
 
On 21st September, 2008 a Dramatics club was started for participation in Antaragni’08. 
 
On 14th October, 2008 a Dance Team comprising of 6 boys and 4 girls was made on the basis of the dance 
club for participation in Antaragni’08. On 19th October 2008 a fine arts competition was held. Out of 52 
participants the artworks of Swathi, Arjun, Nirmal, Prateek were appreciated. 
 
In Antaragni’08 in October 23-26 the students participated in dance, dramatics-parody making, rangoli 
making, clay modeling, tattoo making, quizzes and treasure hunt and stood 4th in Parody making, 5th in 
treasure hand and 2nd in clay modeling. 
 
In antaragni students participated individually and Dibya Deepta Mishra qualified for the second round of the

Registration of the Society of IIT Rajasthan 
 
The IIT Rajasthan was registered as Society under Rajasthan Societies Registration Act 1958 by the Registrar, 
Institutions vide registration no. 1158/Jaipur 2008-2009 on March 24, 2009, in absence of passing of 
amendment of IIT Act 1961 Bill from the parliament. 
 
The Memorandum of Association was signed for the Society by the Chairman, Members and the Director 
with the objectives of and power of the Board in the MoA. 
 
The administration of the Society has been entrusted to a Board of Governors, the first members of which are 
as hereunder:-
1. The Chairman, ex-officio, who shall be the Chairman of the Committee. 
2. Two persons nominated by the Central Government one of whom will the Financial Advisor,
Department of Higher Education representing the Ministry of Finance.
3. Two persons nominated by the Board; and 
4. Director (to be presented by the Director of Mentoring IIT till such time the Director is named and
formally accepts the assignment) to act as Member Secretary.

Financial Brief 
 
The MHRD has released grants-in-aid under normal plan grant of Rs. 650 Lakhs during the financial year 
2008-2009 in favor of IIT Rajasthan under MHRD Sanctioned Letter No. F.No. 41-1/2008 (TS.1 dated 
01/08/2008 for Rs. 250 Lakh and F.No. 41-1/2008 (TS.1 dated 26/03/2009) for Rs. 400 Lakh. Besides above, 
the Institute has also received a sum of 64.08 lakh as fee and other components. Total plan expenditure during 
the year was Rs. 378.60 Lakh (Recurring Rs. 249.61 Lakh and Non-recurring Rs. 128.99).
