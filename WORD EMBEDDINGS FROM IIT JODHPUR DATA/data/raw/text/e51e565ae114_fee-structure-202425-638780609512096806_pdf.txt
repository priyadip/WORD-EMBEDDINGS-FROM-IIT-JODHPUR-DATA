2. For the B.Tech.-M.Tech./MBA dual degree students, the B.Tech. fees
(Tuition & semester fee) will be charged upto 8th semester and 
M.Tech./MBA fees (Tuition & semester fee) would be charged from 9th

3. The students of the MBA and MBA in FinTech & Cybersecurity program
opting for dual degree in second year require to pay day scholars fees at 
the Institute and other applicable fees to the Partner Institute.
4. Part-time/Part-time online/External students are required to pay the
semester fee as per day-scholar category. In case the student wants to 
avail the hostel facility, he/she shall pay the difference of hosteller and 
day scholar fee.
5. All the students (except Part-time/Part-time online/external) are
required to register as hosteller during the admission.
