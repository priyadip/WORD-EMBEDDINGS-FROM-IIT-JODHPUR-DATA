Institute Grievance Redressal Committee IIT Jodhpur constituted the Institute Grievance Redressal Committee to address the grievances of the employees of the Institute in an effective manner. The grievances received by the Institute are forwarded to the Committee to redress the issues based on seriousness. The Objectives of the Institute Grievance Redressal Committee are: To address grievance(s) received from the employees of the Institute, if any arising in discharging their duties Submit a report to the Director within two weeks of receiving the grievance after due consultation with the Employee concerned. Institute Grievance Redressal Committee
IIT Jodhpur constituted the Institute Grievance Redressal Committee to address the grievances of the employees of the Institute in an effective manner.
The grievances received by the Institute are forwarded to the Committee to redress the issues based on seriousness.
The Objectives of the Institute Grievance Redressal Committee are:
To address grievance(s) received from the employees of the Institute, if any arising in discharging their duties
