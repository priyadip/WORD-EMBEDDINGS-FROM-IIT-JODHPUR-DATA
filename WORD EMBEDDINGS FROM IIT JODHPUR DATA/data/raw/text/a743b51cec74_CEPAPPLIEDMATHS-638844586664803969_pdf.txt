Modern engineers and scientists across all
disciplines
are
frequently
faced
with
challenging mathematical models to handle,
with their solutions not always handy. As
technology continues to evolve, most of these
problems require applications of advanced
mathematical
concepts
more
than
ever
before. Unfortunately, analytical mathematics
taught as a part of the majority of engineering
and science undergraduate programs render
the handling of niche problems very difficult.
A clear and practical connection between
abstract mathematical concepts and real-life
engineering problems can be brought about
by
numerical
analysis
of
the
allied
mathematical models. The proposed short
term
course
is
intended
to
detail
the
numerical analysis of some important and
highly applied topics of applied mathematics,
and demonstrate the working solutions via
the use of MATLAB.
