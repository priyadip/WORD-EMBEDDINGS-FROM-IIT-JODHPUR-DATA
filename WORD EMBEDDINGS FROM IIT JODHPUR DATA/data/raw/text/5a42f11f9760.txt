Welcome To Office of Stores & Purchase
Welcome To Office of Stores & Purchase
The Office of Stores & Purchase facilitates the following: 1. Purchase of equipment & stores; 2. Maintenance of Asset Register; Stock Verification; 3. Maintenance of Control Store; Maintenance of lists of stockists and dealers for various items of regular purchase; 4. Liaison with State Purchase Cell and Customs; 5. Processing of write-off & disposal of unserviceable stores/equipment; 6. Advising in the framing of rules for Purchase and Maintenance of Stock Registers, Stock Verification, write-off, etc.; 7. Advising Departments, Centers for Technologies and other academic service facilities with respect to stores & purchase; and 8. Advertisements on request of Offices of the Institute.
