Academic Calendar: Summer Term 2024-25
Important Note:
1.​ Classes will be scheduled on 06 days working (Monday to Saturday), and a regular
course has engagement of 08 lectures for Fractal-1 and 09 lectures for Fractal 2 & 3 
(one lecture of 75 minutes) per one credit to complete the summer term before the 
Convocation 2025.
2.​ If the requisite number of lectures cannot be scheduled, the instructors may, in
consultation with the students in their respective courses, decide on a schedule of extra 
classes.
3.​ Saturdays and Sundays falling within the examination period will be used for scheduling
examinations. Under extraordinary circumstances, examinations of some of the courses 
may be scheduled on the days preceding the Minor/Major examinations.
