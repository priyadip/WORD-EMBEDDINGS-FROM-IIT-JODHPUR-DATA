The activities undertaken at the Institute are documented annually in the form of Annual Reports . These reports provide details of the academic, co-academic, and extracurricular activities carried out at the Institute during that financial year. In 2014, the performance of IIT Jodhpur was reviewed by an External Peer Review Committee. The Committee made recommendations regarding the academic, infrastructural, and administrative aspects of the Institute’s functioning.
