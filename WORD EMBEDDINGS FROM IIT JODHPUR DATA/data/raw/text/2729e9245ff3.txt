Welcome to Manekshaw Centre (MCOENSSR)
Welcome to Manekshaw Centre (MCOENSSR)
Vision Function as a nodal organization and a ‘Special Purpose Vehicle’ (SPV) for Defence Forces, Security Agencies, Academia, Research Institutes and other Scientific Bodies that enables convergence of strategic vision, operational excellence and technological expertise to strengthen national security. Offer specially curated courses exclusive for the personnel from the Defence Forces, Security Agencies and Law-Enforcement Organizations with a focus on next-gen hybrid warfare and emerging technologies and re-employment courses for the Officers, JCOs, ORs and Agniveers to take care of their smooth transition from forces to industries. Productize research in ‘Defence & Security’ and offer research-based solutions to Armed Forces and Security Agencies to cater new-age national security requirements.

Objectives Single window communication to bridge the gap between the Armed Forces, Security Agencies and Academic, Research Institutions under the aegis of the competent authority, Govt. of India Focus on next-gen education, innovation, productization, manufacturing and research-based consulting/operations (classified and non-classified) Create a new-age Security Industrial Complex/Ecosystem to promote Honorable Prime Minister’s Atmanirbhar Bharat initiative to “win India’s wars with Indian solutions” Collaborate with start-ups, industry partners, foreign institutes to achieve higher technology readiness level and cater the requirements of the Indian Armed Forces and Security Agencies Organize ‘Dialogue Series’, Workshops, Seminars, Ideathons, Conference with Indian Armed Forces and other key national security stakeholders on new-age national security challenges, next-gen asymmetric hybrid non-contact warfare, futuristic technologies
