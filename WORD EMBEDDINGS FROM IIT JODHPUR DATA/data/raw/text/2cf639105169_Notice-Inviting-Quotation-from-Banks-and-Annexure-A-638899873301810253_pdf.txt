The Institute is inviting quotation for investment of funds amount of Rs. 55 Crores. Interested Banks may 
submit their quotations in the prescribed proforma (enclosed in Annexure – A) on the Letter Head of the

3. The closed sealed envelope should be superscribed as, “Quotation for Rate of Interest for the
