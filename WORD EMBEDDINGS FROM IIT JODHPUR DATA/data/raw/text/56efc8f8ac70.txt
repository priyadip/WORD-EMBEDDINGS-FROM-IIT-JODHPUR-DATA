Monika Sinha Associate Professor school PhD: Jadavpur University email ms[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Astrophysics, Astroparticle physics call 0291 280 1604 language Web Profile Biography I am currently an associate
		professor in the Department of Physics, IIT Jodhpur. I have joined IIT
		Jodhpur as an assistant professor in 2011. Before joining IIT jodhpur,
		I worked as an Alexander von Humboldt fellow in Institute of
		Theoretical Physics, J. W. Goethe University, Frankfurt, Germany.
		Prior to that I worked as Centenary Fellow in department of Physics,
		Indian Institute of Science (IISc.), Bangalore, India for one year. I
		was a research associate in Theory Division of Saha Institute of
		Nuclear Physics, Kolkata India for three years. I obtained my Ph. D.
		degree from Jadavpur University in 2006. Research I work on interface of
		nuclear-particle physics and astrophysics. My interests in in compact
		stellar objects composed of highly dense matter. It focusses on the
		application of dense matter model to the astrophysical observables.
		The research field covers Pulsars, Magentars, X-ray binaries
