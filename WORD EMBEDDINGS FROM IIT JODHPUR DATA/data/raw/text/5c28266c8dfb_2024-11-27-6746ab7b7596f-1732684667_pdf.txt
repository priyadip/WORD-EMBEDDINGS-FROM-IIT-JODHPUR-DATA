Circular
Subject-Guidelines towards Official tour (including Conference, Seminar, etc.) in
combination with Vacation Leave and LTC-reg.
The following types of cases may arise when an official of lIT Jodhpur combines a journey
on LTC with one on transfer or official tour: -
(i) Leave Travel Concession in combination with transfer journey- An officer going to
hometown on regular leave proceeds therefrom on transfer to the new headquarters.
(Not applicable for IIT Jodhpur)
(ii) Leave travel concession in combination with tour journey-
(a) An
officer
proceeding
with
proper
prior
permission
on
LTC
to
anywhere/hometown from a tour station and returns to headquarters directly
from anywhere/hometown (LTC station); [Admissible limited as per LTC rules,
please refer to Illustration 1]
OR
(b) An officer proceeds on LTC to anywhere/hometown and therefrom to a tour
station with proper prior permission and returns to headquarters from the tour
station; [Admissible, please refer to Illustration 2]
OR
(c) An officer proceeds on LTC to anywhere/hometown and therefrom to a tour
station with proper prior permission and returns to LTC station from the tour
station. [Onward and return journey of LTC is admissible and tour journey is not
admissible, please refer to Illustration 3]
The decision of GOI under Rule 7 (9) of LTC in combination with tour TA - Mode of
regularization says that

In the case (ii) (b), Leave travel Concession as admissible under the rules may be allowed from

anywhere/hometown to the tour station and back to headquarters.
(iii)
Tour Journey in combination with Vacation leave-
(a) An Officer proceeding with proper prior permission on vacation leave to
anywhere/hometown from a tour station and returns to headquarters directly
from anywhere/hometown; [Onward journey to tour station is admissible, please
refer to Illustration 4]
OR
(b) An officer proceeds on vacation leave to anywhere/hometown and therefrom to a
tour station with proper prior permission and returns to headquarters from
the tour station; [Return journey from vacation station to headquarters is
admissible via tour station, please refer to Illustration 5]
OR
(c) An officer proceeds on Vacation to anywhere/hometown and therefrom to a tour
station with proper prior permission and returns to the vacation station from
the tour station. [Not admissible, please refer to Illustration 6]
GOl decision under S.R. 70 (FRSRpart-II) also says that
Ifanofficer ofa Vacation Department combines tour with vacation, i.e., proceeds on tourand then
avails of vacation without returning to his headquarters, he should be granted tour travelling
allowance under these rules for theoutwardjourney only.
It is seen that the officials are proceeding on an official tour in combination with LTC/
vacation leave and claim 100% of the expenses of both journeys, which is in contravention
of the above rules. Some illustrations are given in Annexure A for reference.
In the future, all cases will be dealt with as per the above guidelines. These guidelines
will be applicable from the date of issue.
