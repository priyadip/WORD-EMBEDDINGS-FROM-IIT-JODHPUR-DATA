Subject: Policy for the "Research Excellence Award" of IIT Jodhpur-reg.
The Board of Governors (BoG) in its 37th Meeting held on 29th June 2024 has approved the Policy for
instituting the "Research Excellence Award" of IIT Jodhpur to encourage the Faculty Members for
excellence in Research and Publications. The guidelines are given below:
1.
This Award shall be decided by a Selection Committee constituted by the Director.
2.
The Award recognizes and honors Faculty Members who, through their work at UT Jodhpur,
have achieved excellence in Research, as demonstrated by a high-impact publication, showing
technology development published in the Calendar Year under consideration.
3.
Nominations/ Self-Nominations shall be invited for this Award from the current Faculty
Members of IIT Jodhpur. The nomination should :include a brief description of the work, its
impact, and justification, along with a copy of the publication for which the nomination is
made.
4.
The publication must appear in the Journal in the Calendar Year under consideration for the
Award. An Individual is welcome to nominate one or more colleagues for this Award or Self­
Nominate herself 
/himself.
5.
The period under consideration will be the Calendar Year. The last date of nomination would
be 31•t March of next year. The Award will be given on the Institute's Foundation Day.
6.
The Award shall consist of a Silver Plaque and a Citation. The expenditure for this Award
shall be made from the endowment. In the absence of endowment, the R&D Overhead fund
may be utilized.
7.

Subject:
Policy for the "Teaching Excellence Award" of lIT Jodhpur for the Faculty Members-reg.
The Board of Governors (BoG) in its 37th Meeting held on 29th June 2024 has approved the Policy for the
"Teaching Excellence Award" of lIT Jodhpur to recognize Faculty Member(s) who consistently exhibit
exceptional teaching practices, significantly contribute to student learning outcomes, and inspire students
for excellence.
The guidelines are given below:
1.
Students and Alumni will be invited to nominate a Faculty Member for this Award who has
positively impacted their life. The nomination could be on the following criteria:
~
Engagement and Interaction: Showing proactive engagement with the students within and
beyond the classical boundaries of a classroom, creating an interactive and participatory
learning environment;
~
Support and Mentorship: Offering assistance, guidance, and mentorship to students, helping
them navigate challenges and reach their academic goals effectively;
~
Innovative Teaching Methods: Utilizing innovative pedagogical approaches to teaching that
enhance student learning, foster critical thinking, and accommodate diverse learning styles;
and
~
Passion and Dedication: Demonstrates sincere enthusiasm for teaching and the subject
matter, inspiring students through their dedication and commitment by going beyond the call
of duty.
2.
The last date of nomination would be 31st March of next year. The Award will be given on
Teacher's Day, i.e. 5th September every year.
3.
The Award shall consist of a Silver Plaque and a Citation. The expenditure for this Award shall
be made from the endowment. In the absence of an endowment, an Institute Fund may be utilized.
4.
A duly constituted Committee by the Director will select Faculty Members for the proposed
Award based on the nominations received from the students.
5.
The number of such Awards in a year may be decided by the Institute as per reqUire~
