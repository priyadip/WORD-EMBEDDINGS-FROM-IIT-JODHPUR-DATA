A. INTRODUCTION, BACKGROUND & OVERVIEW
1. Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur was established in 2008, to foster technology
education and research in India. The Institute is committed to technological thought and 
action to benefit the economic development of India. Scholarship in teaching and learning; 
scholarship in research and creative accomplishments; and relevance to Industry are three 
driving forces for us at IIT Jodhpur.
2. The institute is looking for a contractor/ Firm/ vendor for fabrication, supply and erection of
structural steel truss for shed of assembly area of size 18.8 m x 17.7 m and related civil works 
including foundation and flooring at Kendriya Vidyalaya, IIT Jodhpur.
3. The EOI will be received online through portal http://eprocure.gov.in/eprocure/app. In the
Technical Bids, the bidders are required to upload all the documents in .pdf format.
4. Those contractors not registered on CPP Portal are required to get registered beforehand. If
needed they can be imparted training on online bidding process as per details available on 
the website.
5. Contractors are required to submit bid documents as per eligibility criteria on CPP Portal on
or before 25.12.2025.
6. The bidders may be called for presentation before the Institute. 
7. The design submitted by the bidder shall be verified by the Institute, and only those bidders
whose designs are as per the codal provisions shall be considered technically qualified.
B. Eligibility Criteria:
1. The bidder should have successfully completed during the last 07 years in any
a. At least one similar work of size 266 sq. m. each 
 Or 
b. At least two similar works of size 200 sq. m. each
Or 
 c. At least three similar works of size 133 sq. m. each
2. Annual Turnover for the last 03 years (2022-23,2023-24,2024-25) should not be less than Rs.
10 Lacs. A CA certified copy of the same is to be submitted along with the EOI.
3. The bidder should submit the proposed design of the shed (Civil and structural) conforming
to relevant IS codes.
4. Similar work means – Fabrication, supply and erection of the steel structure/ truss work.
