PhD in electrical engineering from IIT Kanpur in 2019. Before joining IIT
Jodhpur as an assistant professor, he worked as a postdoctoral fellow at City
University of Hong Kong, HKSAR, China, and Stevens Institute of Technology, New
Jersey, United States. He was also associated with the Advanced Electronics
Group at the National Institute of Standards and Technology, Maryland, as an
international research associate from Nov. 2023 to Nov. 2024. Dr. Sharma
received the URSI-InRaSS Young Indian Radio Scientist Award in 2019 at URSI
Asia-Pacific Radio Science Conference held in New Delhi. He has
authored/co-authored over 50 journal and conference papers. He is currently
serving as an associate editor for Electronics Letters and an active reviewer
for numerous journals, including IEEE, Optica, and IOP. Dr. Sharma is a senior
member of URSI and a member of IEEE, Optica, and APS.
