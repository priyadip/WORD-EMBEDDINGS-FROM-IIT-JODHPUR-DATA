Dr. Akshay Moudgil delivered an invited research talk on Bioelectronics at the "International Workshop on Semiconductor Devices and Circuits" at IIT Delhi.
Dr. Akshay Moudgil was invited to deliver a research talk on Next-gen Bioelectronic Devices at the "International Workshop on Semiconductor Devices and Circuits" held at IIT Delhi on 29th Mar 2024. A large audience of researchers from IIT Delhi, CSIR-CSIO, Texas Instruments, ST Microelectronics, and SSPL attended this presentation.
