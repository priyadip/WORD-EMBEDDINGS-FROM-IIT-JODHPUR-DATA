Navigating PhD and Beyond, IIT Jodhpur
Navigating PhD and Beyond, IIT Jodhpur
Empowering the Next Generation of Researchers! On January 27, 2025, IIT Jodhpur, in collaboration with the American Chemical
Society (ACS), hosted Navigating PhD and Beyond—a dynamic event designed to
equip young researchers with the essential skills, perspectives, and strategies to
excel in academia and industry. The event featured insightful sessions by distinguished experts, including Prof.
Avinash Kumar Agarwal (Director, IIT Jodhpur), Prof. Ashutosh Sharma, Scholar in
Residence and President, INSA, Dr. Deeksha Gupta (ACS), Dr. Anand Madhukar (TERI SAS), and Dr. Ajay Jha (ACS). Key takeaways: Importance of sustainability in research Mastering the art of scientific communication Navigating career pathways & making the most of a PhD A day filled with knowledge-sharing, networking, and inspiration, this event
reaffirmed the commitment to fostering a robust research ecosystem.
