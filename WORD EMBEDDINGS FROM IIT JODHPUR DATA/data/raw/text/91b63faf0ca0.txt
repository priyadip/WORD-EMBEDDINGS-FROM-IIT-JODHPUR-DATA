India Institute Fellowship Scheme for the academic year 2023-2024
Congratulations to Ms. Amrita Sharma on receiving the prestigious India Institute Fellowship Scheme for the academic year 2023–2024. The scheme supports her 6 months visit to the University of Birmingham under Professor Yongliang Li. Ms. Amrita Sharma is currently working towards earning her doctoral degree in the rapidly developing subject of latent thermal energy storage systems, which have garnered recognition on a global scale. Our sincerest greetings are with her on reaching this significant goal.
