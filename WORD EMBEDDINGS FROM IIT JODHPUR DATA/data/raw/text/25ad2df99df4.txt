processes, and carbon sequestration strategies. By integrating advanced

challenges in water, environment, and climate sustainability. Research Positions Open Research positions are available in the fields of biological wastewater
treatment, advanced oxidation processes for water and wastewater treatment,
nature-based solutions, and micropollutant/transformation product analysis.
