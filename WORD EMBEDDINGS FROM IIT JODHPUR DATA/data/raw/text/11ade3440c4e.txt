23 ISME International Conference on Recent Advances in Mechanical Engineering
23 ISME International Conference on Recent Advances in Mechanical Engineering
Date : 17th -19th December 2025 Details of event: 23 ISME International Conference – 2025 will be held in the picturesque pink city of India, Jaipur from December 17 - 19, 2025. The intention is to setup an environment for knowledge sharing between international and national academia and industry experts. To blend some outdoor fun with the inspiring keynote addresses and expert lectures, a half day city tour has been planned on Saturday, December 19. The conference organizers look forward to all those working in various domains of Mechanical Engineering to showcase their excellence as well as challenges faced in diverse technological advancements. The conference will cover a wide array of topics listed on page 4 of this brochure. The special theme of this conference is integration of AI/ML into mechanical engineering. This conference which is 23 in series of ISME conferences is being jointly organized by IIT Jodhpur, LNMIIT Jaipur and MNIT Jaipur under the aegis of Indian Society of Mechanical Engineers. Conference Brochure can be found here (Download file) Website : https://lnmiit.ac.in/isme-2025/
