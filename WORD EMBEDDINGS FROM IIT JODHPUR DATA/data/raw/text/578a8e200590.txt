Deepak Srivastav Assistant Professor school PhD: Indian Institute of Management, Kozhikode email deepaksrivastav[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Humanitarian Supply Chain, Disaster Operations Management, Sports Analytics, Business Applications of Analytics call 0291 280 1809 About Dr. Deepak Srivastav is an Assistant Professor at the School of Management & Entrepreneurship, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. He holds a PhD in Quantitative Methods and Operations Management from the Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode and a B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from the Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology Bhopal. Prior to joining IIT Jodhpur, Dr. Srivastav served as an Assistant Professor at FLAME University, Pune. He has also held visiting faculty positions at IIM Kashipur, IIM Amritsar, IIM Rohtak, and IIM Sirmaur. His teaching spans operations management, supply chain management, and operations research, among others. Dr. Srivastav brings over five years of industry experience from Applied Materials India Private Ltd. and Lam Research India Private Ltd.. His research interests include humanitarian supply chain management, business applications of analytics, and sports analytics. His work has been published in reputed journals such as Annals of Operations Research and IIMB Management Review, and has been presented at leading international and national conferences, including POMS, EURO, and DSI.
