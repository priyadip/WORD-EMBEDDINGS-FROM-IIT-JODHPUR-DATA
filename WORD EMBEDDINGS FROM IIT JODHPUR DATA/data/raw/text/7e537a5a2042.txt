Our PhD student Miss Surbhi Ramawat has received the best poster award with a cash prize of 250/- Euro in one of the globally renowned conference on thermoelectric materials.
Miss Surbhi Ramawat, a PhD scholar working with Prof Ambesh Dixit and Dr Vijay Singh has received the best poster award with a cash prize of 250/- Euro in 40th International and 20th European Thermoelectric Conference, ICT/ECT 2024, supported by the International Thermoelectric Society (ITS) and the European Thermoelectric Society (ETS).
