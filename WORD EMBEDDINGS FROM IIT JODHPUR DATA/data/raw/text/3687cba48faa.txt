Appala Naidu Gandi awarded Early Career Research Award (ECRA)
Appala Naidu Gandi awarded Early Career Research Award (ECRA)
Appala Naidu Gandi, Assistant Professor, Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering, has been awarded the Early Career Research Award (ECRA) funded by Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB), Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India, for the project Thermoelectric Performance Study Using First-Principles Calculations Based Methods. Duration of the project is 3 years.
