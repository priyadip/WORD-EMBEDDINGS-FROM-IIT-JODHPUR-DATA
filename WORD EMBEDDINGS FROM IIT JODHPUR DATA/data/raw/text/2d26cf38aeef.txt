Before joining IIT Jodhpur, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the  Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the University of Quebec in Outaouais, Canada under the supervision of Prof. Andrzej Pelc. Distributed algorithms for Swarm Robots and Autonomous Multi-Agent systems.

Internet of Robotic Things (IoRT) and Autonomous Mobile Robotic Sensors.

Distributed Network algorithms. Security in Distributed Computing
