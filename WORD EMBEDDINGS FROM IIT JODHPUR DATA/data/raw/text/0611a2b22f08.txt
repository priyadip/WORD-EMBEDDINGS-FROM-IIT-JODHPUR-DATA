Dr. Akshay Moudgil Awarded Teaching Excellence Award
Dr. Akshay Moudgil Awarded Teaching Excellence Award
Teaching Excellence Award Citation Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur proudly bestows this award upon Dr. Akshay, Assistant Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering, for his exceptional contributions to teaching and the overall development of students and alumni. Dr. Akshay’s passion for teaching shines from the very first class, where he makes complex topics accessible through interactive discussions and hands-on learning experiences. Beyond academics, he is a constant source of motivation, offering patient and thoughtful guidance during research challenges and career uncertainties. Always available beyond regular hours, he supports students with dedication and care. His commitment to teaching and research, enthusiasm, problem-solving approach, and continuous pursuit of knowledge have inspired students to embrace perseverance, curiosity, and personal growth. IIT Jodhpur extends its heartfelt congratulations to Dr. Akshay and wishes him continued success in his professional journey.
