Congratulations to Dr. Vandana Sharma for the acceptance of her research article in the journal Communication on Pure and Applied Analysis
Title: Global existence and uniform estimates of solutions to reaction diffusion systems with mass transport type boundary conditions Author: Vandana Sharma Journal: Communication on Pure and Applied Analysis Year: 2021 DOI: Global existence and uniform estimates of solutions to reaction diffusion systems with mass transport type boundary conditions Publisher: American Institute of Mathematics Abstract: We consider reaction diffusion systems where components diffuse inside the domain and react on the surface through mass transport type boundary conditions. Under reasonable hypotheses, we establish the existence of component wise non-negative global solutions which are uniformly bounded in the sup norm.
Title: Global existence and uniform estimates of solutions to reaction diffusion systems with mass transport type boundary conditions
Author: Vandana Sharma
Journal: Communication on Pure and Applied Analysis
DOI: Global existence and uniform estimates of solutions to reaction diffusion systems with mass transport type boundary conditions
Publisher: American Institute of Mathematics
Abstract: We consider reaction diffusion systems where components diffuse inside the domain and react on the surface through mass transport type boundary conditions. Under reasonable hypotheses, we establish the existence of component wise non-negative global solutions which are uniformly bounded in the sup norm.
