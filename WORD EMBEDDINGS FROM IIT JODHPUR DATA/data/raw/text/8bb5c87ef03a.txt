Applications are invited for Ph.D program in Mechanical Engineering Department ( June, 2025)
The Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Jodhpur invites applications for the PhD program for the semester commencing in July 2024. The Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur hosts PhD programs under three major domains namely; Thermofluids, Design, and Advanced Manufacturing with Exposure in emerging areas such as Smart manufacturing, Additive manufacturing, Ultra-precision finishing, Welding, Artificial Intelligence and machine learning, Smart Healthcare, Nano-sensors, Multi-phase flows, Fluid-structure interactions, High speed flows, Hydrogen energy, Gas turbines, Vibration and acoustics, Robotics and automation, Computational solid and fracture mechanics. The following link can be used to submit the online application form: Apply online Kindly note that the application deadline is 20 April 2025. In case of any query with respect to the online application, candidates may contact Department of Mechanical Engineering PG admission ( pgadmission@me.iitj.ac.in / office_me@iitj.ac.in ) Ph.D admission brochure (Download file)
