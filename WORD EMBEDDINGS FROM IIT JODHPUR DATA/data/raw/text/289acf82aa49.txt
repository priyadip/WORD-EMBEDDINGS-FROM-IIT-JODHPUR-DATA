Congratulations Dr. Bhupendra for SERB International Research Experience (SIRE) for the year 2023-2024
Dr. Bhupendra Singh, Assistant Professor at the Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering, IIT Jodhpur, has been selected for the SERB International Research Experience (SIRE) for the year 2023-2024. This will enable him to work at University of Nottingham, UK for a period of six months. Congratulations!
