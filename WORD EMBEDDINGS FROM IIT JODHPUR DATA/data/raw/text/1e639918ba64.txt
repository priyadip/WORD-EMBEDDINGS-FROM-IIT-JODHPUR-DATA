Jitendra Yadav has received the Galileo Galilei Poster Award at the International Symposium on Beyond Li-ion Batteries held in Padova, Italy (Sept 1-6, 2024) for his poster presentation titled "2D Graphitic Carbon Nitride as the Efficient Cathode Material for the Non-aqueous Rechargeable Iron-Ion Battery Under an Ambient Environment."The award was presented by Nobel Laureate in Chemistry (2019) M. Stanley Whittingham , which further adds significance to this recognition. Award Recieved by Jitendra
