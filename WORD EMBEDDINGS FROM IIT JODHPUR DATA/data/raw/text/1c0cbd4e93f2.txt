Departmental Seminar by Prof. Maneesh Thakur on December 11 2023 at 11:00 AM
Departmental Seminar by Prof. Maneesh Thakur on December 11 2023 at 11:00 AM
Speaker : Prof. Maneesh Thakur About the speaker : Prof. Meneesh Thakur is working at ISI Bangalore. His research interest is Algebraic group Theory. Title of the Talk : Rational equivalence on Algebraic Groups Date and Time : 11:00 AM on Dec 11, 2023 Abstract : Rational equivalence on an algebraic variety is like path connectedness for usual topological spaces. We get very useful information on the structure of algebraic groups if we study this equivalence. The talk will explain this notion and present some recent results in the subject, without getting into technicalities. This should be accessible to graduate students as well as PhD students.
