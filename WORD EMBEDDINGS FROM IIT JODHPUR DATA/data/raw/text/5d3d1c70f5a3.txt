Kumar is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at
the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur (IITJ). He earned his Ph.D. and
Master's degrees in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of
Technology Bombay (IITB) in 2020, and holds a Bachelor's degree in Electronics
& Communication Engineering from the National Institute of Technology
Silchar (NITS). ​ At IIT Jodhpur, Dr. Kumar teaches courses such as Embedded Systems, Digital
Systems Laboratory, Hardware-Software Co-design, Formal Verification, Digital
ASIC Design Lab, Processor Design, Network-On-Chip, and ML in VLSI CAD. He has
been recognized with the Distinguished Teacher Award from IIT Jodhpur in
September 2024 and the Qualcomm Faculty Award in April 2024
