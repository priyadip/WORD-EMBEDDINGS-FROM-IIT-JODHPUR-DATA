Skill Development Program on MATLAB/Simulink and Software-Defined Radio (SDR)-Based Communication Systems Design (21st - 24th December 2025)
Skill Development Program on MATLAB/Simulink and Software-Defined Radio (SDR)-Based Communication Systems Design (21st - 24th December 2025)
Department of EE, IIT Jodhpur will be organizing a Skill Development Program on MATLAB/Simulink and Software-Defined Radio (SDR)-Based Communication Systems Design from 21st - 24th December 2025. This intensive skill-development program aims to bridge the gap between theory and practice by providing hands-on exposure to MATLAB/Simulink modelling and real-world implementation of software-defined radio (SDR) based systems. For more details on the program schedule, how to apply for this course, and the organizing team, please visit the course website below: https://sites.google.com/iitj.ac.in/iitj-skill-development-program/home The deadline for registration is 18th December 2025 .
