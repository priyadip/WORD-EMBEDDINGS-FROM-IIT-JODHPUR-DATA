Skill Development Program on MATLAB/Simulink and Software-Defined Radio (SDR)-Based Communication Systems Design (14th-15th and 21st-22nd March 2026)
Skill Development Program on MATLAB/Simulink and Software-Defined Radio (SDR)-Based Communication Systems Design (14th-15th and 21st-22nd March 2026)
Department of EE, IIT Jodhpur will be organizing a Skill Development Program on MATLAB/Simulink and Software-Defined Radio (SDR)-Based Communication Systems Design on 14th-15th and 21st-22nd March 2026. This intensive skill-development program aims to bridge the gap between theory and practice by providing hands-on exposure to MATLAB/Simulink modelling and real-world implementation of software-defined radio (SDR) based systems. For more details on the program schedule, how to apply for this course, and the organizing team, please visit the course website below: https://sites.google.com/iitj. ac.in/iitj-skill-development- program/home The deadline for registration is 10th March 2026.
