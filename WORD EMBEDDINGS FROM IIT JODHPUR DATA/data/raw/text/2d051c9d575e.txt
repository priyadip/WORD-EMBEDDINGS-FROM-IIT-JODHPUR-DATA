Research Seminar by  Dr. Bharat Soni on 26 August 2025 at 12:00PM
Research Seminar by  Dr. Bharat Soni on 26 August 2025 at 12:00PM
Title of the talk: "Optimal Fluid Flow in Networks" Date & Time & Venue : 26 August 2025  at 12 PM, Seminar Hall, Dept. of Mathematics Abstract: In many biological and engineering applications, it is required to transport the fluid through the networks with minimal work done. The fluid transport depends on various geometrical characteristics of the network and the fluid flow parameters. When there are certain obstructions or faults in the network, the optimality criterion deviates. In this talk, I will first discuss the optimal fluid flow criterion for tree-shaped networks by using the electrical circuit analogy. Later, I will discuss its application and the various challenges to adopt the derived theory on the human respiratory system. Furthermore, I will extend my discussion to understand the importance of electrical circuit analogy to study the optimality criterion of fluid flow in planar networks. About the Speaker: Dr. Bharat Soni is currently working as Assistant Professor at Indian Institute of Information Technology Raichur, Karnataka. He obtained his PhD from IIT Roorkee in 2022 and then worked as postdoctoral researcher at University Cote dAzur, CNRS, France. His area of research majorly includes computational fluid dynamics &amp; heat transfer, numerical methods and control systems.
