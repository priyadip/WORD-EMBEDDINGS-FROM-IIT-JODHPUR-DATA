Welcome To The Center For Education Technology (CET)
Educational institutions and policymakers around the globe have begun to embrace immersive teaching-learning technologies that will enable most students to attain optimal educational achievement levels. The Education Technology vertical of IIT Jodhpur was set up under the Centre for Emerging Technologies for Sustainable Development (CETSD) in 2021 to work towards research and delivery, services and training, and social outreach in the area of education technology and educational innovation. In October 2022, the vertical has been merged into the newly formed Centre for Education Technology (CET). The scope of the this Center includes Educational Services: Pedagogical Innovation in blended and hybrid education; Professional Development Continuing Education with upgradation of knowledge and pedagogical skills of Engineering Faculty Technology Development for Teaching-Learning needs, Assessment and Evaluation, Laboratory / Hands-on Instructions Instructional Design The Center has started its PhD program from July 2023 and is coordinating the Integrated Teacher Education Program (ITEP) from July 2024.
