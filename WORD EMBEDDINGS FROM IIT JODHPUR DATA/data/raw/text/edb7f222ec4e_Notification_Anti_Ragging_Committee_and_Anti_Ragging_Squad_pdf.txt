-In exercise of powers vested upon him vide Statute 12 (2) (xiv) of the Institute; the Competent
Authority has constituted the fo1lowing Anti Ragging Committee and Anti Ragging Squad:
Roles and responsibilities of Anti-Ragging Committee:
•
The Committee shall raise student knowledge regarding anti-ragging laws, consequences, and the
applicable laws in place.
•
The Committee will ensure compliance with the provisions of the regulations and any ragging-related
· law currently in effect.
•
The Committee will ban, prevent, and eliminate the cause of ragging, which includes any conduct by
any student or students, whether through words spoken or written or through an act that has the effect
of taunting, treating, or handling a fresher or any other student with rudeness.
•
The Committee will monitor and supervise the Anti-Ragging Squad's work preventing ragging in the
Institute.
