Self-appraisal report for year (2019-20)
Ministry Name: 
 
Ministry of Human Resource Development
Department Name: 
Department of Higher Education
Public Authority Name: 
Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur (IIT-J)
The self-appraisal report submitted by Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur (IIT-J) has 
been audited. It is observed that IIT-J have reasonably complied with the provisions 
stipulated in the guidelines issued by the DoPT vide their OM dated 15th April, 2013 for 
suo-moto disclosure under section 4 of the RTI Act, 2005. Proper linkages have to be 
provided to the information available on the website. Details of CPIO and First Apple 
Authority since 2015 have been disclosed. Vision documents, details of various 
departments, School, research, details of academic programmes offered, etc. have been 
disclosed. 
 
2 
In the report of the Public Authority, the same URL has been shown against all the
details. The manner it has been presented does not help in quick access, For ease of 
access to the information seekers, it would be appropriate if IIT – J provides the relevant 
details in seriatim and in the same manner information has been sought for audit and 
refer to the related URL. 
 
3 
There is scope for further transparency and ease of access to information in terms
of disclosure of (1) decision making process flow chart; (2) channel of submission and 
name and designation of decision making authority; (3) List of Acts, rules, regulations, 
and other guidelines on the basis of which decisions are taken on academic and non-
academic matters and linkage to such documents; (4) furnishing of complete details of 
public procurement of goods and services including local purchase committee; (5) Details 
of grievance redress mechanism; (6) details of RTI replies given; and (7) date of last 
update of data on top right corner (dd/mm/yyyy) of the IIT - J portal.
