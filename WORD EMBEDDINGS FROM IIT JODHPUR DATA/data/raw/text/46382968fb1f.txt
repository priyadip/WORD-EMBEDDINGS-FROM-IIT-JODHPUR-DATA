Welcome To Office of Faculty Affairs/ Establishment (E-I)
The Office of Faculty Affairs/ Establishment (E-I) at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur is responsible for managing the non-faculty staff and ensuring the smooth administration of the institute. Our mission is to support the institute’s operational efficiency and foster a conducive work environment through comprehensive human resource management and effective administrative processes. We handle various functions including recruitment, appointments, service records management, promotions, and retirements of non-faculty staff. Our team is committed to attracting, developing, and retaining skilled personnel essential for the institute's operations. The Office of Faculty Affairs/ Establishment (E-I) ensures that all non-faculty staff members have access to necessary resources and support. We manage employee benefits, handle HR-related inquiries, and maintain accurate and up-to-date personnel records. Our office also conducts orientation and onboarding programs for new hires, ensuring their smooth integration into the institute. We emphasize continuous professional development by organizing training programs and workshops that enhance the skills and capabilities of our non-faculty staff. This commitment to development helps our employees grow in their roles and contribute effectively to IIT Jodhpur's mission. Through our transparent and efficient administrative practices, we aim to create a supportive and dynamic workplace that upholds the highest standards of governance and integrity.
