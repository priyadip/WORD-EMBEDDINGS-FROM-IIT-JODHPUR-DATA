Puneet Sharma Professor school Ph.D.: Indian Institute of Technology Delhi email puneet[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Topological Dynamics, Low Dimensional Chaos call 0291 280 1455 language Web Profile About Puneet Sharma is working as an Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. He served as a faculty member at Department of Mathematics, IISER Bhopal for about six months after which he joined IIT Jodhpur as an Assistant Professor at IIT Jodhpur in 2012. He also served as a postdoctoral Fellow at School of Physical Sciences, JNU, New Delhi for more than a year. He received his Masters and Ph.D. degrees in Mathematics from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi in 2005 and 2011 respectively. His research interests include Topological Dynamics, Symbolic Dynamics, Chaos Theory and their applications. Among his notable achievements, he was awarded first prize for best academic performance in B.Sc(H) Mathematics. He also secured All India Rank 21 in GATE (MA) 2004 Examination.
