Dr. Suraj Srivastava delivered an invited tutorial on "Sparse Signal Processing in 5G and 6G Wireless Communication" at IEEE ICASSP 2025 Satellite Workshop held in Hyderabad
The 2025 IEEE International Conference on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing (ICASSP 2025) will take place in Hyderabad, India, from April 06 to April 11, 2025. This will be the first time an ICASSP is held in India and also marks the 50th anniversary of ICASSP. To promote the ICASSP 2025 conference, a series of satellite events are planned from April 2024 to April 2025 by all the 12 IEEE Signal Processing Society Chapters across India. Dr. Suraj delivered a tutorial on “Sparse Signal Processing in 5G and 6G Wireless Communication” along with Prof. Aditya K. Jagannatham on 2nd April in the first ICASSP 2025 Satellite Workshop, HICC, Novotel Complex, Hyderabad, India. Link: https://2025.ieeeicassp.org/sparse-signal-processing-in-5g-and-6g-wireless-communication/
