Dr. Priyanka Singh, Associate Professor in the Department of Bioscience and Bioengineering at IIT Jodhpur, has been selected for membership in the Indian National Young Academy of Science (INYAS)
Dr. Priyanka Singh, Associate Professor in the Department of Bioscience and Bioengineering at IIT Jodhpur, has been selected for membership in the Indian National Young Academy of Science (INYAS). INYAS was founded by INSA in December 2014 with a vision to promote Science education and networking among Young Scientists. This year in the 9th General Body Meeting in Feb. 2024, 30 new members have been inducted in INYAS for a period of five years across various disciplines (Physical Sciences, Chemical Sciences, Biological Sciences, Mathematical Sciences, Medical and Veterinary Sciences, Engineering Sciences, Earth & Atmospheric Sciences, and Agricultural Sciences).
