Faculty Member Receives Excellence in Doctoral Research Award from IIT Roorkee
Faculty Member Receives Excellence in Doctoral Research Award from IIT Roorkee
We are delighted to share that Dr. Akshay Gupta , Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering, has been honored with the Excellence in Doctoral Research Award by the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee . The award was presented by Dr. Jitendra Singh , Hon’ble Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Science and Technology , in recognition of Dr. Gupta’s outstanding Ph.D. research contributions. This prestigious recognition not only highlights the quality and impact of his doctoral work but also reflects the growing research excellence and academic strength of our institution.
