Dr. Shrutidhara Sarma Selected for INAE Young Associateship 2024
Dr. Shrutidhara Sarma Selected for INAE Young Associateship 2024
Dr. Shrutidhara Sarma Selected for INAE Young Associateship 2024 Dr. Shrutidhara Sarma, Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur and PI of FERN Lab (https://www.shrutidhara.com/) has been honoured with the prestigious INAE Young Associateship 2024 (https://www.inae.in/young-criteria/) in recognition to her outstanding contributions in Mechanical Engineering. The recognition will be presented at the INAE Annual Convention 2024 and the recipients will be formally inducted as INAE Young Associates. The Indian National Academy of Engineering (INAE), established in 1987, consists of the country’s most accomplished engineers, scientists, and technologists, spanning all areas of engineering. INAE functions as an apex body and promotes the advancement of engineering, technology, and related sciences, with a focus on addressing key challenges of national significance.
