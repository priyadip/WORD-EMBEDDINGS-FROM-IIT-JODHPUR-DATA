School of Liberal Arts at IIT Jodhpur congratulates Ms. Saikia and Prof. Sharma on their publication
The School of Liberal Arts at IIT Jodhpur congratulates Ms. Saikia and Prof. Sharma on the publication of their paper titled “Unveiling the Gender Nuances of Wisdom in Negotiation: An Experimental Analysis through Negotiation Game.” This paper appears in the International Journal of Experimental Research and Review, Volume 46, pages 150–164, and is Scopus indexed. https://doi.org/10.52756/ijerr.2024.v46.012
