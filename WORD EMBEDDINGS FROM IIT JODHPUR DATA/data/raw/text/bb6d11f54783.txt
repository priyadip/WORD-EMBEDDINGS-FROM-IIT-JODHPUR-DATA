Achievement - Dr. Akshay Moudgil from Electrical Engineering, IIT Jodhpur
Dr. Akshay Moudgil from Electrical Engineering, IIT Jodhpur, with a team of more than 100 experts from 57 research institutions worldwide, published a comprehensive roadmap for next-generation printable sensor technologies. This research work has been covered and published by several International technical media houses such as PHYS.org, AZO Sensors, NanoWerk, etc., and ranked in the top 5% of all research outputs scored by Altmetric. Paper Details: V. Pecunia, A. Moudgil et al., “Roadmap on Printable Electronic Materials for Next-Generation Sensors,” Nano Futures, Mar. 2024, doi: 10.1088/2399-1984/ad36ff. Link: https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/2399-1984/ad36ff/meta
