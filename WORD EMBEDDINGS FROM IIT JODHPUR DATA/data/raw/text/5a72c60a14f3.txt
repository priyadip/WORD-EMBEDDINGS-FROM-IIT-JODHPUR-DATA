M.Tech-PhD Dual Degree The department currently offers an M.Tech-PhD dual degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Advanced Manufacturing and Thermofluids. A wide range of specialization related courses is currently offered. Specialization: Mechanical Design/ Thermofluids/ Advanced Manufacturing The department offers M. Tech-PhD dual course in Mechanical Engineering within three major domains of Thermofluids, Mechanical Design, and Smart Manufacturing. The students also have the opportunity to work with an interdisciplinary specialization in emerging areas such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Data and Computational Science, Smart Healthcare, Energy Materials, and Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS). Click here for the salient features of specializations. ( Download file) *The expected duration for the award of Ph.D. degree in this program is 5 years. M.Tech-PhD Dual Degree
The department currently offers an M.Tech-PhD dual degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Advanced Manufacturing and Thermofluids. A wide range of specialization related courses is currently offered.
Specialization: Mechanical Design/ Thermofluids/ Advanced Manufacturing
The department offers M. Tech-PhD dual course in Mechanical Engineering within three major domains of Thermofluids, Mechanical Design, and Smart Manufacturing. The students also have the opportunity to work with an interdisciplinary specialization in emerging areas such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Data and Computational Science, Smart Healthcare, Energy Materials, and Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS).

*The expected duration for the award of Ph.D. degree in this program is 5 years. Admitted students are paid the monthly assistantship the same as that of Ph.D. scholars from the first day. The current assistantship is Rs 31000 per month as per the MHRD rules.
