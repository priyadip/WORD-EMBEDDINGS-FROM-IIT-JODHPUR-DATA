Welcome to the Visitors Hostel
Welcome to the Visitors Hostel
The Visitors Hostel at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur is dedicated to providing comfortable and convenient lodging facilities for visiting faculty, researchers, guests, and dignitaries. Our mission is to offer a welcoming and hospitable environment that enhances the experience of visitors to the campus. We manage a range of well-appointed rooms and suites, equipped with modern amenities to ensure a pleasant stay. Our facilities include dining services, internet access, and recreational areas, designed to cater to the diverse needs of our guests. The Visitors Hostel is strategically located to provide easy access to the institute’s academic and research facilities. We aim to offer a serene and conducive atmosphere, allowing guests to relax and focus on their work or engagements at IIT Jodhpur. Our team is committed to delivering exceptional service, ensuring that all guests have a memorable and comfortable stay. We take pride in our attention to detail and personalized approach, addressing the specific requirements and preferences of each visitor. The Guest House also serves as a venue for small conferences, meetings, and social gatherings, providing a versatile space for various events. By offering high-quality accommodation and services, we contribute to the institute’s mission of fostering academic and professional collaboration.
