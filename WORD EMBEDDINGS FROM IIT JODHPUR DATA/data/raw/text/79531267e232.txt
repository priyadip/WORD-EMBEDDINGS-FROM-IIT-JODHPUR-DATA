The Manekshaw Centre of Excellence for National Security and Strategic Research (MCOENSSR) at IIT Jodhpur is a dedicated hub for advancing technological self-reliance in India’s defence sector. It builds a collaborative ecosystem that connects the Indian Army, academia, and industry to address critical security challenges through research and innovation in areas such as autonomous systems and electronic warfare. This National-Level Hackathon is designed as a bridge between concept and deployment. Participants are challenged to convert innovative ideas into practical, defence-ready solutions that can serve as strategic assets for national security. The event brings together multidisciplinary teams to work on real-world problems in a highly focused, mission-oriented environment.
