Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur
Syllabus for the post of JUNIOR TECHNICAL ASSISTANT (Animal House)
(WRITTEN TEST & SKILL TEST)
General anatomy (different body parts and organs) and Physiology- digestive system, urinary system,
respiratory system, circulatory system, skeletal system, nervous system, skin, sense organs, muscular system,
behavior, food, nutrition & housing requirement of laboratory rodents. Biomedical waste disposal methods
and guidelines, different methods use for sterilization of animal feed, water, bedding materials, equipment
and experimental area or animal room. Organ collection and storage methods for histopathology, tissue
processing, staining. Methods of Anesthesia and Euthanasia use in laboratory animals. Ethics and Indian
guidelines for animal experimentation, Animals husbandry practices, different animal restraining methods,
Environmental and physical requirements for experimental rodents. Animal Welfare Board of India - their
role, functions and current status, Role and function of CCSEA. Diseases of small laboratory rodents, Clinical
manifestation, diagnosis, prevention and control of zoonotic diseases. Personal hygiene, Biosafety
Consideration, Occupational Health Hazards and Disaster Management in Laboratory Animal Facilities.
