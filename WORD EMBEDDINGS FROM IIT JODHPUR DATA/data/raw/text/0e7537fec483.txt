Sandeep Kumar Yadav Professor school Ph.D.: Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur email sy[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Signal Processing, Machine Learning, Condition Monitoring, Image Processing call 0291 280 1354 language Web Profile About Dr. Sandeep Kumar Yadav is a Professor in the
Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Jodhpur. He holds a Ph.D. in
Electrical Engineering and an M.Tech. in Nuclear Engineering and Technology
from IIT Kanpur, and a B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering from H.B.T.I. Kanpur.
His research interests include signal processing, condition monitoring, blind
signal demodulation, image processing, artificial neural networks, and machine
learning. He has led several funded research projects in collaboration with agencies
such as DRDO, IBM, UNICEF, DST, and MHRD. Dr. Yadav has received numerous
accolades, including the Young Scientist Award (System Society of India, 2011),
the Graphical System Design Achievement Award (National Instruments, 2012), the
IBM Shared University Research Award (2014), and the Teaching Excellence Award
from IIT Jodhpur (2020). He regularly teaches undergraduate and postgraduate
courses such as Signals and Systems, Digital Signal Processing, Intelligent
Instrumentation, Machine Learning, Adaptive Signal Processing, and Circuit
Theory. He is actively involved in mentoring Ph.D. and M.Tech. students in
emerging areas of intelligent systems and signal analysis.
