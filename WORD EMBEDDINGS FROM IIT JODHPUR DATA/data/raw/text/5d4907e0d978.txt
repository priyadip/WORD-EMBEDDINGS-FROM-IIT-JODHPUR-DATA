Trinity College Dublin delegation visit CETE IIT Jodhpur (Feb 2023) + Prof. Timothy Savage and Dr. P. J. Wall from Trinity College Dublin visited IIT Jodhpur during 20-24th Feb 2023. At Trinity College Dublin (a QS Top 100 University), Prof. Timothy holds a joint appointment with the Schools of Education and School of Computer Science. He has a deep expertise in education pedagogy and education technology, owing to his experience of more than 2 decades in the discipline. He was also the first Dean of Trinity Online initiative and helped setup its first online and MOOC courses. During his visit, he interacted with the Head and the affiliate faculty members of Centre for Education & Technology for Education (CETE). He shared his experience and commended IIT Jodhpur for taking such an initiative integrating Education, Pedagogy, and Education Technology.
