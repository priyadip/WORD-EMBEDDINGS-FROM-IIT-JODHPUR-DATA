Spring 2025. His empirical research lies at the intersection of environmental
and development economics, with an emphasis on climate change, sustainable

inform effective policy solutions. Over the years, Sourya has held various research positions.
His work has been published in reputable ABDC-indexed journals, earning him

forums. Notably, he has collaborated with ADBI (Tokyo) on multiple occasions.

distinctions from the RBI Governor during his master’s studies, and an early
