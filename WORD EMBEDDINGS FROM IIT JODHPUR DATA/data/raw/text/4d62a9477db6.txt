Applications are invited for Ph.D program in Mechanical Engineering Department (July, 2024)
Applications are invited for Ph.D program in Mechanical Engineering Department (July, 2024) The Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Jodhpur invites applications for the PhD program for the semester commencing in July 2024. The Department of Mechanical Engineering, IIT Jodhpur hosts PhD programs under three major domains namely; Thermofluids, Design, and Advanced Manufacturing with Exposure in emerging areas such as Smart manufacturing, Additive manufacturing, Ultra-precision finishing, Weld cracking, Artificial Intelligence and machine learning, Smart Healthcare, Nano-sensors, Multi-phase flows, Fluid-structure interactions, High speed flows, Hydrogen energy, Gas turbines, Vibration and acoustics, Robotics and automation, Computational solid and fracture mechanics. The following link can be used to submit the online application form: https://oa.iitj.ac.in/PG_ADMISSION. (https://oa.iitj.ac.in/PG_ADMISSION). Kindly note that the application deadline is 20 April 2024. In case of any query with respect to the online application, candidates may contact Department of Mechanical Engineering PG admission (pgadmission@me.iitj.ac.in / office_me@iitj.ac.in ) Brochure for Ph.D. admission can be found here (Download file) Advertisement for Admission to Ph.D. Programs (Download file)
