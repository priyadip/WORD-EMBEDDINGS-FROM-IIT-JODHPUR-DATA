Departmental seminar by Prof. Rene M Koenigs
Departmental seminar by Prof. Rene M Koenigs
Title of Talk: "Spin States Matter - From Fundamentals Towards Applications and
Drug Discovery" Prof. Koenigs holds a full professor position in the Institute of Organic Chemistry at
RWTH Aachen University. His research is dedicated to the chemistry of reactive small
molecules and their application to organic synthesis. His primary research interests are
in metal-catalyzed carbene transfer reactions, photochemistry, photocatalysis, and
medicinal chemistry. He is a recipient of many prestigious awards and recognitions.
Please refer to the following website for further details on the speaker. https://www.koenigslab.rwth-aachen.de/cms/koenigslab/das-lehr-undforschungsgebiet/team/~kysv/rene-michael-koenigs/?allou=1&lidx=1 Looking forward to active participation from all of you! Friday, March 07, 2025 |05:00 pm – 06:00 pm| Seminar Hall | Department of Chemistry
