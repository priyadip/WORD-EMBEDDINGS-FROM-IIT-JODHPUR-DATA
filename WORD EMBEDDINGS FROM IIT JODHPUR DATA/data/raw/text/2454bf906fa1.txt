Establishment (E-I) facilitates
The Office of Establishment facilitates the following: (A) General Administration & Discipline Formulation of Policies for non-academic Staff Members; Human resource management including training of Staff Members; Performance appraisal of Staff Members; Instituting procedures for efficiency enhancement; Leave Management; Preparation of an Administrative Manual and ensuring its implementation; Welfare, health and safety of Staff Members; and Celebration of National Events. (B) Employee Service Matters Service matters of non-academic Staff Members relating to appointments, promotions, transfers, terminations, etc.; Maintenance of personal history records of non-academic Staff Members; Deal with matters relating to Staff Member entitlements (such as advances of various types); Maintain & update General Service & Conduct Rules; Advise & mentor non-academic Staff Members; Receipt & Issue of Mail; General Matters; House building & Conveyance Advances; and Group Insurance & Other Employee welfare schemes. (C) Faculty Member Matters Welfare; Training & development; Discipline; Performance Appraisal; Grant of medium- & short-term leaves; and Nominations for attending seminars & conferences, etc. (including travel grants). (D) Faculty Member Recruitment Management of Cadre; Creation of posts; Announcement posts; Management of Reservation Roster; and Facilitate due process for recruitment.
