Chandana N email chandana[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Waste Water Treatment, Solid Waste Management, Environmental Impact Assessment, Life Cycle Analysis (LCA), Faecal Sludge/ Septage Management call 0291 280 1720 About Chandana N is an Assistant Professor in the Centre for Emerging Technologies for Sustainable Development at the Indian Institute of Technology, Jodhpur. She holds a Ph.D. in environmental engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, where she was awarded the prestigious Pairmala Rao award for the best Ph.D. thesis in CTARA, IIT Bombay in 2022 for her doctoral research, titled "Tackling Onsite-Sanitation Challenges in India: Faecal Sludge Management". Chandana also possesses a master's degree in Environmental Engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Warangal, and a bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering. With a total of 10 years of experience in environmental issues and the Information Technology field, her main areas of work include the design and modeling of water and sanitation systems, technical verification of designs for wastewater treatment plants, third-party technical audits, air pollution analysis, and evaluation of various developmental projects implemented by the government. The Sustainable Waste Management Lab is dedicated to conducting cutting-edge research and providing innovative engineering solutions to mitigate waste and alleviate human suffering. Our team, led by Dr. Chandana N, focuses on creating sustainable systems for waste management and environmental protection, contributing to a cleaner and healthier world.
