Welcome To The RTI Cell
Welcome To The RTI Cell
The RTI (Right to Information) Cell at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur is dedicated to promoting transparency and accountability by facilitating access to information under the Right to Information Act, 2005. Our mission is to ensure that information about the institute’s operations, decisions, and policies is readily available to the public, fostering an environment of openness and trust. We manage the receipt, processing, and response to RTI applications, ensuring that all requests are handled promptly and in compliance with the legal requirements. Our team is committed to providing accurate and comprehensive information while protecting sensitive and confidential data as mandated by law. The RTI Cell works closely with various departments to gather the necessary information and to ensure that responses are thorough and timely. We also provide guidance and support to the institute’s staff on RTI-related matters, helping them understand their responsibilities and obligations under the Act. Our office is responsible for maintaining records of all RTI applications and responses, ensuring that the process is documented and transparent. We also facilitate periodic training sessions and workshops to raise awareness about the Right to Information Act and to promote best practices in information disclosure. By fostering a culture of transparency, the RTI Cell contributes to the overall mission of IIT Jodhpur, enhancing public trust and ensuring accountability in the institute’s operations.
