Section F: Approve travel within India
As per " Section F: Approve travel within India " (F12: From Project Funds) of Office Order No. IITJ/REG/2025-26/73 dated 30th July 2025, it is mandatory to obtain prior approval from the Dean of Research and Development (DORD) through the Head before undertaking any travel within India funded by the project.
