Scope of Work:
The scope of work for providing and fixing a conical-shaped tensile fabric shed at the Director’s 
Residence, IIT Jodhpur, covering an approximate area of 4180sq.ft. with a canopy pillar height of about 
12 ft, shall include the following:
1) 
Supply, fabrication, and installation of conical-shaped tensile fabric sheds with mild steel 
(MS) structural framework, including powder coating / approved paint finish.
2) 
 Providing and fixing 900 GSM PVDF-coated tensile fabric, coated on both sides, suitable 
for outdoor use.
3) 
 Fabrication of the structural system using MS pipes/tubes/rolled sections, including base 
plates, anchor bolts, and all necessary welding works.
4) 
Application of anti-corrosive paints and finishing coats / powder coating to all exposed 
steel members.
5) 
Complete installation at site, including all labour, materials, tools, equipment, lifting, and 
positioning of the shed structure.

1. Member Thickness / Structural Specification:
