Amit Bhardwaj Associate Professor school Ph.D.: Indian Institute of Technology Bombay email amitb[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Haptics call 0291 280 1372 language Web Profile Amit Bhardwaj Amit Bhardwaj received his PhD degree from the Department of Electrical
Engineering at IIT Bombay in June 2016. From June 2016 to January 2017, he was
a research associate at IIT Bombay, Mumbai. From February 2017- December
2017, he was an Alexander von Humboldt postdoctoral fellow at the Chair of Media
Technology, Technical University of Munich, Germany. After that, he joined the
Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), South Korea as a
postdoctoral researcher. Currently, he is working as an associate professor in the
Department of Electrical Engineering, at IIT Jodhpur.
He has research experience in the area of virtual reality and multi-sensory human
perception. He has published his research work in top journals and conferences in
the area of haptics and robotics. He has also published a book in the area of
perception
