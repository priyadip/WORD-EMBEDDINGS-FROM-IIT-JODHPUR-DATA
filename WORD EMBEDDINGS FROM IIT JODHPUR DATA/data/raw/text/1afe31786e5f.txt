Dr. Sudhir Kumar Hota Receives the NOST–Anthem Biosciences Best  Thesis Award 2025
IIT Jodhpur is proud to announce that Dr. Sudhir Kumar Hota , a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Dr. Sandip Murarka (Department of Chemistry, IIT Jodhpur) , has been awarded the highly prestigious NOST–Anthem Biosciences Best Thesis Award 2025 . Dr. Hota is among only twelve recipients nationwide to be selected for this distinguished recognition by the NOST Council . This award recognizes his outstanding contributions to doctoral research and reflects the high standards of academic excellence at IIT Jodhpur.
