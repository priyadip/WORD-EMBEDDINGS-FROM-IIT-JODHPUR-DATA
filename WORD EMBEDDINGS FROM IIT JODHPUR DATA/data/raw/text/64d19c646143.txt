Arun Kumar Singh Associate Professor school Ph.D.: Telecom Paris, France email singhak[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Wireless Communications, Communication Theory, Spread spectrum systems, and Satellite based navigation systems call 0291 280 1358 language Web Profile About Arun
Kumar Singh (M'14-SM'20) received the M. Tech. degree in Communication and
Radar engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, India, in
December 2002 and the Ph.D. degree in Electronics and Communications from the
Télécom Paris (also known as École Nationale Supérieure des
Télécommunications), France, in February 2012. He is an Associate Professor in
the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology
Jodhpur, which he joined in 2013. Earlier, from April 2003 to January 2009, he
worked as a scientist at Space Applications Centre, Indian Space Research
Organization, India, where he was part of the system engineering team for
communication and navigation satellite payloads. From May 2012 to April 2013 he
was a research engineer at the Mobile Communications Department, EURECOM,
France.
