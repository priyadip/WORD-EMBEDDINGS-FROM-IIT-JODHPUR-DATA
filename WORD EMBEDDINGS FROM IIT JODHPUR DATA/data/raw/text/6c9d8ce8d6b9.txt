Educational Background: Dr. Swami completed his Ph.D. at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, specializing in groundwater flow and contamination modeling, surface moisture dynamics, and geoenvironmental engineering. Professional Experience: Associate Professor at IIT Mandi (February 2015 - March 2025): Dr. Swami served as an Associate Professor at IIT Mandi for over a decade, contributing significantly to the field of water resources engineering. ​ Associate Professor at IIT Jodhpur (March 2025 - Present): In March 2025, Dr. Swami joined IIT Jodhpur as an Associate Professor in the Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering, bringing his extensive experience in water resources engineering to the institution. Research Interests and Contributions: Dr. Swami is a water resources engineer and he focuses on process level investigation of groundwater flow and contaminant transport through aquifers. His research interests include parameter optimization, aquifer scale modeling, dynamic plant uptake, source identification and application of one health. . Academic Contributions: Throughout his academic career, Dr. Swami has been actively involved in research and consultancy projects, contributing to the advancement of knowledge in his field. His expertise has been instrumental in shaping the curriculum and research initiatives at both IIT Mandi and IIT Jodhpur. ​ Professional Engagements: Dr. Swami has organized and participated in various workshops and conferences. Notably, he was involved in the HYDRUS Winter School held from January 27-31, 2024, aimed at educating participants on the use of HYDRUS software for research and consultancy purposes. Dr. Swami's transition to IIT Jodhpur marks a significant step in his career, where he continues to contribute to the field of civil and infrastructure engineering through his teaching, research, and professional activities.
