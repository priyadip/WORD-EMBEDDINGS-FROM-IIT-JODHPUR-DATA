Research Seminar by Dr. Nilanjan Bag on 10 March 2025 at 12:00PM
Speaker : Dr. Nilanjan Bag Title of the Talk : Some problems on exponential and character sums and its applications in analytic number theory Date, Time and venue : 10 March 2025 at 12:00PM - 1:00PM at Seminar Hall, Dept of Mathematics. Abstract : For abstract please ClickHere About the speaker : Dr. Nilanjan Bag has obtained, Ph.D from IIT Guwahati, and works on Analytic Number theory.
