The Board of Governors suggested the following to the Institute:
1. The Board of Governors appreciated and approved the Proposal of the
Institute to start stackable B.Sc./BS Program in Applied AI and Data
Science. The Board of Governors suggested to have a review of the
programme after 2 Years of its initiation for further action; and
2. The BoG appreciated the Proposal to start B.Sc.-B.Ed. (ITEP) P rogram and
suggested that the Institute may also explore to include the Life Sciences
subjects in the Program.
The Director welcomed the sug gestions and stated that the Institute would take
appropriate actions.
