Welcome to the Office of Security & Transports
Welcome to the Office of Security & Transports
The Office of Security & Transports at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur is dedicated to ensuring the safety and security of the campus community while providing efficient and reliable transportation services. Our mission is to create a secure and well-organized environment that supports the academic and operational needs of the institute. We oversee campus security, implementing measures to protect students, faculty, staff, and visitors. This includes 24/7 security patrols, surveillance systems, and emergency response protocols. Our team is committed to maintaining a safe campus atmosphere, preventing incidents, and addressing security concerns promptly. The Office of Security & Transports also manages transportation services, ensuring the smooth operation of campus shuttles and other transport facilities. We coordinate transportation logistics for academic events, field trips, and daily commutes, striving to provide timely and convenient services for all members of the IIT Jodhpur community. We work closely with local authorities and emergency services to enhance campus security and manage crises effectively. Our office conducts regular safety drills and awareness programs to educate the campus community about safety practices and protocols. By fostering a secure and well-coordinated transport system, the Office of Security & Transports plays a vital role in supporting the institute's mission, ensuring that students and staff can focus on their academic and professional pursuits without concern for their safety and mobility.
