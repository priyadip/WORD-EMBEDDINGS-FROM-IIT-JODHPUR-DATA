Hybrid Classrooms@IITJ
As the world reopens in the wake of the pandemic and students are brought back to the campus in batches, the EdTech vertical has proposed a simple hybrid classroom setup using existing infrastructure to enable a hybrid mode of classroom where a seamless interaction can take place between the instructor and students physically present in the classroom and those joining virtually over a video conference. A hands-on session for all faculty members was held on Saturday, August 07, 2021 in the Lecture Hall Building.
