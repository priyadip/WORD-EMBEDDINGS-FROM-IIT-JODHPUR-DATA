S Chandramouli is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. In December 2008, he has completed his Ph.D in Dynamical Systems  from the Department of Mathematics, University of Groningen, The Netherlands and State University of New York, Stony Brook, USA. He arrived at IIT Jodhpur in November 2009. His research focuses on Dynamical Systems, including Renormalization theory in low-dimensional dynamics, Dynamical Neuroscience,  Fractal Geometry, and q-deformed nonlinear systems. His work has been published  in the leading journals such as Ergodic Theory and Dynamical Systems, Discrete and Continuous Dynamical Systems, Journal of Statistical Physics, Chaos Solitons and Fractals, Journal of Difference Equations and Applications, Applied Mathematics and computation, Nonlinear Dynamics. etc.
