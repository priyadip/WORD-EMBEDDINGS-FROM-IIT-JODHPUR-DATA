Committee for projection of UG Programs of the Institute
In exercise of powers vested upon him vide Statute 12 (2) (xiv) of the Institute, the Competent Authority
has constituted the following Committee to prepare & implement a roadmap for the systematic
projection of UG Programs offered by 'the Institute for attracting UG students with higher JEE
(Advanced) rank for all programs:
Terms of Reference:
1.
The Committee shall prepare and implement a roadmap for the systematic projection of UG
Programs offered by the Institute.
2.
The Committee shall aggressively project UG Programs, their strengths, other unique features and
facilities through but not limited to Institute Website, Internet, Social Media, Facebook live,
YouTube, print, etc. to ensure that all seats are filled up.
3.
The Committee shall propose schemes for attracting higher ranked JEE (Advanced) Students.
Frequency of meeting: As and when required.
Tenure: 1st September 2024 to 31st August 2025
