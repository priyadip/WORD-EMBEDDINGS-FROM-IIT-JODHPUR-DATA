Congratulations to Dr. Sandip Murarka for receiving the prestigious 'Humboldt Research Fellowship for Experienced Researchers'
In recognition of Dr. Sandip Murarka’s research achievements to date the Alexander von umboldt Foundation awarded him the highly prestigious and competitive, “Humboldt Research Fellowship for Experienced Researchers” for a period of 18 months.
