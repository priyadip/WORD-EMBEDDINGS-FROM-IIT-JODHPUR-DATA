Scope of Work:
The scope of work will include Repair of Basketball Court and installation of 10–12 layer (4 mm) synthetic 
all-weather acrylic system including primer, resurfacer, cushion layers, color coats, and line marking as 
mentioned below.
1. Cleaning and preparation of the existing basketball court surface, including removal of loose material
and debris.
2. Repair of cracks, depressions, and damaged areas using approved repair compounds and leveling to
ensure a smooth base.
3. Application of 1 layer of acrylic primer for proper bonding.
4. Application of 2 layers of acrylic resurfacer to create a uniform base coat.
5. Application of 6 layers of acrylic cushions system to achieve a 4 mm all-weather shock-absorbing
6. Application of 2 layers of acrylic color coat suitable for outdoor sports courts.
7. Application of 1 layer of court line marking as per standard basketball dimensions.
8. Final cleaning, inspection, and handing over of the completed synthetic all-weather basketball court.
