Departmental Seminar by Prof. Eknath Ghate on 27th February 2025 at 11:00 AM
Departmental Seminar by Prof. Eknath Ghate on 27th February 2025 at 11:00 AM
Speaker : Prof. Eknath Ghate Title of the Talk : The Tau of Ramanujan Date, Time and venue : 27th February 2025, 11:00 AM at Seminar Hall, Dept of Mathematics. About the speaker: Prof. Eknath Prabhakar Ghate is a Professor of Mathematics specializing in number theory and working at the School of Mathematics, Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai, India. He was awarded the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize for science and technology, the highest science award in India, for the year 2013 in the mathematical sciences category.
