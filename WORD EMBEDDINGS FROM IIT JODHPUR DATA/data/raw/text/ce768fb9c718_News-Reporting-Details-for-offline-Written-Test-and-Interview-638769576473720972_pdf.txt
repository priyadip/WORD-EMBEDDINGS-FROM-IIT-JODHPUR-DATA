Reporting Details for Offline Written Test and Interview
All the candidates Shortlisted for Written Test/Interview for Ph.D. Admission of the Department of
Mechanical Engineering are hereby requested to appear for the offline written test and/or interview as per
the following schedule:
Please note the following:
(1) You must report on time in physical mode for the written test and interview.
(2) Traveling allowances will be provided as per government norms.
(3) No request will be entertained to change the date for the written test and interview.
(4) You are advised to produce a government issued photo identity card at the time of the written exam and
interview. Keep your other documents ready for the verification.
(5) Written test will consist of two sections;
Section I: General Aptitude and Mathematics
Section II: Domain Specific i.e., only one from the following list
(a) Thermofluids Engineering (b) Manufacturing (c) Solid Mechanics and Design
(6) You are advised to visit department’s web page before interview
(Link for department website: http://iitj.ac.in/department/index.php?id=me)
(7) You are advised to visit department faculty profile and their webpages before interview. (Link for
department faculty members: http://iitj.ac.in/department/index.php?dept=me&id=faculty_members)
(8) The Institute reserves the right to withdraw admission in case candidate DOES NOT fulfill the minimum
qualification/required percentage of marks in the qualifying examination or supplies any false
information.
(9) Please note that this call is merely a request for written test / interview and in any way does not
guarantee admission for PhD Program at IIT Jodhpur.
