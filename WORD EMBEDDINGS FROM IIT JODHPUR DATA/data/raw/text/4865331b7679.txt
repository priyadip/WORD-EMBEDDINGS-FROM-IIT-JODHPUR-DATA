Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee email bhupendra[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Pavement Materials; Pavement Analysis; Pavement Design; Sustainable Pavement Materials call 0291 280 1659 language Web Profile About: Dr. Bhupendra Singh is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. His research focuses on pavement materials, pavement analysis, and design. He has been serving at IIT Jodhpur since January 2021. Prior to this, he worked as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at the National Institute of Technology Patna from July 2018 to January 2021. He also held a contractual Assistant Professor position at Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar from January 2018 to June 2018. Dr. Singh was honored with the Young Scientist in Transportation Systems Award at the 5th Annual Research Meet (ARM) 2019 by Venus International Foundation. He also received the Science and Engineering Research Board International Research Experience (SIRE) Fellowship for 2023-24 to conduct research at the University of Nottingham. His recent publications have appeared in leading journals, including the Journal of Materials in Civil Engineering, International Journal of Pavement Engineering, Road Materials and Pavement Design, Journal of Cleaner Production, Materials and Structures, and Construction and Building Materials.
