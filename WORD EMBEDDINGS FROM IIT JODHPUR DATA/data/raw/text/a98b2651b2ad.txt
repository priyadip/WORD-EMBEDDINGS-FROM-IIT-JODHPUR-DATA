Engineering Innovation
The Department of Electrical Engineering offers capability linked opportunities for undergraduate students to complete Department Area Specialization in Engineering Innovation. To help students broaden their horizons and gain course/work experience, she/he may be permitted to proceed to other academic institutes/ Industry/ R&D organizations in India or abroad. Academic leave can be requested for the same. At the completion of an Engineering Innovation Project, the student will submit a report which will be evaluated by a duly appointed examiner(s) by DUGC. A student can also opt for M.Tech.(dual degree) in leading to M.Tech. in Department Area Specialization in Engineering innovation.
