CSR - Corporate Social Responsibility Contributions to IIT Jodhpur by corporations qualify under the mandatory CSR obligation of companies as per Section 135 of the Companies Act. Corporate Support Framework Approved under Schedule VII of Section 135 of the Companies Act, making corporate contributions to IIT Jodhpur eligible under mandatory CSR obligations. Registration Details IIT Jodhpur is officially registered for CSR activities. CSR Registration Number: CSR00006699 80G Tax Benefit Companies donating to IIT Jodhpur receive complete tax benefits under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.
