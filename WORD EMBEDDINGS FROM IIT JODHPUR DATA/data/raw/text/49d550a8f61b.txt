Kunwar Aditya Associate Professor school Ph. D.: Ontario Tech University (UOIT), Oshawa, ON, Canada email kunwar[dot]aditya[at]iitj[dot]ac[dot]in biotech Power Electronics, Automotive Electronics, Wireless Power Transfer, Transportation Electrification, Electrical Energy Storage Systems call 0291 280 1384 language Web Profile About Kunwar
Aditya received the B.E. degree from Bharati Vidyapeeth University, Pune,
India; the M.Tech. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras
Hindu University), Varanasi, India; and the Ph.D. degree from the Ontario Tech
University (UOIT), Oshawa, ON, Canada, in 2009, 2012, and 2016,
respectively—all in electrical engineering. From July to November 2017, he was a Research Fellow at the Rolls-Royce
Corporate Lab at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. In December 2017,
he joined BorgWarner Inc. in Canada as a Hardware Engineer, where he worked on
the development of electric vehicle motor controllers for 800V battery packs.
In April 2019, he joined Vitesco Technologies Canada as an Associate Electrical
Engineer.There he led the development of sensors and actuators for electric vehicle
thermal management systems. Currently, he is working as an Associate Professor in the Department of
Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Jodhpur,
India. His research interests include power electronics and control applied to
wireless charging, electrical energy storage systems, and transportation
electrification. Dr. Aditya was awarded the IIT (BHU) Gold Medal for securing
the highest academic standing among all electrical engineering students of the
2012 cohort. He also received the Outstanding Thesis Award for his Ph.D. work
from the Faculty of Engineering and Applied Science at UOIT.
