Dr. Shrutidhara Sarma (डॉ. श्रुतिधरा शर्मा) Elected Member of The National Academy of Sciences, India (NASI)
Dr. Shrutidhara Sarma (डॉ. श्रुतिधरा शर्मा) Elected Member of The National Academy of Sciences, India (NASI)
Dr. Shrutidhara Sarma (डॉ. श्रुतिधरा शर्मा), Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur and PI of FERN Lab (https://www.shrutidhara.com/) has been has been elected as a Member of The National Academy of Sciences, India (NASI) in the field of Engineering Sciences and Technology. The Academy bestows this prestigious Membership upon scientists who have made outstanding contributions across various domains of Science and Technology. This recognition stands as a testament to Dr. Sarma’s impactful research, innovation, and dedicated service to the scientific community. Established in 1930, NASI is the country’s first science academy, founded under the vision of eminent physicist Prof. Meghnad Saha. The Academy continues to promote scientific excellence through research, dialogue, and public engagement. Dr. Sarma will be formally inducted during the 95th Annual Session of NASI, to be held in December 2025, at IIT Guwahati. List of Selected Candidates for NASI Membership 2025
