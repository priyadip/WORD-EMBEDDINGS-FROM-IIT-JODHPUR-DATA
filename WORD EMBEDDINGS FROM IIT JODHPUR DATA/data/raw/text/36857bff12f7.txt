Welcome to the Office of Resources and Alumni (DORA)
The Office of Alumni Relations at IIT Jodhpur serves as the central platform for nurturing meaningful and enduring connections between the institute and its alumni community worldwide. With a vision to build a strong, engaged, and supportive alumni network, the office plays a vital role in advancing the institute’s mission and vision by fostering networking, collaboration, and fundraising initiatives. Key Roles and Objectives: Engagement & Networking : Strengthening lifelong relationships by organizing alumni meets, chapter launches, reunions, and professional networking opportunities. Outreach & Communication : Maintaining continuous dialogue with alumni through digital platforms, newsletters, and social media channels. Fundraising : Promoting a culture of giving back by designing and managing fundraising campaigns, endowment drives, and targeted initiatives to support students, research, and infrastructure. Student–Alumni Connect : Facilitating mentorship, internships, and career guidance opportunities, enabling students to benefit from alumni expertise and professional networks. Primarily, these activities are coordinated through the Society of Alumni Affairs (SAA) & Alumni Chapters. Institutional Advancement : Aligning alumni contributions with the strategic development goals of the institute, thereby enhancing IIT Jodhpur’s global presence and reputation.

Message Of DORA Dear Esteemed Alumni, It is with great pride and heartfelt gratitude that I reach out to you—the distinguished graduates of IIT Jodhpur—who have carried forward the spirit and values of our institute across the world. Your accomplishments and dedication continue to inspire current students and enhance the global reputation of IIT Jodhpur. Our alumni community is a cornerstone of our strength and growth. Your success stories, your leadership, and your unwavering commitment to “giving back” enrich the fabric of our institution and create new opportunities for generations to come. Whether through mentorship, collaboration, or philanthropy, your involvement shapes the future of IIT Jodhpur in profound ways. As we continue to build and nurture our alumni network, I encourage each of you to stay connected, engage actively, and contribute in ways that resonate with you. Together, we can amplify our collective impact and help IIT Jodhpur reach even greater heights on the global stage. Thank you for being an integral part of our journey. We look forward to growing stronger together.
