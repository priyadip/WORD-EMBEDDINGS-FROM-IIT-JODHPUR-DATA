2. For the B.Tech.-M.Tech./MBA dual degree students, the B.Tech. fees

M.Tech./MBA fees (Tuition & semester fee) would be charged from 9th

semester fee as per day-scholar category. In case the student wants to 
avail the hostel facility, he/she shall pay the difference of hosteller and 
day scholar fee.

required to register as hosteller during the admission.
