Exhibition of Course Learnings and Outcomes
Dr. Gaurav Vinod Vaidya and Ms. Ruchi Shah School of Design, IIT Jodhpur Date: 23 August 2024 The 3D Visualization and Sketching and Representation exhibition by Master of Design students showcases the outcomes of their course assignments, combining creative and technical skills. The exhibition demonstrates students’ ability to translate abstract ideas into visual representations using various techniques.
Dr. Gaurav Vinod Vaidya and Ms. Ruchi Shah
School of Design, IIT Jodhpur
Date: 23 August 2024
The 3D Visualization and Sketching and Representation exhibition by Master of Design students showcases the outcomes of their course assignments, combining creative and technical skills. The exhibition demonstrates students’ ability to translate abstract ideas into visual representations using various techniques.
