Creation of Endowed Chair To support outstanding faculty members of the institute, Faculty Chairs can be created in name of a person, corporate etc. These Faculty Chairs can be created in different domains based on mutual agreement between the institute and donor. These Chairs are usually conferred upon a faculty for a period of three years and may get renewed based on her/his performance and interest.

Supporting Civil Infrastructure The institute requires buildings to support several new initiatives it undertakes from time to time. In recent times the institute has started many new programs to resonate constantly changing landscape of technology.  The institute promotes interdisciplinary research through Interdisciplinary Research Platform (IDRP) and the institute has Technology Park and Incubation Centre to host and support industries.  The institute has taken initiatives in many new emerging fields e.g.   Internet of Things, Cyber physical Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Smart Manufacturing by starting new PG programs. The institute is inculcating new pedagogy through experiential learning and providing enough flexibility to students; the hallmark of our new curricula is adequate emphasis on nurturing value system and ethics. The institute is also participating several national missions. To support and host all these activities, we need civil infrastructure. The donor can donate a building for a specific purpose and named after person, corporate house etc.
