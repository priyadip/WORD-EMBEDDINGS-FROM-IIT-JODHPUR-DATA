Dr. Nishant to represent India in the BRICS Young Scientist Forum 2024
Dr Nishant has been selected to represent India in the BRICS Young Scientist Forum 2024, focused on AI-based research and development. The event will take place in Sochi, Russia, from 25th to 29th November, and he will be participating as part of the DST delegation.
