Prof. Ram Prakash from the Department of Physics, IIT Jodhpur has been elected as the President of the Plasma Science Society of India (2026–2028)
During the 40th PSSI National Plasma Symposium on Plasma Science and Technology (Plasma-2025) held at IIT Tirupati between 27-29 December, 2025. Department of Physics, IIT Jodhpur is delighted to share that Professor Ram Prakash has been elected as the President of Plasma Science Society of India (PSSI) for the term of 2026-2028.  Established in 1978, the Plasma Science Society of India is a premier national society dedicated to the promotion of plasma science and technology. The Society plays a vital role in advancing research, education, and applications of plasma science across the country. PSSI actively fosters collaboration among researchers, academic institutions, national laboratories, and industry through publications, conferences, educational initiatives, and coordinated research programs. It also works to popularize plasma science by supporting academic curricula, public outreach, and the development of plasma scholars. For more details, click here .
