SYNERGY EDITION #1

STEM for HER Scholarship
Twelve female students from IIT Jodhpur have been selected for
the prestigious STEM for HER Scholarship Program, a CSR
initiative by Genpact in collaboration with United Way Hyderabad.
This initiative empowers young women in technology by offering:
Scholarships worth ₹40,000 per student, renewable across all
years of study.
Structured mentorship from industry professionals
throughout the academic year.
Students were selected through a rigorous evaluation process,
emerging as top achievers from across the country. 🎓 Udaan Scholarship
Three students from IIT Jodhpur have been selected as recipients
of the prestigious Udaan Scholarship, supported by TransUnion
CIBIL and facilitated through FORSTU.
Awardees:
Mr. Mota Ram
Mr. Prince Kumar Jain
Mr. Kachhawa Goutham
This scholarship of ₹50,000 to 4th-year students aims to
empower deserving students from low-income background and
foster their academic journey in technology and innovation.
