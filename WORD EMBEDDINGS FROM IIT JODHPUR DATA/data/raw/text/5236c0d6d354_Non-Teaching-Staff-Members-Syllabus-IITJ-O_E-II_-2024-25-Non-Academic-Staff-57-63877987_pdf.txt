Physics/Chemistry/Materials Science 
1. Electricity and Magnetism 
2. Waves and Optics 
3. Solid State Physics 
4. Basic Electronics 
5. Quantum Mechanics

Handling high end equipment such as NMR, AFM, SEM, XRD, SQUID, PPMS, UV-Vis and IR

Handling experience of Clean Room equipment such as Optical Lithography, LASER, writer, E-beam 
lithography, Physical vapor deposition systems, Diffusion, oxidation and annealing 
furnaces, Wire-binder and wafer dicing systems,
