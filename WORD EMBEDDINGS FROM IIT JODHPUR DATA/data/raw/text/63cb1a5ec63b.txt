Sanjeev Junior Research Fellow Sanjeev Sanjeev joined our group at CETSD, IIT Jodhpur on 1st September 2023, as a Junior Research Fellow, in an industry-sponsored project. Sanjeev received is M.Sc. in Physics from National Institute of Technology (NIT) Srinagar, India in 2023. He did his M.Sc. project under the supervision of Prof. Mohd. Ikram at NIT Srinagar and completed his M.Sc. Summer Research Fellowship (SRF) Programme under the supervision of Dr. Ashish Kumar at UPES, Dehradun, sponsored by Indian Academy of Sciences (IASc), Bangalore. Sanjeev holds a B.Sc. in Physical Science from Dyal Singh College, University of Delhi. Sanjeev has valuable experience in synthesis and characterization techniques, including XRD, FESEM/EDX, XPS, Raman Spectroscopy, FTIR Spectroscopy, QUID VSM, and UV-Visible spectroscopy
