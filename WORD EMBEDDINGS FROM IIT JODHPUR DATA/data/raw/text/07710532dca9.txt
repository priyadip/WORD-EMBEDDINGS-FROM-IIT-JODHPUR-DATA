The transcript is a verified copy of the grade card of the student packed and sealed in an envelope and will be issued only at the end of the program. This service is available on payment of fee as mentioned below: SN Documents Applied For Fees (Including Postal Charges) 1 Transcript (for Passed Out Students) Within India (By Post): Rs. 1500/- Abroad (By Post) US $50 2 For Third Party Requests Sent via email to an Institute/Organization/University etc. directly through the IITJ) India (By Mail) Rs. 500/- Abroad (By Mail) US $15 Note: "The GST (18%) is applicable for all the graduates". Any graduate student can request a set of the transcript by providing the following mandatory details: Name Roll Number Email address Mobile Number Name of Program Full Postal Address with Pin code Transcript link at https://erponline.iitj.ac.in/exapp/auth/signup.htm?module_id=5 In case of any query related to the transcript, please send an email to transcript@iitj.ac.in For Current Students: Students will get a Grade Card at the end of each semester on ERP.
