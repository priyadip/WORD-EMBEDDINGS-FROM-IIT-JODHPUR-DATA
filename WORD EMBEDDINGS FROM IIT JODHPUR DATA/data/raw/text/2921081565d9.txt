Dr. Priyanka Singh has won the prestigious and competitive ”BRNS-Young 
Scientist Research Award“ from the Department of Atomic Energy, 
Government of India. The award comes with the funding support on the 
project titled, ”Role of Centrosome protein STIL in Cancer”. 
Congratulations!!
