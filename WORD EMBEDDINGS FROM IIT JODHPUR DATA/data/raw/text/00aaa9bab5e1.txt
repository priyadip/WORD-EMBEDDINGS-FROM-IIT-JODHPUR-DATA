Congratulations to Dr. Sandip Murarka for CRSI Bronze Medal
Congratulations to Dr. Sandip Murarka for CRSI Bronze Medal
Dr. Sandip Murarka has been awarded the prestigious CRSI Bronze  Medal by the Chemical Research Society of India (CRSI) for his outstanding contribution to the field of chemical sciences. The Chemical Research Society of India (CRSI) instituted Bronze, Silver, and Gold medals in 1999. The medals aim to recognize Indian researchers who have made significant contributions at various levels in Chemistry.
