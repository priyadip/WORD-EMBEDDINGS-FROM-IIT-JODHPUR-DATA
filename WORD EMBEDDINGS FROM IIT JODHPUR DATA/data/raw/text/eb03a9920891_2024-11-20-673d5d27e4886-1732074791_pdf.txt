In accordance with the Revised Commercial Establishment Licensing Rules-2024 of the Institute as
approved by the Board of Governors, the Competent Authority has constituted the following
Commercial Establishment Licensing Committee (CELC):
Terms of Reference:
•
To submit the recommendations for the licensing of commercial establishments within the Institute to the
Competent Authority for consideration and approval;
•
To recommend the license fees for various commercial establishments based on market rates or otherwise. For
this purpose, the Committee may adopt a method/ formula for calculating the License fees of the commerical
eastablishment depending upon its location, sale, profit, utility and other relevant aspects;
•
To recommend the revision of license fee for commercial establishments from time to time;
•
To address the complaints received from the Campus community against the commercial establishments; and
•
To monitor the quality of products, cleanliness of outlets and premises for all commercial establishments.
Frequency of meeting: At least once in two months OR as and when required.
Tenure: Two Years w.e.f. the date of issue of this Notification.
