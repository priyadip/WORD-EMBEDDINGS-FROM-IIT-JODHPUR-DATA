Congratulations to Shraddha Choudhary, Ph.D. Student, working under the supervision of Dr. Kirankumar R. Hiremath, Associate Professor, for having received the Best Paper Award at IEEE TENCON 2019, September 2019.
Congratulations to Shraddha Choudhary, Ph.D. Student, working under the supervision of Dr. Kirankumar R. Hiremath, Associate Professor, for having received the Best Paper Award at IEEE TENCON 2019, September 2019.
Shraddha Choudhary, Ph.D. Student, working under the supervision of Dr. Kirankumar R. Hiremath, Associate Professor, Department of Mathematics, received Best Paper Award in "Recent Advances on Materials and metasurface based Devices and Antennas for High Frequency Application" for their research work titled “Random Phase Gradient Metasurface for Broadband RCS Reduction” at the IEEE TENCON 2019, held during 17-20 October 2019 at Kochi, India.
