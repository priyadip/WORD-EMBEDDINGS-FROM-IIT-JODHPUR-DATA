Research Seminar by Dr. Tuhina Mukherjee, on 02 Dec'25 at 4:30 PM
Research Seminar by Dr. Tuhina Mukherjee, on 02 Dec'25 at 4:30 PM
Title of the talk: On analysis of  mixed type PDEs Date, Time & Venue: 02 Dec'25 at 4:30PM at 5:15PM on Seminar Room, Dept. of Mathematics Abstract: In this talk, we will discuss the existence of weak solutions to some elliptic PDEs involving mixed type operators or mixed type boundary conditions. In particular, we will see results on the existence of weak solutions and some of their characteristics for PDEs governed by local-nonlocal, (p,N) and polyharmonic type operators.
Title of the talk: On analysis of  mixed type PDEs
Date, Time & Venue: 02 Dec'25 at 4:30PM at 5:15PM on Seminar Room, Dept. of Mathematics
Abstract: In this talk, we will discuss the existence of weak solutions to some elliptic PDEs involving mixed type operators or mixed type boundary conditions.
In particular, we will see results on the existence of weak solutions and some of their characteristics for PDEs governed by local-nonlocal, (p,N) and polyharmonic type operators.
