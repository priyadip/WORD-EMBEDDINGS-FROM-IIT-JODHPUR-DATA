Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)for Legal vetting of draft MoU/ Agreement
1. Employees may submit a document to the Approving Authority through the Head of
Department/School/Center/Office.
2. The following will be the Approving Authorities to consider the subject matter for Legal
Vetting as mentioned against the authorities:

10. Registrar • Agreement / MoD pertaining to procurement of Goods
and Servicesnot covered above.
Agreement / MoD pertaining to financial matters not
covered above.
11. Estate Officer • Agreement / MoD pertaining to Estate Matters.
12. Advisorto Director • Agreement / MoD pertaining to any other matter.
13. Director • AnyMatters under special circumstances.
3. The Approving Authority will refer to the document for legal vetting mentioning other
stakeholders to be kept in the loop.
4. On receipt of a document referred bythe Approving Authority, the Legal Cellwill examine
the document.
5. During the process of legal vetting, the concerned employee and other concerned
authorities will be kept in the loop to respond to mutual queries and clarifications on the
document of and by the employee and the hired Advocate (Outsourced employee).
6. After legal vetting, the Legal Cell will send the final document to the Office of the
Approving Authority as mentioned above under intimation to the employee for further
official action.
7. In case of a requirement for legal vetting by an outside agency (an empaneled
Advocate(s)), specific instructions / approval will be sought from the Advisor to the
Director by the Legal Cell of the Institute.
