The School of Liberal Arts at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur invites applications for its two-year MSc in Computational Social Science (CSS) programme
The School of Liberal Arts at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur invites applications for its two-year MSc in Computational Social Science (CSS) programme. This interdisciplinary programme integrates social theory, data analytics, and computational methods to address complex societal challenges, preparing students for research, policy, and industry-oriented careers.
