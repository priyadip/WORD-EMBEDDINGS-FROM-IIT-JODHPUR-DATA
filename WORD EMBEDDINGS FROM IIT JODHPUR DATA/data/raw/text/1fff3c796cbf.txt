an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering. He is also
affiliated with the Rishab Center for Research and Innovation in Clean Energy
and the IITJ-AIIMSJ Medical Technology Center. He received his Ph.D. from IIT
Bombay in the area of multiphase flows and heat transfer. He is the recipient
of the National Award for Best M.Tech Thesis by the Indian Society for
Technical Education (ISTE) for his research on lignite coal gasification. He
received the Excellence in Thesis Work Award from IIT Bombay for his
outstanding research contributions during his Ph.D. At IIT Jodhpur, Dr.
Kothadia has been honoured with the Teaching Excellence Award (2021) and the
Institute Award for Research Excellence (2025) in the Young Researcher
category. With a prolific
academic record, he has authored over 80 research papers published in reputed
international journals and conferences. He is the PI/Co-PI in 14 sponsored and
consultancy projects, securing total funding exceeding ₹20 crore. His
collaborations span major industrial and government bodies, including Thermax
Limited, DRDO, DST, ANRF, ARDB, ISRO, DAE, ICMR, the Ministry of Defence and
the Ministry of MSME. In the domain of innovation, Dr. Kothadia has been
granted one patent and has filed four provisional patents.

Kothadia has been honoured with the Teaching Excellence Award (2021) and the
Institute Award for Research Excellence (2025) in the Young Researcher
category. With a prolific
academic record, he has authored over 80 research papers published in reputed

the Ministry of MSME. In the domain of innovation, Dr. Kothadia has been
granted one patent and has filed four provisional patents.
