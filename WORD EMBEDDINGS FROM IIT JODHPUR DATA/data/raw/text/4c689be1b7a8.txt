The School of Liberal Arts, IIT Jodhpur, congratulates Dr. Kanak Yadav on her publication
The School of Liberal Arts, IIT Jodhpur, congratulates Dr. Kanak Yadav on the publication of the article “The precarious case of the zero-waste solution to the planetary problem” in the edited collection Ecocritical Explorations of the Climate Crisis: Planetary Precarity and Future Habitability (2025), co-edited by Janet M. Wilson, Barbara Schmidt-Haberkamp, and Om Prakash Dwivedi. New York: Routledge.
